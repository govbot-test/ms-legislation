Section 1:
HR 38 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Calvert House Resolution 38 A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING AUBREY LYNN ATCHISON UPON BEING NAMED THE 2025 STATE GAMES OF MISSISSIPPI OVERALL YOUTH ATHLETE OF THE YEAR. WHEREAS, Aubrey Lynn Atchison of Biloxi, Mississippi, has been named the 2025 State Games of Mississippi Overall Youth Athlete of the Year, an honor bestowed upon a young athlete whose performance, character and perseverance exemplify the highest ideals of athletic excellence and sportsmanship; and WHEREAS, Aubrey is a senior at Biloxi High School and has dedicated 13 years to the sport of softball, including three  years competing in the State Games of Mississippi, reflecting sustained commitment, discipline and competitive excellence; and WHEREAS, during the 2025 State Games of Mississippi, Aubrey delivered an exceptional performance by posting a .636 batting average, scoring four runs and recording five runs batted in (RBIs), thereby distinguishing herself among the most accomplished youth athletes in the state; and WHEREAS, Aubrey's achievements at the State Games capped a standout season in which she earned First Team All-Region 4-7A honors, was selected to participate in the Mississippi Gulf Coast All-Stars Futures Game, and was recognized as Biloxi High School's Offensive Player of the Year; and WHEREAS, Aubrey's path to success has been marked by significant adversity, including a serious ankle injury sustained in 2022 that required a prolonged recovery, after which she returned to varsity competition while still in the eighth grade; and WHEREAS, despite sustaining two additional injuries in subsequent years, Aubrey remained unwavering in her commitment to her team and her sport by continuing to train, encouraging her teammates, and leading with determination, patience and grace; and WHEREAS, through these challenges, Aubrey has demonstrated resilience and maturity beyond her years, embodying the principle that true leadership is revealed through perseverance, humility and purpose, as reflected in her words:  "Softball is so much more than just a sport to me.  It is where I found my passion, my people, and my purpose"; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives of the State of Mississippi to recognize and commend outstanding young Mississippians whose achievements and character bring honor to their communities and to the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate Aubrey Lynn Atchison upon being named the 2025 State Games of Mississippi Overall Youth Athlete of the Year, and extend sincere best wishes for continued success in all her future academic, athletic and personal endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Aubrey Lynn Atchison and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
