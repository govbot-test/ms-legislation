MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

By: Senator(s) Tate, McCaughn, Hickman

Senate Resolution 25

(As Adopted by Senate)

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE LAMAR SCHOOL RAIDERS FOOTBALL TEAM AND HEAD COACH JACOB LAND FOR WINNING THE MAIS 4A DIVISION 111 STATE CHAMPIONSHIP WHICH WAS THE TEAM'S SEVENTH STATE CHAMPIONSHIP AND FIRST TITLE SINCE 2018.

     WHEREAS, we join the team, coaches, students and administration of Lamar School in Meridian for winning the MAIS 4A Division 111 State Championship by defeating Brookhaven Academy 34-17 on November 22, 2025, which was the team's seventh State Championship and its first title since 2018; and

     WHEREAS, Lamar School had one school between them and their goal, Brookhaven Academy.  The Raiders came out firing on all cylinders, two touchdowns to kick the game off, putting them up to a 14-0 lead early on.  The Lamar defense was absolutely electric with two game-changing pick-sixes, the first coming from senior Jalin Trotter and the second coming from senior Mikeal Willis; and

     WHEREAS, Lamar Head Football Coach, Jacob Land, said on the win, "They just weren't going to get beat.  And that's what I love about them.  I'm so proud of them.  Our players are so resilient, just great young men and I'm excited to see where they go in the future"; and

     WHEREAS, the Lamar School 2025-2026 Football Roster:  Jalin Trotter, Robert Gardner, Jhaqwon Slack, Jackson Whitcomb, Luke Brady, Samuel Roberts, Mikeal Willis, Sullivan Reed, Gavin Aull, Ashton Jones, Walker Mims, Hamp Haarala, Benson Oates, Maddox Mims, Briggs Calvert, Cade Vowell, Logan Cross, Knox Davis, Landon Johnson, Caleb Randall, Joe Morrow, Pace Perrett, Preston Perrett, Dkharian Towner, Chase Putman, Luke Morrow, Tyler Brown, Bryson Boswell, Cormac Dulaney, Lucas Williams, James Ross, Henry Prewitt, Wendell Bell, Mason Brock, Nate Lee, Hayes Lancaster, Ryan McDaniel, Sam Hines, Brett Calvert, Jack Voss; Head Coach, Jacob Land and Assistant Coach, McLeod Norwood; and

     WHEREAS, it is with great pride that we recognize this group of student-athletes who have brought honor to their school, their community and to the State of Mississippi:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Lamar School Raiders Football Team and Head Coach Jacob Land for winning the MAIS 4A Division 111 State Championship which was the team's seventh state championship and first title since 2018, and extend our best wishes to the team, coaches, parents and sponsors for future success.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Lamar School Head Coach Jacob Land for the team and Head of School Leigh Ann Ballou, forwarded to the Lamar School Board of Trustees and made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
