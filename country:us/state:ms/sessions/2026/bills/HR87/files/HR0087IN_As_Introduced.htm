MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Representative Carpenter

House Resolution 87

A HOUSE RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE TISHOMINGO COUNTY HIGH SCHOOL "BRAVETTES" DANCE TEAM UPON WINNING THE 2025 MISSISSIPPI STATE CHAMPIONSHIP IN THE 1A-4A HIP-HOP DIVISION.

     WHEREAS, the Tishomingo County High School Bravettes Dance Team achieved a historic milestone on December 12, 2025, by winning the program's first-ever state championship in the 1A-4A Hip-Hop Division during competition held in Jackson, Mississippi; and

     WHEREAS, this remarkable victory marks a defining moment in the history of Tishomingo County High School athletics and performing arts, demonstrating the Bravettes' commitment to excellence, discipline and teamwork as they competed against talented dance teams from across the state; and

     WHEREAS, under the outstanding leadership of Head Coach Alexis Rogers, with the valuable guidance of Assistant Coach Abigail Williford, the Bravettes Dance Team devoted countless hours to rigorous practice, preparation and performance refinement, resulting in a championship performance that showcased their precision, creativity and athleticism; and

     WHEREAS, the team was guided on the floor by senior Dance Captains Gracie Wilson and Maycie Westbrook, whose leadership, determination and encouragement inspired their teammates to perform at the highest level and to pursue the goal of bringing home the program's first state title; and

     WHEREAS, the championship Bravettes Dance Team roster is comprised of a talented and dedicated group of student performers including:  seniors, Lily Cooley, Ava Reed, Britney Mock, Gracie Wilson and Maycie Westbrook; junior, Caroline Robinette; sophomores, Lilly Edge, Kylie Hallmark, Caroline Robinson and Shelby Wallace; and freshman, Gracelyn Carter; and

     WHEREAS, through their unity, discipline and unwavering commitment to excellence, the members of the Bravettes Dance Team have represented Tishomingo County High School with distinction, bringing pride, honor and recognition to their school, their community and the State of Mississippi; and

     WHEREAS, the team's historic state championship would not have been possible without the continued support of their families, school administrators, faculty, fellow students and the loyal supporters of Tishomingo County High School who have encouraged and celebrated the Bravettes throughout their journey; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend excellence in our student-athletes and performing arts programs, particularly those such as the Tishomingo County High School "Bravettes" Dance Team whose dedication and achievement serve as an inspiration to young people throughout the State of Mississippi:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Tishomingo County High School "Bravettes" Dance Team upon winning the 2025 State Championship in the 1A-4A Hip-Hop Division, and extend best wishes for continued success in all their future endeavors.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Head Coach Alexis Rogers and Assistant Coach Abigail Williford on behalf of the Tishomingo County High School "Bravettes" Dance Team, and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
