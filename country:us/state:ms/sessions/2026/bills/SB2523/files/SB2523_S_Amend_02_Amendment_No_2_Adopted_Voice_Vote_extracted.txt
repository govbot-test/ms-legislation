Section 1:
SB2523_S_Amend_02 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 2 PROPOSED TO Senate Bill No. 2523 BY: Senator(s) Chism AMEND on line 252 by striking the words "brought forward" and inserting "amended" in lieu thereof. FURTHER, AMEND below line 451 by inserting the following as a new subsection: (*) The board shall not permit a person subject to the registration requirements under Sections 45-33-21 through 45-33-63 to reside in a dormitory or other student living accommodation located on the campus of or managed by a state institution of higher learning. FURTHER, AMEND the title to conform.
