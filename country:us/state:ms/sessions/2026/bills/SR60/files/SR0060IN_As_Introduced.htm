MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Senator(s) DeBar, Michel, McMahan, Hickman

Senate Resolution 60

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING THE 25 COACHES FROM ACROSS MISSISSIPPI WHO HAVE BEEN CHOSEN FOR THE 2025-2026 "TOMORROW'S 25 COACHING FELLOWSHIP" CLASS.

     WHEREAS, the Tomorrow's 25 Coaching Fellowship is presented by the Mississippi Association of Coaches (MAC) and the Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA); and

     WHEREAS, the Tomorrow's 25 Coaching Fellowship Class seeks to annually select and invest in 25 future leaders for Mississippi in the area of high school and middle school athletics; and

     WHEREAS, the following candidates were chosen for the 2025-2026 Tomorrow's 25 Coaching Fellowship Class:  Ahmad Alexander (East Tallahatchie School District); Shay Ashford (Amory School District); Chris Bedenbaugh (Hattiesburg Public School District); Shadejha Brown (Kemper County School District); Matt DeFillips (Long Beach School District); Christine Demus (Yazoo County School District); Pashonda Edwards (Tupelo Public School District); Debrah Everett (Lauderdale County School District); Margaret Falatko (DeSoto County Schools); Gina Fulton (Rankin County School District); Carenzo George (Hinds County School District); Lindsey Griffin (Western Line School District); Broderick Hall (East Jasper School District); David Hendershot (Rankin County School District); Scott Landry (Biloxi Public School District); Scotti Miles (DeSoto County Schools); Micah Moment (Booneville School District); Alphaka Moore (Lamar County Schools); Dr. Conswayla Nickson (Perry County School District); Quincy Patrick (Harrison County School District); Jalessa Taylor (Madison County Schools); Trey Ward (Tupelo Public School District); Kenton Wheaton (Meridian Public School District); Nicki Whitten (DeSoto County Schools); Kevin Williams (Corinth School District); and

     WHEREAS, with the announcement of the 20252026 cohort, the State of Mississippi now celebrates a significant milestone as 100 coaches have completed or are participating in the Tomorrow's 25 Coaching Fellowship since its founding, reflecting a growing statewide commitment to leadership development in amateur athletics; and

     WHEREAS, the cumulative impact of these 100 coaches extends far beyond the playing field, positively influencing thousands of student-athletes, strengthening school communities, and contributing to the long-term cultural and civic vitality of the State of Mississippi; and

     WHEREAS, all 25 candidates were identified by a selection committee and represent the best the state has to offer based on their competency, character, and commitment to their school and community; and

     WHEREAS, the Tomorrow's 25 Coaching Fellowship seeks to assist in three areas of growth for each candidate:  Coaching the Whole Athlete (Leadership, Resilience, Empathy, and Responsibility), the opportunity to network with 24 of their peers who have demonstrated the capacity to be the state's future leaders in athletics, and access to current leaders in the associations (MAC, MHSAA) and college athletics to learn how they can grow their spheres of influence in their communities and across the state; and

     WHEREAS, the ultimate goal of the Tomorrow's 25 Coaching Fellowship is for the selected candidates to deliberately work on their craft in a cohort of their peers, so that ultimately their student-athletes and overall communities benefit for years to come:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby congratulate the selected coaches for the 2025-2026 Tomorrow's 25 Coaching Fellowship Class and extend best wishes for continued success in the future.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be transmitted to the Mississippi Association of Coaches, the Mississippi High School Activities Association, and the University of Mississippi's School of Education, and be made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
