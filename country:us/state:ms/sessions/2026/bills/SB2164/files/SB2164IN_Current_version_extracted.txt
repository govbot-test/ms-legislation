Section 1:
SB2164 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations By: Senator(s) Thomas Senate Bill 2164 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO DREAM INNOVATIONS, INC., FOR THE PURPOSE OF DEFRAYING EXPENSES RELATED TO DREAM WORC FOR MATCHING FUNDS FOR GRANT AWARD FOR PURCHASING EQUIPMENT AND TRANSPORTATION, RENOVATING FACILITIES, UPGRADING ELECTRICAL AND NETWORK CAPABILITIES, TRAINING AND HIRING STAFF FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1. The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to Dream Innovations, Inc., for the purpose of defraying expenses related to Dream WORC for matching funds for grant award for purchasing equipment and transportation, renovating facilities, upgrading electrical and network capabilities, training and hiring staff for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027............................................ $ 1,400,000.00. SECTION 2.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 3.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
