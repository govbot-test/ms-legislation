MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Representative Fondren

House Resolution 120

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE PASCAGOULA HIGH SCHOOL BOYS BASKETBALL TEAM AND HEAD COACH LORENZO WRIGHT UPON WINNING THEIR FIRST CHAMPIONSHIP IN THE SCHOOL'S HISTORY.

     WHEREAS, the Pascagoula High School Boys Basketball Team are the Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class 6A state basketball champions after accomplishing an extraordinary 2025-2026 season as dominant contenders with a 28-6 overall record, winning the final 18 games of the season; and

     WHEREAS, the championship 56-54 win over Terry High School is also historic because it is the first-ever basketball championship title that Pascagoula High School has won since the school's existence; and

     WHEREAS, this impressive team also dominated district-wide by ending the district season undefeated with a record of 12-0; and

     WHEREAS, this historic win is a proud milestone for Pascagoula High School and the surrounding community, reflecting the dedication of student-athletes, coaches, families and supporters who stood behind the team throughout the season; and

     WHEREAS, under the leadership of Head Coach Lorenzo Wright, the team demonstrated discipline, resilience and unity from the opening tip-off of the season to the final buzzer of the championship game, setting a standard of excellence both on and off the court; and

     WHEREAS, Coach Wright's guidance is establishing a culture of preparation, accountability and competitive success within the program; and

     WHEREAS, stand-out senior Blake Nettles won the Class 6A Mr. Basketball for the State of Mississippi; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend the achievements of student-athletes who bring distinction to their schools and communities through discipline, teamwork and excellence:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Pascagoula High School Boys Basketball Team and Head Coach Lorenzo Wright upon winning the Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class 6A State Championship and having an undefeated district record.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Head Coach Lorenzo Wright, to the administration of Pascagoula High School and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
