Section 1:
HB1090 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Public Health and Human Services By: Representative Brown House Bill 1090 AN ACT TO REQUIRE THE DEPARTMENT OF MENTAL HEALTH AND COMMUNITY MENTAL HEALTH CENTERS TO EXPAND OUTPATIENT SERVICES AND COMMUNITY-BASED SERVICES FOR FORMERLY INCARCERATED INMATES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF CORRECTIONS AND FOR THE PURPOSE OF REDUCING CRIME; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1. The Department of Mental Health and community mental health centers shall expand outpatient services and community-based services for former inmates of the Department of Corrections who are no longer incarcerated within a correctional institution of the Department of Corrections.  The expanded outpatient services and community-based services shall be for such former inmates who were diagnosed with a mental illness while housed within a state correctional institution of the Department of Corrections.  The expansion of the outpatient services and community-based services shall be paid out of any available funds appropriated for such expansion of services for the former incarcerated inmates of the Department of Corrections. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
