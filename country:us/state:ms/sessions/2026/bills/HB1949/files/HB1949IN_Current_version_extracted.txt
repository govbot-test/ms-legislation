Section 1:
HB1949 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations A By: Representative Bailey (23rd) House Bill 1949 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE GOVERNING AUTHORITIES OF THE TOWN OF BIG CREEK TO ASSIST IN PAYING COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH PURCHASING FIRE TURNOUT CLOTHING AND A DEFIBRILLATOR FOR THE BIG CREEK VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the governing authorities of the Town of Big Creek, Mississippi, to assist in paying costs associated with purchasing fire turnout clothing and a defibrillator for the Big Creek Volunteer Fire Department for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027....................................................... .................................................... $ 18,500.00 SECTION 2.  The funds appropriated in Section 1 of this act shall be expended as follows: (a)  Fire turnout clothing................. $ 16,000.00 (b)  Defibrillator.......................... $ 2,500.00 TOTAL............................................... $ 18,500.00 SECTION 3.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 4.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
