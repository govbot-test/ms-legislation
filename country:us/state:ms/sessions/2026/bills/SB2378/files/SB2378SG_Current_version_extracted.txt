Section 1:
SB2378 (As Sent to Governor) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Accountability, Efficiency, Transparency By: Senator(s) Wiggins, Hickman, Boyd, Fillingane, Simmons (12th), Simmons (13th) Senate Bill 2378 (As Sent to Governor) AN ACT TO AMEND SECTION 25-7-89, MISSISSIPPI CODE OF 1972, TO INCREASE THE FEE A COURT REPORTER MAY RECEIVE PREPARING TRANSCRIPTS AND OTHER PAPERS; TO PROVIDE THAT COURT REPORTERS SHALL FORMAT TRANSCRIBED DOCUMENTS TO REASONABLY MINIMIZE THE NUMBER OF PRINTED PAGES; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  Section 25-7-89, Mississippi Code of 1972, is amended as follows: 25-7-89.  Each court reporter shall receive * * * Two Dollars and Forty Cents ($2.40) Four Dollars ($4.00) per page for each transcript of testimony, or copy of such transcript, which shall be taxed as costs to each party or individual who may obtain such transcript or copy thereof.  The court reporter shall use such font, spacing and indentures, as practical, to reasonably minimize the number of printed pages.  The court reporter shall file with the clerk an original and one (1) copy of the transcript, for which copy no charge shall be made. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
