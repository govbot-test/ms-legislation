Senate Resolution 34

A RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING VARDAMAN HIGH SCHOOL JUNIOR ELI EDMONDSON FOR WINNING THE MHSAA CLASS 1A INDIVIDUAL STATE GOLF CHAMPIONSHIP.

     WHEREAS, we join the students, coaches, faculty and administration of Vardaman High School in extending congratulations to Junior Eli Edmondson for winning the Mississippi Class 1A Individual State Golf Championship on October 31, 2025; and

     WHEREAS, this championship is a significant achievement celebrated by his school and local media, with his team also placing second in the State 1A Tournament, solidifying a successful season for the young golfer; and

     WHEREAS, Eli won his first state golf title shooting a two-day total of 140, two under par, to earn top honors while helping his team to a state runner-up finish; and

     WHEREAS, "I was certainly excited for myself, but I was proud for all our team," Edmondson said; and

     WHEREAS, the championship was played at Hillandale in Corinth where Edmondson described the greens as "pretty fast," but the course overall "not too bad."  "The first day I wasn't very clean.  I had a bunch of bogeys, but I managed to knock in a lot of birdies, too," Edmondson said; and

     WHEREAS, Eli held a four-stroke lead entering day two.  "l just stayed aggressive.  I played pretty much the same way I did the first day.  Just trying to extend the lead, not trying to give it up, not trying to keep it the same.  Most of the time when I try to protect a lead or protect any kind of shots, it always ends up going bad, because I'm not playing as free.  So I just play aggressive, play how I normally play.  I generally play better that way," Edmondson said; and

     WHEREAS, Edmondson, who has already had a lot of success on the golf course, took winning the state title in stride.  "I was excited, but at the same time, it kind of made me realize that I don't need to take anything for granted, because it can all slip away.  I'm motivated to build on this year's success next year.  It's holding yourself up to a high standard.  You still have to go out there and put in the time, the effort, and just try to get better every day," Edmondson said; and

     WHEREAS, it is with great pride that we recognize this young golfer who has brought honor to his school, his community and to the State of Mississippi:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby congratulate Vardaman High School Junior Eli Edmondson for winning the MHSAA Class 1A Individual State Golf Championship, and extend to Eli and his family our best wishes for future success.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Eli Edmondson and the Vardaman High School Golf Coaches, on behalf of the team, and forwarded to Vardaman High School Principal Lucas Ferguson, and be made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
