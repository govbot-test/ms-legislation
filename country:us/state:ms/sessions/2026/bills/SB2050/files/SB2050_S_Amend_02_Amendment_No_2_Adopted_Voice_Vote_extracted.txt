Section 1:
SB2050_S_Amend_02 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 2 PROPOSED TO Senate Bill No. 2050 BY: Senator(s) Williams, Blackmon AMEND by striking the new language on lines 50 through 55 and replace it with the following: (e) "Artificial intelligence" or "AI" means a machine-based system that can, for a given set of human-defined objectives, make predictions, recommendations or decisions influencing real or virtual environments. Artificial intelligence systems use machine- and human-based inputs to perceive real and virtual environments; abstract such perceptions into models through analysis in an automated manner; and use model inference to formulate options for information or action.

Raw Text:
SB2050_S_Amend_02 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 2 PROPOSED TO Senate Bill No. 2050 BY: Senator(s) Williams, Blackmon AMEND by striking the new language on lines 50 through 55 and replace it with the following: (e) "Artificial intelligence" or "AI" means a machine-based system that can, for a given set of human-defined objectives, make predictions, recommendations or decisions influencing real or virtual environments. Artificial intelligence systems use machine- and human-based inputs to perceive real and virtual environments; abstract such perceptions into models through analysis in an automated manner; and use model inference to formulate options for information or action.
