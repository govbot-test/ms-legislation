Adopted

 

COMMITTEE AMENDMENT NO 1 PROPOSED TO

 

Senate Bill No. 2408

 

BY: Committee

 

     Amend by striking all after the enacting clause and inserting in lieu thereof the following:

 

     SECTION 1.  (1)  As used in this act, the following terms shall have the meanings as defined in this section unless the context clearly requires otherwise:

          (a)  "Commission" means the Mississippi Soil and Water Conservation Commission.

          (b)  "Levee project" means a levee system bordering portions of the Yazoo River, within Yazoo County, and funded by federal grants and/or appropriations.

          (c)  "Political subdivision" means any agency, board, department or commission of the State of Mississippi or of the federal government and any political subdivision of the State of Mississippi.

     (2)  The commission shall be authorized to construct a Levee Project and perform, without limitation, all necessary construction, pre-construction and design activities, including environmental mitigation activities and right-of-way acquisitions.

     (3)  The commission shall be authorized to enter into contracts with public and private entities, as it deems necessary, to carry out the purposes of this section.

     (4)  The authority granted under this section shall be construed liberally to authorize the performance of any and all activities necessary to construct the levee project.

     (5)  The commission shall be authorized to maintain the Levee Project and to contract with one or more political subdivisions for such maintenance for a period not to exceed ninety nine (99) years and shall further have the power to cede all rights of way and levees and the maintenance, management and control thereof to the federal government.  A political subdivision may provide funding to defray the cost to maintain the levee project.

     SECTION 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage.

     Further, amend by striking the title in its entirety and inserting in lieu thereof the following:

 

     AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE MISSISSIPPI SOIL AND WATER CONSERVATION COMMISSION TO CONSTRUCT AND MAINTAIN A LEVEE SYSTEM ALONG THE YAZOO RIVER, WITHIN YAZOO COUNTY; TO ALLOW THE COMMISSION TO PERFORM NECESSARY ACTIVITIES RELATED TO SUCH CONSTRUCTION AND MAINTENANCE; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES.
