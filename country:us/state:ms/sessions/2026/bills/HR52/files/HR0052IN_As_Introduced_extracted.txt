HR 52 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representatives Sanford, Carpenter House Resolution 52 A RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING THE TREMENDOUS WORK OF VETERANS OUTREACH AND COMMENDING THE ORGANIZATION'S EFFORTS TO IMPROVE THE LIVES OF OUR AMERICAN HEROES THROUGH SERVICE AND FELLOWSHIP. WHEREAS, with remarkable dedication and steadfast care for America's truest heroes, Veterans OutReach has faithfully served Mississippi's veterans since 2009, helping the residents of the Mississippi State Veterans Homes in Collins and Kosciusko feel respected, supported and at home; and WHEREAS, the mission of Veterans OutReach is to provide spiritual guidance, counseling and essential goods and services not otherwise supplied by the State of Mississippi or other government agencies to veterans residing in the Mississippi State Veterans Homes; and WHEREAS, the Mississippi State Veterans Homes are state-owned and operated facilities providing care to veterans, many of whom arrive medically indigent, elderly, homeless or without family support, and in need of basic necessities and companionship; and WHEREAS, working in close coordination with nursing home staff, Veterans OutReach endeavors to identify unmet needs and provide assistance in a manner that preserves the dignity of each veteran and avoids preferential treatment; and WHEREAS, from whirlpool tubs and air mattresses to nebulizers, oxygen concentrators, and lifts and chairs, Veterans OutReach has provided equipment and furniture to the residents of Mississippi State Veterans Homes, equipping them with what they need to live comfortably while making their environment feel like home; and WHEREAS, Veterans OutReach conducts numerous ongoing projects to enhance the quality of life for resident veterans and, through its efforts, the organization has been able to provide residents with various individual and communal goods, including clothing, shoes, personal items, computers, game tables and a grand piano for the chapel; and WHEREAS, beyond addressing their physical needs, the devoted volunteers of Veterans OutReach are also committed to meeting the social needs of each veteran, making regular personal visits to residents, fostering lasting bonds of friendship, and expressing appreciation, respect and compassion for those who have selflessly served our nation; and WHEREAS, Veterans OutReach hosts and organizes social events to nurture a sense of community and bring people together, including bingo games, cookouts, tailgating parties, birthday celebrations and holiday parties, giving veterans an opportunity to enjoy fellowship with each other; and WHEREAS, encouraging members of the community to get involved and give back to those who have given so much, Veterans OutReach funds, plans and conducts an annual Adopt-A-Veteran Christmas Gift Drive, building spirit and appreciation for our veterans while benefiting their lives in tangible ways; and WHEREAS, through its unwavering commitment to service, Veterans OutReach has demonstrated a profound dedication to honoring and caring for Mississippi's most vulnerable and often forgotten veterans; and WHEREAS, the admirable work and positive impact of Veterans OutReach has garnered the gratitude and support of Mississippi Veterans Affairs, as they stand united in their goal to honor and care for our state's veterans; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to honor fine organizations driven by a mission to serve the brave United States soldiers who have risked their lives for us during a vulnerable time in their lives, especially those such as Veterans OutReach, an outstanding model for volunteer groups across the state and throughout the nation: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend Veterans OutReach for its outstanding service and dedication to the veterans of the State of Mississippi and express sincere gratitude to the volunteers, supporters and leadership of Veterans OutReach for their continued commitment to honoring, supporting and caring for Mississippi's veterans. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Veterans OutReach and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
