Senate Concurrent Resolution 542

(As Adopted by Senate and House)

A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION COMMEMORATING THE LIFE AND LEGACY OF JACKSON STATE UNIVERSITY TIGER MASCOT DAVID "WAVEE DAVE" CHAMBERS AND HIS COMMITMENT TO HIS ALMA MATER AND COMMUNITY ON THE OCCASION OF THE 75TH ANNIVERSARY CELEBRATION OF KAPPA ALPHA PSI FRATERNITY ON MARCH 17, 2026.

     WHEREAS, David "Wavee Dave" Chambers, a 1988 graduate of Jackson State University, embodied Tiger Pride for over two decades as the university's celebrated mascot, captivating fans with spirited performances, unicycle antics, and enthusiastic leadership that energized generations of students, alumni, and supporters; and

     WHEREAS, Mr. Chambers' creative and dynamic presence on the field helped build a culture of spirit and excellence at Jackson State University, inspiring the formation of the "Baby Tigers" and motivating young performers to pursue both academic success and passionate representation of "Thee Tiger Legacy"; and

     WHEREAS, Mr. Chambers graciously continued to bring joy and excitement to fans through his mascot role for more than 20 years, even as health challenges emerged, demonstrating unwavering commitment to his alma mater and the broader Jackson community; and

     WHEREAS, David "Wavee Dave" Chambers' life and legacy stand as enduring symbols of spirit, service, and inspiration, exemplifying the values of leadership, perseverance, and community engagement that reflect the best traditions of Jackson State University and the State of Mississippi; and

     WHEREAS, the 75th Anniversary of the Delta Delta Chapter of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc., chartered at Jackson State University, presents an appropriate occasion to honor individuals whose contributions have enriched the cultural, educational, and civic fabric of Mississippi:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING THEREIN, That we do hereby honor and recognize the famous Tiger Mascot David "Wavee Dave" Chambers for his significant contributions to the heritage and spirit of Jackson State University and the State of Mississippi.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That March 17, 2026, is designated as "Wavee Dave Day" in the State of Mississippi in tribute to his enduring legacy, and that citizens are encouraged to observe this day with appreciation for his influence on generations of Tiger fans and Mississippians.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to members of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc., on Kappa Day at the State Capitol honoring the legacy of notable members, and made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
