MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Representative Ford (73rd)

House Resolution 31

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE MISSISSIPPI COMMUNICATION ASSOCIATION ON THE OCCASION OF ITS 100TH ANNIVERSARY.

     WHEREAS, the Mississippi Communication Association was founded in 1926 by educators and scholars dedicated to the study, teaching and advancement of human communication; and

     WHEREAS, for 100 years, the Mississippi Communication Association has served as a professional organization committed to excellence in communication education, research and public discourse throughout the State of Mississippi; and

     WHEREAS, the association has provided a vital forum for teachers, students, researchers and practitioners to share scholarship, foster collaboration, and promote ethical and effective communication in classrooms, communities and civic life; and

     WHEREAS, the Mississippi Communication Association has played a significant role in strengthening higher education in Mississippi by supporting faculty development, student engagement and academic excellence across colleges and universities; and

     WHEREAS, through its conferences, publications and service, the association has contributed to the preparation of generations of Mississippians for leadership, citizenship and professional success; and

     WHEREAS, the year 2026 marks the 100th anniversary of the Mississippi Communication Association, a milestone worthy of recognition and celebration; and

     WHEREAS, the Mississippi Communication Association will commemorate its centennial anniversary on February 6, 2026, honoring its history, achievements and ongoing commitment to the advancement of communication in the State of Mississippi; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize outstanding organizations such as the Mississippi Communication Association, whose 100 years of unwavering service to bettering the lives of individuals and the profession as a whole has made Mississippi a better place for all its citizens:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Mississippi Communication Association on the occasion of its 100th anniversary and express heartfelt appreciation for its century of service to education, scholarship and the people of this state.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That we do hereby extend best wishes to the Mississippi Communication Association for continued success as they embark on their second century of service.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to the Mississippi Communication Association and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
