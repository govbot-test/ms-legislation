Section 1:
SB3154 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations By: Senator(s) Chassaniol Senate Bill 3154 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE CITY OF KOSCIUSKO, MISSISSIPPI, FOR THE PURPOSE OF DEFRAYING COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH CERTAIN INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS AND PURCHASES FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1. The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the City of Kosciusko, Mississippi, for the purpose of defraying costs associated with certain infrastructure projects and purchases for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027............................................. $ 1,100,988.00 SECTION 2.  Of the funds appropriated under the provisions of Section 1 of this act, the following amounts are to be allocated as follows: To defray costs of infrastructure improvements, including street paving, stormwater management, sewer and street department equipment and park enhancements............ $ 778,200.00 To defray costs of purchasing and equipping law enforcement vehicles.................................. $ 322,788.00 SECTION 3.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 4.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
