Section 1:
SB3385 (As Passed the Senate) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Local and Private By: Senator(s) Whaley Senate Bill 3385 (As Passed the Senate) AN ACT TO AMEND CHAPTER 946, LOCAL AND PRIVATE LAWS OF 2022, TO EXTEND THE REPEAL DATE ON THE AUTHORITY OF THE BOARD OF SUPERVISORS OF MARSHALL COUNTY, MISSISSIPPI, TO CONTRIBUTE FUNDS TO THE BYHALIA AREA ARTS COUNCIL; TO INCREASE THE AUTHORIZED CONTRIBUTION AMOUNT; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  Chapter 946, Local and Private Laws of 2022, is amended as follows: Section 1.  The Board of Supervisors of Marshall County, Mississippi, is authorized and empowered, in its discretion, to contribute an amount not to exceed * * * One Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($1,500.00) Seven Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($7,500.00) each fiscal year to the Byhalia Area Arts Council, a qualified charitable 501(c)(3) organization. Section 2.  This act shall be repealed from and after July 1, * * * 2026 2028. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage.
