SR 69 (As Adopted by Senate) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Senator(s) Fillingane Senate Resolution 69 (As Adopted by Senate) A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE COLUMBIA HIGH SCHOOL WILDCATS FOOTBALL TEAM FOR WINNING THE 2025 MISSISSIPPI HIGH SCHOOL ACTIVITIES ASSOCIATION (MHSAA) CLASS 4A STATE CHAMPIONSHIP. WHEREAS, the Columbia High School Wildcats Football Team from Marion County, Mississippi, distinguished itself during the 2025 football season by capturing the Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class 4A State Championship, earning statewide recognition for excellence in interscholastic athletics; and WHEREAS, the Wildcats concluded a remarkable postseason run with a championship victory on December 5, 2025, at Davis Wade Stadium in Starkville, Mississippi, defeating Kosciusko High School to secure the Class 4A state title, demonstrating discipline, resilience, and competitive excellence on Mississippi's largest high school football stage; and WHEREAS, throughout the regular season and playoffs, the Wildcats consistently displayed a balanced and dominant style of play, combining a strong defensive presence with timely and effective offensive execution, as reflected in their decisive victories during postseason competition; and WHEREAS, the team's success was achieved through the collective effort and commitment of its student-athletes, whose hard work, sportsmanship, and perseverance exemplified the ideals of Mississippi high school athletics; and WHEREAS, the championship achievement of the Columbia High School Wildcats Football Team was made possible through the efforts of the student-athletes on the 2025 Class 4A State Championship Team Roster:  Junior Curney, Tra Lewis, Christian Expose, Drew Bilderback, Keelan Williamson, Savarion Gray, Ashton Daniels, Chris May, Kavion Newson, BJ Lee, Tyrone Davis, Clyde Peters, Greyson Carney, Tyron Averett, Jonathan Lowery, Hagi Cain, Ashton Thompson, Brydan Webber, Larry Allen, Logan Johnson, Tyson Brown, Billy Fisher, JP Haddox, Alex Bolton, Tay Nguyen, Jordyn Edwards, Ashton Franklin, Jacob Harris, Terry Neal, Kaven Bridges, Amari Swarptue, Jadari Jefferson, Lawal Owokunle, Grady Swann, Tyson Thompson, Earl Loyd, Patrick Peters, Greg Laird, Achilles Pittman, Zentavious Sims, Tanner Kilgore, Tristen Ballew, Jeremy Harper, Ike Broome, Camden Fornea, Derick James, Mac Walley, Tobias Walker, Rico Crosby, Kathan Neese and Brayden Thornhill; Head Football Coach Chip Bilderback; Associate Head Football Coach Craig Cluff; Defensive Coordinator Chris Fortenberry; Assistant Coaches:  Eric Booth, Alex Davis, Kendrick Hardy, Kevin Hurley, Garon Malone and Jaymie Palmer; Athletic Trainer Nicole Keys; Team Doctor, Dr. Bert Beisel; and Team Managers: Quinlan Williams, Jake Racadio, Damien Williams, Jakwon Lewis and Jamari Peters; and WHEREAS, the 2025 Class 4A State Championship is a source of pride for Columbia High School students, families, and supporters, reinforcing the values of dedication and teamwork; and WHEREAS, it is with great pride that we recognize these student-athletes who have brought honor to their school, their county, and the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Columbia High School Wildcats Football Team for winning the 2025 Mississippi High School Activities Association Class 4A State Championship, and extend to the team, coaches, parents and sponsors our best wishes for continued success in all their future academic and athletic endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to the administration of Columbia High School, Head Coach Chip Bilderback on behalf of the Wildcats Football Team, and made available to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
