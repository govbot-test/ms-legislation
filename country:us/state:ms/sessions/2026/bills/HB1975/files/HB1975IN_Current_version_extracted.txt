Section 1:
HB1975 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations A By: Representative Bailey (23rd) House Bill 1975 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE BOARD OF SUPERVISORS OF CALHOUN COUNTY TO ASSIST IN PAYING COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH REPAIRING AND RESURFACING COUNTY ROAD 418 AND REPAIRING AND RESEALING COUNTY ROAD 433 FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the Board of Supervisors of Calhoun County, Mississippi, to assist in paying costs associated with repairing and resurfacing County Road 418 and repairing and resealing County Road 433 for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027.... $ 1,100,000.00 SECTION 2.  The funds appropriated by Section 1 of this act shall be expended as follows: (a)  Repairing and resurfacing County Road 418......... ................................................... $ 500,000.00 (b)  Repairing and resealing County Road 433........... ................................................... $ 600,000.00 TOTAL............................................ $ 1,100,000.00 SECTION 3.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 4.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
