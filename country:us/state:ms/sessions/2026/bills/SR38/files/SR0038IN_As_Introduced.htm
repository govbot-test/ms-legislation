MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Senator(s) Whaley, England, Michel

Senate Resolution 38

A RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING BRINKLEY BOSWELL OF STAR, MISSISSIPPI, AS "MISS RODEO MISSISSIPPI 2026" ON THE OCCASION OF THE DIXIE NATIONAL LIVESTOCK AND RODEO SHOW.

     WHEREAS, Brinkley Boswell, daughter of Peter and Laura Boswell, is from Star, Mississippi, and is honored to serve as "Miss Rodeo Mississippi 2026"; and

     WHEREAS, a graduate of the University of Mississippi with a degree in Marketing and Communications, Brinkley spends much of her time traveling across Mississippi and beyond, representing and promoting the sport of rodeo, agriculture, and the western way of life; and

     WHEREAS, her platform, "Be a FAN:  First to Serve, Always Kind, and Never Above Anyone," reflects her heart for service, community, and her mission to turn every rodeo spectator into a fan; and

     WHEREAS, outside of the arena, Brinkley enjoys working her horses, spending time with friends and family, volunteering with her church, and staying active; and

     WHEREAS, even as a young girl, Brinkley has always had a love for animals and the western lifestyle, all of which make her truly honored to be crowned Miss Rodeo Mississippi 2026:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby recognize Brinkley Boswell of Star, Mississippi, as "Miss Rodeo Mississippi 2026" on the occasion of the Dixie National Livestock and Rodeo Show and extend to her our best wishes for future success.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Brinkley Boswell, forwarded to the Commissioner of Agriculture and Commerce and made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
