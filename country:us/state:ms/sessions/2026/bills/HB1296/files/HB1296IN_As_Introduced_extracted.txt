Section 1:
HB1296 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Public Health and Human Services By: Representative Owen House Bill 1296 AN ACT TO DIRECT THE STATE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH TO CONDUCT EDUCATIONAL ACTIVITIES FOR HEALTH CARE PROVIDERS, PATIENTS AND THE PUBLIC ON THE SIGNS, SYMPTOMS AND MANAGEMENT OF AMNIOTIC FLUID EMBOLISM; TO PROVIDE THAT THE DEPARTMENT SHALL MAKE AVAILABLE INFORMATION ABOUT AMNIOTIC FLUID EMBOLISM ON THE DEPARTMENT'S WEBSITE; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1. (1)  This section shall be known as "Maeghan's Law." (2)  As used in this section, "amniotic fluid embolism" means a rare obstetric emergency in which amniotic fluid, fetal cells, hair or other debris enters the maternal bloodstream leading to cardiovascular collapse, respiratory failure, and disseminated intravascular coagulation. (3)  The State Department of Health shall conduct educational activities for health care providers, patients and the public on the signs, symptoms and management of amniotic fluid embolism. (4)  The department shall make available information about amniotic fluid embolism on the department's website. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
