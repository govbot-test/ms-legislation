2026 Regular Session

House Resolution 28

(As Adopted by House)

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING MRS. LESHANDA JOHNSON UPON BEING NAMED SUMMIT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL'S 2025 TEACHER OF THE YEAR.

     WHEREAS, Mrs. LeShanda Johnson, an outstanding and well admired 4th grade English Arts teacher in Summit, Mississippi, is being celebrated and honored as she has been named Summit Elementary School's 2025 Teacher of the Year; and

     WHEREAS, a truly exceptional educator, Mrs. Johnson is known for pouring her heart into teaching as well as for having her classroom as a special haven where students feel seen, challenged and encouraged to grow as readers, writers and confident leaders; and

     WHEREAS, Mrs. Johnson's exceptional instructional excellence and unwavering commitment to the children she serves each day is exemplified daily with a spirit of zeal and joy that is showered upon her students; and

     WHEREAS, Mrs. Johnson's collaboration with colleagues, students and families is known for creating a school culture of respect and success and her demonstration of leadership and innovation inside and outside of the classroom serves a model for her colleagues and others; and

     WHEREAS, in addition to her success in the classroom, Mrs. Johnson is a full-time caretaker of her husband, Mr. Dedrick Johnson, a respected former coach and teacher at McComb High School, who has also made a profound impact on countless students throughout his career; and

     WHEREAS, Mrs. Johnson truly represents the very best of education as she is selfless, committed and deeply rooted in love for children and the community; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize, honor and celebrate inspirational educators such as Mrs. Johnson whose effective classroom teaching is truly the backbone in creating an exceptional education system that provides  students with knowledge and skills to be successful as they are this great State's future:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate Mrs. LeShanda Johnson on being named Summit Elementary School's 2025 Teacher of the Year, and wish her continued success both inside and outside of the classroom in all her future endeavors.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Mrs. LeShanda Johnson, Lakya Washington, Principal of Summit Elementary School, Superintendent of the McComb School District and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
