Senate Bill 2563

AN ACT TO EXEMPT CERTAIN REGISTERED PROVIDERS OF SERVICES FOR HOMELESS PERSONS FROM CERTAIN FILING FEES AND LICENSURE APPLICATION FEES; TO AMEND SECTIONS 25-7-9 AND 63-1-43, MISSISSIPPI CODE OF 1972, TO CONFORM; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES.

     BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI:

     SECTION 1.  Providers of services for homeless persons registered with a Continuum of Care Program shall be exempted:

          (a)  From the civil filing fees in chancery court for matters relating to the alteration of birth and marriage certificates for homeless persons.

          (b)  From the application fees for driver's licenses, identifications cards, or learner's permits under Section 63-1-43 for homeless persons.
