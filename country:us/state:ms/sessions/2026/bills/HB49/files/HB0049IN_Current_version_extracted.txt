Section 1:
HB 49 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations A By: Representative Johnson House Bill 49 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE GOVERNING AUTHORITIES OF THE CITY OF NATCHEZ TO ASSIST IN PAYING COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH THE ROSELAWN SUBDIVISION STORMWATER INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECT, WEST STIERS LANE STORMWATER INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECT AND CONCORD AVENUE STORMWATER INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECT FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the governing authorities of the City of Natchez, Mississippi, to assist in paying costs associated with the Roselawn Subdivision Stormwater Infrastructure Project, West Stiers Lane Stormwater Infrastructure Project and Concord Avenue Stormwater Infrastructure Project for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027 ................................................. $ 5,450,000.00 SECTION 2.  The money appropriated by Section 1 shall be used for the following purposes: (a)  Roselawn Subdivision Stormwater Infrastructure Project $ 2,550,000.00 (b)  West Stiers Lane Stormwater Infrastructure Project ................................................. $ 1,600,000.00 (c)  Concord Avenue Stormwater Infrastructure Project.. ................................................. $ 1,300,000.00 TOTAL............................................ $ 5,450,000.00 SECTION 3.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 4.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
