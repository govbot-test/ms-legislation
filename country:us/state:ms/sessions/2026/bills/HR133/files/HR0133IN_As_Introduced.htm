MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Representative Hood

House Resolution 133

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE EUPORA HIGH SCHOOL EAGLES CHEERLEADERS AND CHEER COACHES JAMIE WALKER AND KRISTY TINDALL ON WINNING THE 2025 MISSISSIPPI HIGH SCHOOL ACTIVITIES ASSOCIATION (MHSAA) 2A STATE CHAMPIONSHIP.

     WHEREAS, on December 13, 2025, with an unbelievable performance surely to be remembered for years to come, the Eupora High School Eagles Cheerleading Team reigned supreme when the cheerleaders captured the 2025 Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) 2A Game Day Division Cheer State Championship at the Mississippi Coliseum in Jackson, Mississippi; and

     WHEREAS, the Eupora Eagles' incredible state title solidified the squad's high-performing reputation as a consistent program known for its precise movements appearing effortless as the athletically fit members of the team safely performed complex routines as a single, cohesive unit throughout the season; and

     WHEREAS, through the Eupora High School cheerleaders' dedication and excellence in performance, members of the team have demonstrated exceptional character and discipline, whether in the classroom or on the sidelines or the mat, and have developed unparalleled teamwork representing the highest standards of sportsmanship and leadership through school spirit; and

     WHEREAS, the extraordinary success of the Eupora High School Eagles would not have been possible without the countless hours of intense practice, positive attitudes and enthusiasm of each of the following talented student-athletes who comprise the 2025 cheerleading squad:  Chloe Bass, Gwendolyn Browder, Mallory Collum, Gracie Dean, Caleigh Fason, Olivia Frank, Kinley Gilbert, Emma Gray, Adalee Hardin, Swayze Hodges, Kinsley Huffman, Madison Johnson, Brinkley Lee, Ansley Mason, Avery McCluskey, Taylor Payton, Bentley Ray, Tinsley Richardson, Jamesha Rush, Cydney Smith and Corinne Walker; and

     WHEREAS, the cheerleaders have been superbly coached and inspired by Cheer Coaches Jamie Walker and Kristy Tindall, who together have combined their considerable talents and years of experience to refine the squad's skills and precision in execution; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and honor the achievements of outstanding student-athletes such as the Eupora High School Eagles Cheerleaders, who have brought pride and honor to their families, school, community and the State of Mississippi:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Eupora High School Eagles Cheerleaders and Cheer Coaches Jamie Walker and Kristy Tindall on winning the Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class 2A Game Day Division Cheer State Championship and extend best wishes for continued success in all their future athletic and academic endeavors.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to the Eupora High School Eagles Cheerleading Team and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
