Adopted

 

AMENDMENT NO 1 PROPOSED TO

 

Cmte Sub for House Bill No. 1940

 

BY: Representative Oliver

 

     AMEND by inserting the following new Sections 10 through 13 and renumbering the succeeding section:

"SECTION 10.  In addition to all other sums appropriated to the department, the following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the State Department of Education for the period beginning upon passage of this act and ending June 30, 2027................................................ $ 9,000,000.00

     This additional appropriation is for the implementation and operation of the Adolescent Literacy Initiative created in the House amendment to Senate Bill No. 2294, 2026 Regular Session.

SECTION 11.  In addition to all other sums appropriated to the department, the following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the State Department of Education for the period beginning upon passage of this act and ending June 30, 2027................................................ $ 3,480,000.00

     This additional appropriation is for the implementation and operation of the Mississippi Math Act, including the Moving Mathematics in Mississippi Program, created in the House amendment to Senate Bill No. 2294, 2026 Regular Session.

SECTION 12.  In addition to all other sums appropriated to the department, the following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the State Department of Education for the period beginning upon passage of this act and ending June 30, 2027.................................................. $ 120,600.00

     This additional appropriation is for the incorporation of financial literacy components within the existing curriculum offered in Grades 6 through 8, as required in the House amendment to Senate Bill No. 2294, 2026 Regular Session.

     SECTION 13.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State Treasury to the credit of the proper fund or funds as set forth in this act, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers, in the manner provided by law."

     AMEND FURTHER the title on line 1 by inserting the words "GENERAL FUNDS," before the word "CAPITAL"
