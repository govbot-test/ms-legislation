This additional appropriation is for the purpose of defraying expenses related to the construction and furnishing of the Department of Public Safety headquarters. SECTION 5.  In addition to all other sums herein appropriated, the following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money to the credit of the Capital Expense Fund, and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the Department of Finance and Administration for the period beginning upon passage of this act and ending June 30, 2027..... $ 6,000,000.00. This additional appropriation is for the purpose of defraying operational expenses, including, but not limited to, salaries resulting from the loss of the Emergency Management Performance Grant (EMPG). SECTION 8.  In addition to all other sums herein appropriated, the following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money to the credit of the Capital Expense Fund, and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the Mississippi Emergency Management Agency for the period beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2026.. $ 2,672,869.00. This additional appropriation is for the purpose of providing funding to counties in a proportional amount that would have been received through the Emergency Management Performance Grant (EMPG). SECTION 9.  In addition to all other sums herein appropriated, the following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State Treasury to the credit of the Administrative Office of Courts and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the Mississippi State Supreme Court - Administrative Office of Courts for the period beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2026.. $ 4,000,000.00. This additional appropriation is for the purpose of defraying expenses related to court reporters and trial judge support staff. SECTION 10.  In addition to all other sums herein appropriated, the following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money to the credit of the Capital Expense Fund, and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the Mississippi State Supreme Court - Administrative Office of Courts for the period beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2026............................................................... ................................................ $ 796,572.00. This additional appropriation is for the purpose of defraying expenses related to trial judge appointments and payments of terminal leave. SECTION 11.  In addition to all other sums herein appropriated, the following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State Treasury to the credit of the Mississippi Board of Medical Licensure and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the Mississippi Board of Medical Licensure for the period beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2026....................................................... ................................................ $ 25,000.00. This additional appropriation is for the purpose of defraying expenses related to the replacement of a vehicle. SECTION 12.  In addition to all other sums herein appropriated, the following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State Treasury to the credit of the Tidelands Fund No. 3345200000 to the Department of Marine Resources and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the Department of Marine Resources for the period beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2026............ $ 5,309,560.00. This additional appropriation is for the purpose of defraying expenses related to prior year Tidelands projects. SECTION 13.  In addition to all other sums herein appropriated, the following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money to the credit of the Education Enhancement Fund, and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the Mississippi Department of Education for the period beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2026.. $    16,626.00. This additional appropriation is for the purpose of defraying expenses related to the Educable Child Program. SECTION 14.  In addition to all other sums herein appropriated, the following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State Treasury to the credit of the Board of Examiners for Social Workers and Marriage and Family Therapists and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the Board of Examiners for Social Workers and Marriage and Family Therapists for the period beginning upon passage of this act, and ending June 30, 2027......................... $    50,000.00. This additional appropriation is for the purpose of defraying expenses related to licensing system upgrades and maintenance. SECTION 15.  In addition to all other sums herein appropriated, the following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money to the credit of the Capital Expense Fund, and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the Department of Human Services for the period beginning upon passage of this act and ending June 30, 2027. $ 4,500,000.00. This additional appropriation is made for the purpose of defraying the expense related to income verification software. SECTION 16.  In addition to all other sums herein appropriated, the following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State Treasury to the credit of the Department of Human Services and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the Department of Human Services for the period beginning upon passage of this act and ending June 30, 2027............................................................... ................................................ $ 1,500,000.00. This additional appropriation is made for the purpose of defraying the expense related to income verification software. SECTION 17.  In addition to all other sums herein appropriated, the following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money to the credit of the Capital Expense Fund, and allocated in a manner as determined by the Treasurer's Office, to defray the expenses of the Mississippi State Department of Health for the period beginning upon passage of this act and ending June 30, 2027..... $ 3,748,030.00. This additional appropriation is for the purpose of defraying legal expenses associated with the City of Jackson Water litigation. SECTION 18.  In addition to all other sums herein appropriated, the following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any special source funds which are received or otherwise become available to the Mississippi Office of Student Financial Aid, to defray the expenses of the Mississippi Office of Student Financial Aid for the period beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2026.......... ................................................ $ 5,100,000.00. This additional appropriation is for the purpose of defraying expenses related to Higher Education Legislative Plan Grant (HELP), Mississippi Resident Tuition Assistance Grant (MTAG), and Mississippi Eminent Scholars Grant (MESG). SECTION 19.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State Treasury to the credit of the proper fund or funds as set forth in this act not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers, in the manner provided by law. SECTION 20. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after passage. Further, amend by striking the title in its entirety and inserting in lieu thereof the following: AN ACT MAKING AN ADDITIONAL APPROPRIATION OF CAPITAL EXPENSE FUNDS, EDUCATION ENHANCEMENT FUNDS AND SPECIAL FUNDS FOR FISCAL YEARS 2026 AND 2027 TO DEFRAY THE EXPENSES OF THE OFFICE OF THE ATTORNEY GENERAL; THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC SAFETY; THE DEPARTMENT OF FINANCE AND ADMINISTRATION; THE MISSISSIPPI EMERGENCY MANAGEMENT AGENCY; THE MISSISSIPPI STATE SUPREME COURT - ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICE OF COURTS; THE MISSISSIPPI BOARD OF MEDICAL LICENSURE; THE DEPARTMENT OF MARINE RESOURCES; THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION; THE BOARD OF EXAMINERS FOR SOCIAL WORKERS AND MARRIAGE AND FAMILY THERAPISTS; THE DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN SERVICES; THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH; THE MISSISSIPPI OFFICE OF STUDENT FINANCIAL AID; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES.
