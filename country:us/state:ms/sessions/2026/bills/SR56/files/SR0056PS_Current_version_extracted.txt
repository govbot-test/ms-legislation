SR 56 (As Adopted by Senate) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Senator(s) Fillingane Senate Resolution 56 (As Adopted by Senate) A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE SUMRALL HIGH SCHOOL BOBCATS SOCCER TEAM AND COACH JARED LEE FOR WINNING THEIR FIRST-EVER CLASS 5A STATE CHAMPIONSHIP. WHEREAS, the Sumrall High School Bobcats Soccer Team captured its first-ever State Championship in boys soccer with a 4-0 win at the 2026 Class 5A Soccer Championship on February 21, 2026; and WHEREAS, senior Wyatt Jones was named as the game's MVP, scoring three goals on the afternoon; and WHEREAS, Head Coach Jared Lee said, "This year's team understood its mission.  They're special because so many people knew their roles and played them to perfection.  It's been a year, not a year-long process, but a multiyear process of continuous growth, with people buying in, giving everything they have, committing to the program, and trusting that we'll get here one day.  Sometimes that takes time, and it has taken us time as well.  To finally be here and see that growth pay off for the boys, I'm so happy for then.  They've 100% earned it, not just this year"; and WHEREAS, the 2025-2026 Sumrall High School Bobcats Soccer roster is:  Blake Breazeale, Warner Jones, Boston Trussell, Sawyer Hamner, Hudson Long, Carson McMullen, Landon Banks, Mac Curtis, Beau Ewert, Wyatt Jones, Henry Hamner, Tyler Gaughan, Brody Graham, Nathan Johnson, Cooper Walker, Landon Brewer, Wes Corts, Fisher Lee, Brad Loftin, Landry Haden, Carsen Leslie, Riley Eddins and Tripp Richards; and Coach Jared Lee; and WHEREAS, it is with great pride that we recognize this group of student-athletes who have brought honor to their school, their community and to the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Sumrall High School Bobcats Soccer Team and Coach Jared Lee for winning their first-ever Class 5A State Championship, and extend to the team, coaches, parents and sponsors our best wishes for future success. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Coach Jared Lee and Sumrall Principal Sandy Hampton for the team, forwarded to the Sumrall School Board, and made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
