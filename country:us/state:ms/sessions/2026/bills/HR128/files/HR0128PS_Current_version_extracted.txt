HR 128 (As Adopted by House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representatives Summers, Butler-Washington House Resolution 128 (As Adopted by House) A RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING AND COMMENDING PAMELA MOODY FOR BEING NAMED THE 20252026 OUTSTANDING MUSIC EDUCATOR FOR MISSISSIPPI BY THE NATIONAL FEDERATION OF STATE HIGH SCHOOL ASSOCIATIONS. WHEREAS, Pamela Moody, the Choral Director at Jim Hill High School, has spent more than two decades helping scholars find their voices, and now her work is being recognized on a national stage; and WHEREAS, Mrs. Moody has been named a 20252026 Outstanding Music Educator for Section 3 (Mississippi) by the National Federation of State High School Associations (NFHS), and this outstanding honor places her among a select group of performing arts educators nationwide who are recognized for excellence, leadership and lasting impact; and WHEREAS, each year, NFHS honors outstanding educators across music, speech and debate, and theatre, and for the 20252026 school year, only eight high school music educators nationwide were selected for section-level recognition, and Mrs. Moody represents Mississippi in Section 3; and WHEREAS, Mrs. Moody's career with Jackson Public Schools spans more than 20 years, beginning at Siwell Middle School and continuing at Jim Hill High School, where her choral ensembles consistently earn superior ratings at district, state, and national competitions, and colleagues credit her success to high expectations, intentional instruction and an unwavering belief in her scholars; and WHEREAS, with an influence that extends well beyond her own classroom, Mrs. Moody serves as High School Choral Division Chair for Jackson Public Schools and District VI Chair for the Mississippi Music Educators Association, and she mentors future educators, conducts honor choirs, and leads professional development workshops for choral directors across the state; and WHEREAS, Mrs. Moody holds advanced degrees in music education and educational leadership and was named Mississippi Music Educator of the Year in 2025, and as she is known for her professionalism, artistry, and service, she continues to elevate music education while creating spaces where scholars are challenged, supported and inspired; and WHEREAS, this national recognition reflects not only Mrs. Moody's dedication to her craft, but also the strength of arts education within Jackson Public Schools, and for her scholars and colleagues alike, her leadership is a reminder that excellence, when paired with purpose, has the power to resonate far beyond the stage; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to honor outstanding Mississippi educators, especially those such as Mrs. Moody, whose dedication and leadership bring honor to the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby recognize and commend Pamela Moody for being named the 20252026 Outstanding Music Educator for Mississippi by the National Federation of State High School Associations, and extend best wishes for continued success in all her future endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Pamela Moody and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
