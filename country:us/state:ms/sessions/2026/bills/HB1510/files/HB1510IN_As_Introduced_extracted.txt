Section 1:
HB1510 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Judiciary B; Appropriations A By: Representative Bell (65th) House Bill 1510 AN ACT TO AMEND SECTION 25-32-4, MISSISSIPPI CODE OF 1972, TO INCREASE THE NUMBER OF ASSISTANT PUBLIC DEFENDERS AND STAFF FOR THE SEVENTH CIRCUIT COURT DISTRICT; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  Section 25-32-4, Mississippi Code of 1972, is amended as follows: 25-32-4.  The public defender of the Seventh Circuit Court District may appoint * * *three (3) five (5) full-time assistant public defenders, one (1) investigator, and one (1) legal assistant who shall perform duties in the Seventh Circuit Court District and the Capitol Complex Improvement District (CCID) Inferior Court.  Such appointments shall be made in addition to those authorized as of July 1, 2023 in Section 25-32-3.  The full-time assistant public defenders and support staff shall receive compensation in an amount equal to the compensation paid to full-time assistant public defenders and support staff in the Seventh Circuit Court District subject to available funds specifically appropriated by the Legislature. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
