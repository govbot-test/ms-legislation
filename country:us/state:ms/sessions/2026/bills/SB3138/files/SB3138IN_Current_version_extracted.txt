Section 1:
SB3138 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations By: Senator(s) Mumford Senate Bill 3138 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE TOWN OF BOLTON, MISSISSIPPI, FOR THE PURPOSE OF DEFRAYING COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH INFRASTRUCTURE IMPROVEMENTS TO CERTAIN STRUCTURES FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1. The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the Town of Bolton, Mississippi, for the purpose of defraying costs associated with infrastructure improvements to certain structures for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027....................................................... ................................................ $ 2,750,000.00. SECTION 2.  Of the funds appropriated under the provisions of Section 1 of this act, the following amounts are to be allocated as follows: To defray costs of repair of, renovation of, and improvements to the city's town hall........................ $ 1,500,000.00 To defray costs of repair of, renovation of, and improvements to the former Department of Human Services building to convert the building to a community center and multipurpose building................... $ 1,250,000.00 SECTION 3.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 4.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
