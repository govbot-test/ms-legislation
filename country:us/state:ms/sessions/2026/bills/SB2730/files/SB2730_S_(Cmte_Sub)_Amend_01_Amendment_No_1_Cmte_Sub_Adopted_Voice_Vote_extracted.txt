SB2730_S_(Cmte_Sub)_Amend_01 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 1 PROPOSED TO Cmte Sub for Senate Bill No. 2730 BY: Senator(s) Simmons (12th) AMEND below line 32 by inserting the following as a new section and renumbering subsequent sections accordingly: SECTION 3.  The immunity from civil liability granted under this act shall not apply to any act or omission arising out of wanton, willful, or intentional misconduct. FURTHER, AMEND by inserting before the period on line 35 the following language: except as provided in Section 3 of this act FURTHER, AMEND by inserting before the period on line 37 the following language: except for conduct described in Section 3 of this act FURTHER, AMEND by striking the following language on line 43: , and shall stand repealed on June 30, 2026 FURTHER, AMEND the title to conform.
