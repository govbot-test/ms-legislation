HB 794 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations A By: Representative James-Jones House Bill 794 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE BOARD OF SUPERVISORS OF SHARKEY COUNTY TO ASSIST IN PAYING COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH IMPLEMENTING BUSINESS RECOVERY ACTIVITIES, BEGINNING SITE DEVELOPMENT FOR TWO PENDING BUSINESSES, AND ESTABLISHING BUSINESS ATTRACTION AND RETENTION STRATEGIES TO ENCOURAGE NEW BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT AND HELP EXISTING BUSINESSES FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the Board of Supervisors of Sharkey County, Mississippi, to assist in paying costs associated with implementing business recovery activities, beginning site development for two (2) pending businesses, and establishing business attraction and retention strategies to encourage new business development and help existing businesses struggling to recover from reduced revenues and a shortage of employees resulting from the effects of the 2023 tornado for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027.. $ 1,000,000.00. SECTION 2.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 3.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
