Senate Resolution 47

(As Adopted by Senate)

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE SOUTH PANOLA HIGH SCHOOL AIR FORCE JROTC DRONE TEAM FOR ITS THIRD CONSECUTIVE GRAND CHAMPIONSHIP IN A SINGLE SEASON.

     WHEREAS, with sharp focus, solid teamwork, and outstanding leadership, the South Panola High School Air Force JROTC Drone Team has pulled off a truly historic achievement  a perfect three-event sweep, taking home its third consecutive Grand Championship at the Big JROTC Showdown in Mesquite, Texas; and

     WHEREAS, operating like a well-oiled machine, Team A soared to victory as Grand Champions, battling top-tier competition from Texas and Arkansas.  Meanwhile, Team C secured the Judge's Award, recognized for its professionalism, teamwork, and creative decision-making under pressure; and

     WHEREAS, this victory marks a first in school history.  Never before has a South Panola Air Force JROTC drone team captured three major championship titles in a single season.  This was the result of discipline, commitment, and the never-quit mindset that defines South Panola Cadets; and

     WHEREAS, the South Panola High School Air Force JROTC Drone Team accomplished the following titles:  (a) third consecutive Championship in a single season, (b) top 4 in the nation among all Air Force JROTC drone programs, (c) top 50 in the world - competing with the best globally.  All-South Panola JROTC programs are ranked in the Top 25 nationwide across all service branches.  Now, the team sets its sights on the next mission  the Marion Military Institute STEM Challenge in Marion, Alabama.  South Panola's motto is:  Flight is Our Legacy.  The Sky Our Command; and

     WHEREAS, "This is more than a win  it's a message to every JROTC team out there," said Tech Sergeant Rodney Williams, Aerospace Science Instructor and Drone Team Head Coach.  "South Panola Cadets are showing what leadership, discipline, and heart can achieve."  These wins show more than talent.  They prove what happens when cadets train hard, support each other, and lead with character; and

     WHEREAS, with the guidance of instructors Tech Sergeant Williams, Master Sergeant Vonetta Roseman, Ms. Lashandra Carr, and Mrs. Denise Pope, South Panola is setting a new bar for excellence.  They'll go for an unprecedented fourth Grand Championship in a single season; and

     WHEREAS, it is with great pride that we recognize this outstanding group of student-athletes who "flew, coded, strategized and supported" their team to unprecedented victory, bringing honor to their school, community and to the State of Mississippi:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the South Panola High School Air Force JROTC Drone Team for its third consecutive Grand Championship in a single season, and extend our best wishes for future competition success.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to South Panola Drone instructors on behalf of the team, forwarded to South Panola Principal David Odom and the South Panola School District School Board, and be made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
