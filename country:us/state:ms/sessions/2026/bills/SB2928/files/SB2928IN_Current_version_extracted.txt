Section 1:
SB2928 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Local and Private; Judiciary, Division A By: Senator(s) Fillingane Senate Bill 2928 AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE BOARD OF SUPERVISORS OF LAMAR COUNTY, MISSISSIPPI, TO EMPLOY THE PART-TIME COUNTY PROSECUTING ATTORNEY AS A FULL-TIME ELECTED OFFICIAL DURING HIS OR HER TERM OF OFFICE AND TO PRESCRIBE THE COMPENSATION FOR SUCH ATTORNEY; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The Board of Supervisors of Lamar County, Mississippi, in its discretion, may employ the part-time county prosecuting attorney as a full-time elected official during his or her term of office.  Such attorney shall receive additional compensation of ninety percent (90%) of the annual salary of the Lamar County and Youth Court Judge.  The salary authorized by this section and by Section 25-3-9, Mississippi Code of 1972, for the county prosecuting attorney shall be the sole and complete salary to be paid by Lamar County for such attorney, notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage.
