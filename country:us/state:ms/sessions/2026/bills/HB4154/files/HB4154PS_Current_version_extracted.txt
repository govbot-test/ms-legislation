HB4154 (As Passed the House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Judiciary B By: Representative James-Jones House Bill 4154 (As Passed the House) AN ACT TO RESTORE THE RIGHT OF SUFFRAGE TO KAREN SMITH OF YAZOO COUNTY, MISSISSIPPI. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The right of suffrage is hereby fully and completely restored to Karen Smith of Yazoo County, Mississippi, who was disqualified as an elector as a result of her conviction for the crime of Embezzlement in Yazoo County, on March 22, 2011, for which she was sentenced to serve ten (10) years probation under the supervision of the Mississippi Department of Corrections. She was discharged on November 19, 2018.  The Legislature is informed that she has since conducted herself as a law-abiding and honorable citizen in a good and lawful manner. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage.
