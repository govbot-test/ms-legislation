MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

House Resolution 40

(As Adopted by House)

A RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING AND COMMENDING JORDAN ANTHONY FOR WINNING THE 2025 BOWERMAN AWARD AND FOR HIS MANY EXTRAORDINARY ACCOMPLISHMENTS.

     WHEREAS, track and field champion, Jordan Anthony, of Tylertown, Mississippi, has won the 2025 Bowerman Award, a truly outstanding achievement; and 

     WHEREAS, while a student at Tylertown High School, Jordan won the Gatorade Track and Field Player of the Year Award in the 2020-2021 and 2021-2022 seasons, and he set new records in the State of Mississippi for the 100m (10.12s) and 200m (20.35s), earning national acclaim as one of America's fastest high school sprinters; and

     WHEREAS, in 2021, Jordan won both the Nike 100m and the Adidas Boston Boost High School Games 100m championship titles, and in 2022, he won the indoor Nike National 60m and 200m as well as the outdoor USA Track and Field (USATF) under 20 200m championship titles; and

     WHEREAS, in 2025, Jordan was named National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) champion, winning both the indoor 60m and the outdoor 100m events, and SEC champion, winning the indoor 60m and the outdoor 100m and 200m events; and

     WHEREAS, as an University of Arkansas Razorback, Jordan broke program records for the 60m (6.47s) and 100m (9.75s) dashes, making him the ninth fastest runner in the history of the world, and his brilliant performance while at Arkansas culminated in his winning the 2025 Bowerman Award, recognizing him as the top student-athlete in American collegiate track and field; and

     WHEREAS, after winning the 2025 NCAA championship in Eugene, Oregon, in June 2025, Jordan announced that he was officially turning pro in track and field, and he was a dual-sport athlete at the University of Arkansas, competing as a sprinter and a wide receiver on the football team, and now, he will focus solely on his professional track career, and with his exceptional speed, he is considered a contender for Team USA at future Olympic games; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to honor outstanding Mississippians, especially those such as Jordan Anthony, who has exemplified the highest standards of excellence and has brought honor to Tylertown, Walthall County, the State of Mississippi, and to all the individuals and communities inspired by his stellar character, dedication and extraordinary achievements:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby recognize and commend Jordan Anthony for winning the 2025 Bowerman Award and for his many extraordinary accomplishments.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Jordan Anthony and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
