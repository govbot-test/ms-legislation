HR 167 (As Adopted by House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Arnold House Resolution 167 (As Adopted by House) A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING COACH MICHAEL SMITH UPON HIS EXTRAORDINARY SUCCESS AS HEAD COACH OF THE BOONEVILLE HIGH SCHOOL BASKETBALL PROGRAM AND RECOGNIZING HIS LASTING IMPACT ON STUDENT-ATHLETES AND THE COMMUNITY OF PRENTISS COUNTY. WHEREAS, Coach Michael Smith has established himself as one of the most accomplished and respected high school basketball coaches in the State of Mississippi, serving as Head Coach of the Booneville High School Basketball Program and Athletic Director, and in doing so, has elevated the standard of excellence for interscholastic athletics in northeast Mississippi; and WHEREAS, over the course of an extraordinary 22-year coaching career, Coach Smith has amassed an astounding 765 career victories and secured 11 state championships, a testament to his sustained excellence, discipline and commitment to building a premier basketball program in the City of Booneville; and WHEREAS, under his leadership, the Booneville Blue Devils have become synonymous with competitive greatness, recently capturing the MHSAA Girls' Class 3A State Championship with a commanding 54-31 victory over Belmont High School, marking the program's fourth state title in five years and further solidifying its place among Mississippi's elite; and WHEREAS, Coach Smith's remarkable career winning percentage of approximately 75 percent places him in rare company alongside some of the most revered figures in the history of basketball, demonstrating that excellence is not confined to the national stage, but may be cultivated and sustained in communities where dedication, vision and purpose reside; and WHEREAS, embodying the philosophy of "making the big time where you are," Coach Smith has transformed Booneville, a small town in Prentiss County, into a beacon of basketball excellence, so much so that the Booneville High School basketball court fittingly bears his name in recognition of his enduring contributions; and WHEREAS, beyond wins and championships, Coach Smith's true legacy lies in his unwavering commitment to the development of young people, instilling in his student-athletes the values of discipline, perseverance, humility and respect, and emphasizing that the measure of success is not solely found in victories, but in the lives shaped and the character built along the way; and WHEREAS, Coach Smith's leadership is rooted in compassion and authenticity, as evidenced by his steadfast devotion to all of his players, celebrating their triumphs while sharing in their disappointments, demonstrating that true coaching extends far beyond the scoreboard and into the hearts and lives of those he mentors; and WHEREAS, a proud product of northeast Mississippi, Coach Smith excelled academically as valedictorian at New Site High School and continued his education at Northeast Mississippi Community College and the University of Mississippi, where he graduated with honors, equipping himself with the knowledge and purpose to impact future generations through education and athletics; and WHEREAS, his passion for coaching was ignited by a simple yet profound moment of mentorship at a youth basketball camp, where he witnessed firsthand the transformative power of encouragement and instruction, a moment that has since defined his life's work; and WHEREAS, his influence extends beyond Booneville, as he has been selected to coach the Mississippi Boys All-Star Team, further affirming the respect and admiration he commands across the state; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize individuals such as Coach Michael Smith, whose life's work reflects the very best of Mississippi values, demonstrating that through faith, humility and dedication, one may achieve greatness while uplifting others: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate Coach Michael Smith upon his outstanding coaching success as Head Coach of the Booneville High School Basketball Program, and extend highest appreciation for his immeasurable contributions to the lives of his student-athletes, the Booneville community and the State of Mississippi. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Coach Michael Smith and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
