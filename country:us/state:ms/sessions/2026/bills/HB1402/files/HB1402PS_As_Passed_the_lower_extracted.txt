Section 1:
HB1402 (As Passed the House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Transportation By: The Entire Membership House Bill 1402 (As Passed the House) AN ACT TO DESIGNATE A SEGMENT OF MISSISSIPPI HIGHWAY 49 LOCATED IN YAZOO COUNTY, MISSISSIPPI, AS THE "COLONEL DONNELL BERRY MEMORIAL HIGHWAY"; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  (1)  A segment of U.S. Highway 49 North located in Yazoo County, Mississippi, and extending for a distance of approximately one (1) mile is designated and shall be known as the "Colonel Donnell Berry Memorial Highway." (2)  The Mississippi Department of Transportation shall erect and maintain appropriate signs along and approaching the segment of highway described in subsection (1) of this section. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
