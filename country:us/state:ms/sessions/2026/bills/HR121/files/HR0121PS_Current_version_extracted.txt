HR 121 (As Adopted by House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Massengill House Resolution 121 (As Adopted by House) A RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING AND COMMENDING JON GREY MORRISSON OF WEST UNION HIGH SCHOOL FOR HIS OUTSTANDING ATHLETIC ACCOMPLISHMENTS. WHEREAS, Jon Grey Morrisson, a senior at West Union High School in Myrtle, Mississippi, has demonstrated exceptional skill, teamwork and determination, both on the basketball court and on the baseball field, during his high school athletic career; and WHEREAS, in 2025, Mr. Morrisson was recognized for his standout performance during the 2025 baseball season, which included a .303 batting average and a 2.38 ERA with 54 strikeouts, and he was named Mr. Baseball as the 2025 Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class 1A Player of the Year; and WHEREAS, additionally, in 2026, Mr. Morrisson was named Mr. Basketball as the MHSAA Class 1A Basketball Player of the Year, as he averaged a double-double with 15 points and 10 rebounds; and WHEREAS, on Wednesday, December 3, 2025, Mr. Morrisson signed with Itawamba Community College to continue his amazing baseball career, and he was surrounded by his family, both blood and school, as well as his coaches from the respective teams as he put pen to scholarship; and WHEREAS, throughout his baseball and basketball seasons of play with the West Union Eagles, Mr. Morrisson played with clear eyes, a full heart and unimaginable amounts of fortitude, and his mental stamina and athletic prowess never wavered from the moment he first hustled onto both the court and the field, as he outlasted, outplayed and ultimately overpowered his opponents; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend excellence, especially that displayed by Jon Grey Morrisson, who has brought honor to West Union High School and to the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby recognize and commend Jon Grey Morrisson for his outstanding athletic accomplishments, and extend best wishes for continued success in all his future endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Jon Grey Morrisson and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
