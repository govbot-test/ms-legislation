HR 84 (As Adopted by House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Eure House Resolution 84 (As Adopted by House) A RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING AND COMMENDING THE ST. PATRICK CATHOLIC HIGH SCHOOL LADY IRISH SOCCER TEAM FOR WINNING THEIR FOURTH CONSECUTIVE CLASS 1A STATE CHAMPIONSHIP. WHEREAS, the St. Patrick Catholic High School Lady Irish Soccer Team completed a historic season, capturing their fourth consecutive Class 1A State Championship on February 13, 2026; and WHEREAS, after winning their third straight title in 2025, the Lady Irish entered the 20252026 season determined to continue their legacy, and led by the largest senior class in program history, including captains:  Nora Mestayer, Lila Miller, Bella Jordan and Samantha Johnson, this group was driven by hard work, discipline, teamwork, commitment and a steadfast dedication to putting God first; and WHEREAS, despite battling early-season injuries, the team's depth and emerging young talent helped propel them forward, and the Lady Irish finished district play with a perfect 90 record, securing their sixth consecutive District Championship, a testament to their consistency, resilience and competitive spirit; and WHEREAS, the team advanced through the playoffs and defeated Our Lady Academy 20 in the South State Championship to earn a spot in the Class 1A State Championship match, and in the title game, the Lady Irish defeated St. Andrew's Episcopal School 20 to claim their fourth straight state title; and WHEREAS, the Lady Irish concluded the season with an outstanding 1844 overall record and are ranked No. 10 in Mississippi across all classifications; and WHEREAS, the Lady Irish have been superbly coached and inspired by Head Coach Angela McClure and Assistant Coaches Courtney Head, Carissa Harrison and Jim Thriffiley, who have broadened the team's knowledge of the game and refined their skills in execution; and WHEREAS, Bella Jordan was named MVP of the state championship game, while Nora Mestayer earned the Scholar Athlete Award, and 11 seniors anchored this remarkable season with exceptional leadership, character and commitment, and three seniors:  Bella Jordan, Gia Encrapera and Samantha Johnson, will continue their soccer careers at the collegiate level; and WHEREAS, this championship season cements the Lady Irish legacy as one of excellence, perseverance, faith and unmatched dedication to their program; and WHEREAS, in their tireless dedication, the Lady Irish have developed a winning, cooperative spirit and a high degree of sportsmanship, and their activities and attitudes reflect great credit upon the individual players and the entire coaching staff; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and honor the accomplishments of outstanding student-athletes, especially those such as the St. Patrick Catholic High School Lady Irish Soccer Team, whose hard work, dedication and excellence on the field brings honor to St. Patrick Catholic High School, their community and the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the St. Patrick Catholic High School Lady Irish Soccer Team for winning their fourth consecutive Class 1A State Championship and extend best wishes for continued success in all their future endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to the St. Patrick Catholic High School Lady Irish Soccer Team and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
