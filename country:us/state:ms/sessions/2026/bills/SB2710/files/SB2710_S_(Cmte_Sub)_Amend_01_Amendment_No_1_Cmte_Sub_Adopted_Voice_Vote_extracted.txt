Section 1:
SB2710_S_(Cmte_Sub)_Amend_01 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 1 PROPOSED TO Cmte Sub for Senate Bill No. 2710 BY: Senator(s) Fillingane AMEND by striking the language on lines 30 through 48 and inserting in lieu thereof the following: (c)  Any act attempted or committed by a child which is a crime of violence pursuant to Section 97-3-2 while in possession, whether openly or concealed, of any gun, rifle, pistol, or other firearm of any kind will be in the original jurisdiction of the circuit court; (d)  Any act attempted or committed by a child which is a violation of Section 97-37-17 and which would be a felony if committed by an adult will be in the original jurisdiction of the circuit court; and (e) Any act attempted or committed by a child which would be a felony if committed by an adult while in possession, whether openly or concealed, of a stolen firearm will be in the original jurisdiction of the circuit court. AMEND on line 49 by changing "( * * *cg)" to "( * * *cf)". AMEND on line 137 by restoring the old language "five (5)" and deleting "fifteen (15)". FURTHER, AMEND on line 174 by striking the following language: , and shall stand repealed on June 30, 2026 FURTHER, AMEND the title to conform.
