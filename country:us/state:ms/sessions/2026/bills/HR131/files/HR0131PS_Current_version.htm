MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Representative Barnett

House Resolution 131

(As Adopted by House)

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE WAYNE ACADEMY LADY JAGUARS SOFTBALL TEAM AND HEAD COACH DUSTY HILLMAN UPON WINNING THE 2025 MISSISSIPPI ASSOCIATION OF INDEPENDENT SCHOOLS (MAIS) CLASS 3A DIVISION II STATE SOFTBALL CHAMPIONSHIP.

     WHEREAS, the Wayne Academy Lady Jaguars Softball Team completed a historic and inspiring 2025 season by capturing the Mississippi Association of Independent Schools (MAIS) Class 3A Division II State Softball Championship, securing the first softball state title in school history; and

     WHEREAS, the Lady Jaguars claimed the championship in dramatic fashion by defeating Bowling Green School of Louisiana by a score of 6-5 in the decisive Game 3 of a highly competitive best-of-three series, earning a 2-1 series victory on their home field at the Wayne Academy softball complex; and

     WHEREAS, the championship series was fiercely contested, with both teams entering the final game evenly matched and ultimately finishing the series having each scored forty runs against one another, reflecting the intensity and balance of competition between two outstanding programs; and

     WHEREAS, the Lady Jaguars' championship season stands as a testament to perseverance and resilience, as the team overcame early adversity, including a six-game losing streak and a 2-7 start to the season, before rallying to finish with a 19-14-1 record and an unforgettable postseason run; and

     WHEREAS, under the leadership of Head Coach Dusty Hillman, whose championship experience and unwavering belief in his players guided the program, the Lady Jaguars developed into a cohesive and determined unit, embodying hard work, discipline and an unyielding commitment to excellence; and

     WHEREAS, the championship game itself exemplified the team's grit and resolve, as the Lady Jaguars overcame a late 5-2 deficit in the sixth inning by scoring four runs to reclaim the lead, and then held firm defensively in the final inning to secure the victory and the title; and

     WHEREAS, outstanding individual performances contributed to the team's success, including strong offensive efforts at the plate and a complete-game pitching performance that anchored the Lady Jaguars' championship victory, while each player fulfilled her role in a collective display of teamwork and determination; and

     WHEREAS, the Wayne Academy Lady Jaguars represented their school, community and supporters with pride, sportsmanship and excellence, inspiring all who followed their remarkable journey from early-season struggles to championship triumph; and

     WHEREAS, the success of this championship team reflects not only the dedication of its players and coaching staff, but also the unwavering support of parents, school administrators, volunteers, the broader Wayne Academy and community family; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend the achievements of student-athletes whose perseverance, teamwork and excellence bring honor and distinction to their schools and communities:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Wayne Academy Lady Jaguars Softball Team and Head Coach Dusty Hillman upon winning the 2025 MAIS Class 3A Division II State Softball Championship, and extend best wishes for continued success in all their future athletic and academic endeavors.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Head Coach Dusty Hillman on behalf of the Lady Jaguars Softball Team, to the administration of Wayne Academy and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
