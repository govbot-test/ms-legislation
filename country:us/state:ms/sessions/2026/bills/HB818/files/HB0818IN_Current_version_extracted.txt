Section 1:
HB 818 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations A By: Representative Porter House Bill 818 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE GOVERNING AUTHORITIES OF THE CITY OF MCCOMB TO ASSIST IN PAYING COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH THE CONSTRUCTION AND EQUIPPING OF A FIRE STATION IN SOUTH MCCOMB AND PURCHASING A NEW FIRE TRUCK AND EQUIPMENT FOR THE STATION FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the governing authorities of the City of McComb, Mississippi, to assist in paying costs associated with the construction and equipping of a fire station in South McComb and purchasing a new fire truck and equipment for the station, as provided in Section 2, for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027.... $ 5,000,000.00. SECTION 2.  The money appropriated by Section 1 shall be used as follows: (a)  Construction of new fire station.... $ 2,000,000.00 (b)  Equipping the fire station...........$   500,000.00 (c)  Purchase of new fire truck...........$ 2,000,000.00 (d)  Equipping the fire truck.............$   500,000.00 TOTAL............................................ $ 5,000,000.00. SECTION 3.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 4.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
