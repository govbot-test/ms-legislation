Section 1:
SB2179 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations By: Senator(s) Blackmon Senate Bill 2179 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE TOWN OF PICKENS, MISSISSIPPI, FOR THE PURPOSE OF DEFRAYING THE COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH CERTAIN INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1. The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the Town of Pickens, Mississippi, for the purpose of defraying the costs associated with certain infrastructure projects for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027 $ 760,000.00 SECTION 2.  Of the funds appropriated under Section 1, the following amounts are to be allocated as follows: To defray costs of resurfacing and repairing roadways and repairing sewer pipes in the Union Street area................ $ 300,000.00 To defray costs of acquiring new water pumps as well as repairing and improving water systems, including the booster station on State Highway 17......................................... $ 300,000.00 To defray costs of renovating, repairing and improving the old city hall on Yazoo Street................................ $  60,000.00 To defray costs of renovating, repairing, improving and equipping a city park.......................................... $ 100,000.00 SECTION 3.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 4.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
