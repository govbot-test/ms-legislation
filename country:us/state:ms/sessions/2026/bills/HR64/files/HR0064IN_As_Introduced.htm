MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Representatives Summers, Brown, Butler-Washington

House Resolution 64

A RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING AND COMMENDING THE BLACKBURN MIDDLE SCHOOL PANTHERS FOOTBALL TEAM FOR WINNING THE MIDDLE SCHOOL FOOTBALL CHAMPIONSHIP GAME.

     WHEREAS, the Blackburn Middle School Panthers Football Team capped off an outstanding season with an exciting 1814 victory over Nichols Middle School in the Middle School Football Championship Game earlier this season, finishing the year undefeated, earning a perfect 90 record; and

     WHEREAS, offensively, the team averaged 30 points a game, while defense delivered six shutouts in the nine games played, and during each game in their rise to the top, the Panthers mental stamina and determination never waivered from the moment they ran onto the field until the last down was played, and their hard work and motivation were so apparent throughout the entire season; and

     WHEREAS, the Panthers are comprised of the following talented student-athletes:  Jarquis Brown, Jarvis Brown, Jay Brown, Teryji Burns, Brian Collins, Khameron Crockett, King Gardner, Derwin Greenwood, Krishaun Harrison, Brandon Jacob, Armari Johnson, Armon Johnson, Hunter Jones, Elijah Madison, Tequandra McClendon, Cameron McQuitter, Micheal Moore, Marquez Reese, Jakolby Robinson, James Stigger, Darrin Stuckey and Michael Thorton; and

     WHEREAS, the team also has several scholars who serve in student government roles:  Class Representative, Mr. Blackburn, Mr. 8th Grade, and Mr. Homecoming King, and one scholar had the distinguished honor of being one of the top three Algebra 1 students, and numerous scholars received Term 1 Benchmark achievements; and

     WHEREAS, the Panthers have been superbly coached and inspired by Head Coach, Kevin Goss; Assistant Coaches, K.T. Hubbard, Dexter Thomas Nelson, Josh Nelson, Tyrone Powell and Corey Sims; and

Athletic Director, Tanisha Walker; and each coach has broadened the team's knowledge of the game while refining their skills in execution; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and honor the achievements of student-athletes such as the Blackburn Middle School Panthers Football Team, who have brought honor to their school, the City of Jackson, and to the State of Mississippi:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Blackburn Middle School Panthers Football Team and Head Coach Kevin Goss for winning the Middle School Football Championship Game, and extend best wishes for continued success in all their future endeavors.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to the Blackburn Middle School Panthers Football Team, Head Coach Kevin Goss, Principal Eric Jackson, and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
