Section 1:
SB2998 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations By: Senator(s) Simmons (12th) Senate Bill 2998 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE TOWN OF METCALFE TO DEFRAY EXPENSES FOR THE TOWN'S WATER AND SEWER SYSTEM FOR FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is hereby appropriated, out of any money in the Capital Expense Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the Town of Metcalfe to defray expenses for the improvement of the town's water and sewer system for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027  $ 748,750.00. SECTION 2.  The money appropriated in this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the Capital Expense Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 3.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
