MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Representative Blackwell

House Resolution 56

A RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING AND COMMENDING THE SOUTH JONES HIGH SCHOOL "BAND OF BRAVES" MARCHING BAND FOR WINNING THE 2025 BANDS OF AMERICA CLASS A GRAND NATIONAL CHAMPIONSHIP.

     WHEREAS, the South Jones High School "Band of Braves" Marching Band has built a reputation as one of the most successful performing arts groups in the State of Mississippi, a truly outstanding achievement; and

     WHEREAS, South Jones High School in Ellisville, Mississippi, has proudly served the Jones County School District ever since it opened its doors in 1965, and under the current leadership of Superintendent Dr. B.R. Jones, and the band leadership of Brian Joyce, the Band of Braves has grown into one of the region's most respected and accomplished music programs; and

     WHEREAS, from its earliest days, the band has maintained a tradition of excellence, consistently earning superior ratings at festivals and competitions in both marching and concert performance, and that commitment to musical artistry, discipline, and teamwork has taken the Band of Braves across the United States, where they have performed for audiences nationwide while representing their school, community and state with pride; and

     WHEREAS, beyond competitive success, the South Jones Band remains deeply connected to its community, and the program is a familiar and welcomed presence at local parades, community festivals, civic celebrations, and church events, embodying the spirit of service and school pride that defines South Jones High School; and

     WHEREAS, in recent years, the Band of Braves has reached new heights on the state, regional, and national stage, earning numerous prestigious honors, including:  2025 Band of America (BOA) Class A Grand National Champions; 2023 BOA Southeastern Regional Grand Champion - Troy University; BOA Regional Class Champions - Jacksonville State University (2021); East Tennessee University (2022); Mississippi 5A State Marching Champions (2019, 2021-2025); 2019 WGI Winds Scholastic Open International Champions and Mississippi Sweepstakes Award Winners (2017-2019, 2021-2025); and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend outstanding Mississippi students, especially those such as the members of the South Jones High School "Band of Braves" Marching Band, who have brought honor to South Jones High School and to the State of Mississippi:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby recognize and commend the members of the South Jones High School "Band of Braves" Marching Band for winning the 2025 Bands Of America Class A Grand National Championship, and extend best wishes for continued success in all their future endeavors.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Mr. Brian Joyce, Director of Bands, and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
