MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

By: Representative Felsher

House Bill 1728

AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE MISSISSIPPI DEPARTMENT OF FINANCE AND ADMINISTRATION TO SELL CERTAIN REAL PROPERTY AND IMPROVEMENTS LOCATED IN THE CITY OF BILOXI, HARRISON COUNTY, MISSISSIPPI; TO PRESCRIBE THE MANNER OF SALE; TO REQUIRE APPRAISAL OF THE PROPERTY; TO PROVIDE FOR THE DISPOSITION OF THE PROCEEDS; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES.

     BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI:

     SECTION 1.  The Mississippi Department of Finance and Administration is authorized, in its discretion and acting on behalf of the State of Mississippi, to sell and convey all right, title and interest of the State of Mississippi in and to the real property and improvements located in the City of Biloxi, Harrison County, Mississippi, commonly known as 1141 Bayview Avenue, Biloxi, Mississippi, together with any improvements located thereon and any property related thereto.  The Department of Finance and Administration shall obtain a legal description of the property prior to conveyance, and such legal description shall be included in the deed or other instrument of conveyance.

     SECTION 2.  The sale authorized under Section 1 of this act may be made at public or private sale, for cash or upon such other terms and conditions as the Department of Finance and Administration may deem appropriate and in the best interest of the State of Mississippi.  The department is authorized to advertise the sale in such manner as it determines appropriate and may determine the manner and timing of the sale.  The property shall be sold for not less than the fair market value as determined by two (2) professional, certified general appraisers selected by the Department of Finance and Administration as provided in Section 29-1-1.

     SECTION 3.  The Department of Finance and Administration is authorized to execute and deliver, in the name of and on behalf of the State of Mississippi, any deeds, bills of sale, or other instruments necessary to convey the property.

     SECTION 4.  The proceeds derived from the sale of the real property and improvements authorized under this act, after deduction of reasonable costs and expenses incurred in connection with the sale, shall be deposited into the State General Fund.

     SECTION 5.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage.
