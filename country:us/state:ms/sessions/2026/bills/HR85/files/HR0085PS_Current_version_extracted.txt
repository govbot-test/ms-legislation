HR 85 (As Adopted by House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Eure House Resolution 85 (As Adopted by House) A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE ST. PATRICK HIGH SCHOOL BOY'S SOCCER TEAM AND HEAD COACH RYAN WORSHAM ON WINNING THE 2026 MISSISSIPPI HIGH SCHOOL ACTIVITIES ASSOCIATION (MHSAA) CLASS I BOYS SOCCER STATE CHAMPIONSHIP. WHEREAS, on February 20, 2026, in a contest sure to be remembered for years to come, the St. Patrick High School Boy's Soccer Team defeated St. Andrew's Episcopal School in the 2026 Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class I Boys Soccer State Championship played in Ridgeland, Mississippi, with a one-nil win, incredibly bringing the gold ball home to Biloxi for the first time in nearly a decade; and WHEREAS, finishing one of the team's most remarkable seasons in program history, the Fighting Irish managed to do in the final  match what no other team accomplished this postseason - score a goal against St. Andrew's - and the victory in the championship set a phenomenal new school record for wins as the team, undefeated at home, finished the season 23-3; and WHEREAS, the 2025-2026 Fighting Irish, four-time state champions, established themselves as an elite team in Mississippi able to defeat anyone regardless of classification, including the state's 7A powerhouse teams, and the squad's dominance on the pitch resulted in their being ranked #2 in the state and #18 nationally across all classifications by MaxPreps; and WHEREAS, boasting a flawless 10-0 record in region play, the Fighting Irish, Region 8 District Champions, were explosive in attack and disciplined in defense all season, scoring 95 goals while conceding just 14; and WHEREAS, throughout the season, the performance of all members of the Boy's Soccer Team was nothing less than spectacular, and for their impressive play, several team members were honored with top awards, including Tristan Davis, who scored the single goal with his unbelievable 60-yard kick of a lifetime and was named Most Valuable Player of the championship match, and Cameron Messer, who was named the Mississippi Coast Player of the Year by the Mississippi Coast Coaches Poll; and WHEREAS, the team's extraordinary success would not have been possible without each of the following talented 2025-2026 Fighting Irish student-athletes, particularly the strong senior class, many of whom have been teammates for years, including six who will continue their careers at the next level - a testament to the culture, leadership and development within the program:  John Dreger, Grant Wall, Tristan Davis, Caleb McCune, Alex Baty, Ayden Cosper, Peter Barhonovich, Cameron Messer, Payton Taylor, Luke Shaw, Hunter Robinson, Lucas Kurihara, Gage Anderson, Sam McClain, Bishop Smith, Byron Theriot, Niko Barhonovich, Miles Smith, Bentley Puzz, Cobie Martino, Kingston Davis, Brady Gopffarth, Noah Sullivan, Tripp Sala, Luke Warburton, Graham Mattina, Cooper Messer, Sage Viglianco, Sam Bernadas, David Weeks, Joshua Dalon, Owen Vogle and Walter Sulas; and WHEREAS, the St. Patrick High School Boy's Soccer Team's championship run will be long remembered not just for the title, but for the standard set by the team built on humility, mental toughness, and an unwavering commitment to excellence, as evidenced by the squad's unparalleled spirit of teamwork and sportsmanship and the year-round training and hard work of each and every individual team member to be the best athlete he can possibly be; and WHEREAS, the St. Patrick High School Boy's Soccer Team has been superbly coached and inspired by fifth-year Head Coach Ryan Worsham and assistant coaches BJ Williams and Zach Seymour, who have broadened the players' knowledge of the game and refined their skills in execution, and the team has been loyally supported and encouraged with each challenging game, both on their home turf and away, by the school administration and local community; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend the superb accomplishments of Mississippi student-athletes, especially those such as the St. Patrick High School Boy's Soccer Team, whose excellence on the field and in the classroom bring honor to their school, community and the entire State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the St. Patrick High School Boy's Soccer Team and Head Coach Ryan Worsham on winning the 2026 Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class I Boys Soccer State Championship and extend best wishes to the team members and coaches for continued success in all their future endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Head Coach Ryan Worsham on behalf of St. Patrick High School Boy's Soccer Team and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
