MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Senator(s) Tate, Seymour, Simmons (12th)

Senate Resolution 30

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING AUBREY LYNN ATCHISON, A SENIOR AT BILOXI HIGH SCHOOL, AS THE STATE GAMES OF MISSISSIPPI 2025 YOUTH ATHLETE OF THE YEAR.

     WHEREAS, the State Games of Mississippi 2025 Ochsner Rush Health Athletes of the Year were recognized by the Mississippi State Legislature and Governor Tate Reeves during a visit to the Capitol in March; and

     WHEREAS, the 2025 Games featured more than 5,200 athletes competing in more than 40 events across 18 counties, and three athletes personified the ideals of the State Games in participation, sportsmanship, special achievements and positive attitudes; and

     WHEREAS, the three athletes, who each competed in the 2025 games, were unique contributors to their individual sports, and thus were named Ochsner Rush Health Athletes of the Year:  Aubrey Lynn Atchison of Biloxi (Youth Athlete of the Year), Kaytelyn Harris of Quitman (Female Athlete of the Year), and Daniel Wile of Meridian (Male Athlete of the Year); and

     WHEREAS, "It is a great honor to be recognized by our statewide lawmakers and Governor Reeves," said Missye Dozier, Executive Director of the Games.  "State Games holds sporting events throughout the state, creating multiple opportunities for amateur athletes and contributing to the economies of each city in which they are held"; and

     WHEREAS, Aubrey Atchison of Biloxi is a senior at Biloxi High School and has been playing softball for 13 years, including three years competing in the State Games of Mississippi.  In the 2025 State Games, she posted a .636 batting average, scored four runs, and tallied five RBIs.  Her performance capped off a standout season that included First Team All-Region 4-7A honors, selection to the Mississippi Gulf Coast All-Stars Futures Game, and was recognized as Biloxi High School's Offensive Player of the Year; and

     WHEREAS, Aubrey's path to success hasn't been easy.  After breaking her ankle in 2022, she faced a long road to recovery, eventually returning to the varsity team while still in 8th Grade.  Two more injuries followed, but Aubrey never gave up  showing up to train, support her teammates and lead through adversity.  She says the challenges taught her patience, resilience and how to lead with grace.  "Softball is so much more than just a sport to me.  It's where I found my passion, my people and my purpose," Aubrey said; and

     WHEREAS, it is with great pride that we recognize this student-athlete who has brought honor to her school, her community and to the State of Mississippi:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate Aubrey Lynn Atchison, a senior at Biloxi High School, as the State Games of Mississippi 2025 Youth Athlete of the Year, and extend to Aubrey, her parents and coaches our best wishes for future success.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Aubrey Lynn Atchison and her family, forwarded to the State Games of Mississippi, and made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
