MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Senator(s) Tate, Frazier, Seymour

Senate Resolution 35

(As Adopted by Senate)

A RESOLUTION TO EXTEND THE RECOGNITION AND THANKS OF THE MISSISSIPPI SENATE TO "VETERANS OUTREACH OF MISSISSIPPI" FOR ITS OUTSTANDING SERVICE AND DEDICATION TO THE VETERANS OF MISSISSIPPI.

     WHEREAS, Veterans OutReach of Mississippi has faithfully served Mississippi's veterans since 2009, helping America's heroes feel respected, supported, and at home; and

     WHEREAS, the mission of Veterans OutReach of Mississippi is to provide spiritual guidance, counseling, and essential goods and services not otherwise supplied by the State of Mississippi or other government agencies to veterans residing in the Mississippi State Veterans Homes; and

     WHEREAS, the Mississippi State Veterans Homes are state-owned and operated facilities providing care to veterans, many of whom arrive medically indigent, elderly, homeless, or without family support, and in need of basic necessities and companionship; and

     WHEREAS, Veterans OutReach works in close coordination with nursing home staff to identify unmet needs and provide assistance in a manner that preserves the dignity of each veteran and avoids preferential treatment; and

     WHEREAS, dedicated volunteers of Veterans OutReach make regular personal visits to residents, fostering bonds of friendship and expressing appreciation, respect, and compassion for those who have served our nation; and

     WHEREAS, Veterans OutReach of Mississippi serves veterans residing at the Collins State Veterans Home in Collins, Mississippi, and the Kosciusko State Veterans Home in Kosciusko, Mississippi; and

     WHEREAS, Veterans OutReach conducts numerous ongoing projects to enhance the quality of life for resident veterans, including providing clothing, personal items, furniture, lifts and chairs, hosting bingo games, cookouts, meals, celebrations, and conducting an Adopt-A-Veteran Christmas Gift Drive; and

     WHEREAS, through its unwavering commitment to service, Veterans OutReach has demonstrated a profound dedication to honoring and caring for Mississippi's most vulnerable and often forgotten veterans, and it is with great pride that we recognize the dedication, expertise, and commitment of this veterans service support group which has made a lasting impact on our state:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby extend the recognition and thanks of the Mississippi Senate to "Veterans OutReach of Mississippi" for its outstanding service and dedication to the veterans of Mississippi.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That the Mississippi State Senate expresses its sincere gratitude to the volunteers, supporters, and leadership of Veterans OutReach for their continued commitment to honoring, supporting, and caring for Mississippi's veterans.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That the Mississippi Veterans Affairs (MSVA) has extended its gratitude, support, and its cooperation with Veterans OutReach of Mississippi.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Veterans OutReach as a symbol of the high esteem in which it is held by the Mississippi State Senate and the citizens of this great state.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to the Veterans OutReach of Mississippi and to the Mississippi VA Executive Director Mark Smith and made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
