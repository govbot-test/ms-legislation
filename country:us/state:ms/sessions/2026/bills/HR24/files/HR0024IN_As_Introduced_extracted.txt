Section 1:
HR 24 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Porter House Resolution 24 A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE SOUTH PIKE HIGH SCHOOL BOYS AND GIRLS POWERLIFTING TEAMS FOR WINNING THE MISSISSIPPI HIGH SCHOOL ACTIVITIES ASSOCIATION (MHSAA) CLASS 2A STATE CHAMPIONSHIP. WHEREAS, the South Pike High School Boys and Girls Powerlifting Teams reigned supreme at the Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class 2A State Championship, an incredible accomplishment reflecting years of dedication, discipline, teamwork and extraordinary coaching; and WHEREAS, fans crowded the Mississippi Coliseum to watch the South Pike High School Boys and Girls Powerlifting Teams showcase their exemplary skill at the state championship after their impressive South State victories, and the teams did not disappoint, as they captured their eleventh and twelfth championship titles with their exceptional performances; and WHEREAS, the South Pike teams have ascended to the pinnacle of powerlifting excellence this season thanks to the hard work and unmatched spirit of the dedicated student-athletes, whose hours of practice spent deadlifting, squatting and bench pressing paid off in spades as they faced the state's top athletes and emerged triumphant; and WHEREAS, in 2002, Tyrone Varnado became the South Pike High School Powerlifting Coach, unaware that his leadership would establish a lasting dynasty as he built the program into a powerful force to be reckoned with, and in the 24 years he has spent coaching these outstanding teams, Coach Varnado has broadened the athletes' knowledge of the sport and refined their skills in execution, leading his teams to victory time and time again; and WHEREAS, the members of these distinguished teams will surely treasure the memories they have made throughout their remarkable season, constantly flooded with the feeling of greatness that accompanies being a champion as they look with pride upon their many banners hanging in the Willie E. Jones Coliseum; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend outstanding Mississippi student-athletes, especially those such as the South Pike High School Boys and Girls Powerlifting Teams, and it is with great pride that we congratulate this elite group of student-athletes, who have brought honor and pride to their school, their community and the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the South Pike High School Boys and Girls Powerlifting Teams upon winning the Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class 2A State Championship, and extend best wishes to the teams for continued success in all their future endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to the South Pike High School Boys and Girls Powerlifting Teams and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
