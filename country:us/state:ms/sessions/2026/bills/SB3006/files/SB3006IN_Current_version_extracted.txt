Section 1:
SB3006 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations By: Senator(s) Simmons (12th) Senate Bill 3006 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO BOLIVAR COUNTY TO DEFRAY EXPENSES ASSOCIATED WITH REPAIRS, RESURFACING AND IMPROVEMENTS TO STREETS LOCATED IN SUPERVISOR DISTRICT 1 FOR FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is hereby appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to Bolivar County to defray expenses associated with repairs, resurfacing and improvements to streets located in Supervisor District 1 for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027............................................ $ 1,000,000.00. SECTION 2.  The money appropriated in this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 3.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
