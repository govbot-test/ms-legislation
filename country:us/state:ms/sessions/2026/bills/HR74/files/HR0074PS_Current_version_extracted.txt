HR 74 (As Adopted by House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representatives Elliott, Calvert House Resolution 74 (As Adopted by House) A RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING AND COMMENDING MR. RICKY HOOD, CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER FOR THE BOYS AND GIRLS CLUBS OF EAST MISSISSIPPI, FOR HIS CONTINUOUS HARD WORK AND DEDICATED SERVICE. WHEREAS, for over 35 years, Mr. Ricky Hood has served his community by dedicating his time and hard work to the Boys and Girls Club; and WHEREAS, Mr. Hood is currently the Chief Executive Officer for the Boys and Girls Clubs of East Mississippi, which includes four sites in Lauderdale County, Neshoba County, and Attala County, and with the help of a dedicated staff of 52, many of whom are former club members, the organization served over 900 children daily at the six sites prior to the COVID-19 pandemic; and WHEREAS, throughout the pandemic, the Club provided over 2,500 meals daily, and it also provided virtual learning for several of the communities in East Mississippi, and currently, the Boys and Girls Clubs provide services for over 600 children daily; and WHEREAS, born in Montgomery, Alabama, as one of 16 brothers and sisters, Mr. Hood developed a love for the Club at an early age, and he was a member of the West End Boys and Girls Club in Montgomery, where Mike Anderson served as a tremendous mentor for him; and WHEREAS, Mr. Hood graduated from Robert E. Lee High School in 1978, obtained a basketball scholarship to Mississippi State University, and later transferred and graduated from Murray State University with a degree in business administration; and WHEREAS, upon graduation, Mr. Hood played professional basketball on the international circuit for eight years in Switzerland, Spain, Italy and Portugal, and in 1988, he started working summers at the Boys and Girls Club in Meridian, Mississippi, and the reason he has worked in the Boys and Girls Club for over 35 years is because of his earlier experiences and his deep desire to mentor and help children; and WHEREAS, since serving as the Chief Executive Officer for the Boys and Girls Clubs of East Mississippi, Mr. Hood has garnered numerous awards on the local, state, regional and national level, including the Southeast Regional Herman S. Prescott Award (mentoring), the Errol Sewell Award (leadership), and most recently, the Lou Dantzler Award, and all of this recognition was based on leadership, integrity, respect for others and commitment to excellence; and WHEREAS, Mr. Hood has also received other awards, including the Mississippi Area Council Professional of the Year, the BGCA regional BLUE STAR AWARD, the NAACP Man of the Year Award, and the Meridian Public Schools Parent of the Year Award; and WHEREAS, outside of his outstanding service with the Boys and Girls Club, Mr. Hood is married to Vicky Hood, a retired educator, and has four children, eight grandchildren and four great-grandchildren; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize exceptional individuals, especially those such as Mr. Hood, whose extraordinary service brings honor to the Boys and Girls Club and to the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby recognize and commend Mr. Ricky Hood, Chief Executive Officer for the Boys and Girls Clubs of East Mississippi, for his continuous hard work and dedicated service, and extend best wishes for continued success in all his future endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Mr. Ricky Hood and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
