House Resolution 59

(As Adopted by House)

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE SIMPSON ACADEMY LADY COUGARS SOFTBALL TEAM AND HEAD COACH MATT HARRISON UPON WINNING THE MAIS CLASS 4A DIVISION I STATE SOFTBALL CHAMPIONSHIP.

     WHEREAS, on October 12, 2025, the Simpson Academy Lady Cougars Softball Team secured the MAIS Class 4A Division I State Softball Championship by defeating Parklane Academy 6-4 in the decisive third game of a best-of-three championship series held at the Magee Sportsplex; and

     WHEREAS, the Lady Cougars earned their place in the championship series through a dominant playoff performance, including an 8-1 victory over Bayou Academy and a commanding 12-0 shutout of Parklane Academy, demonstrating excellence in pitching, defense and offensive production; and

     WHEREAS, throughout the postseason, the team displayed determination, unity and resilience, playing their highest caliber of softball when it mattered most and securing home-field advantage for the opening game of the championship series; and

     WHEREAS, the 2025 Simpson Academy Lady Cougars Softball Team roster includes:  Danni Cooper, Tinsley Aldridge, Gracie Pinkerton, Lucy Walker, Jadyn Sanford, Kylin Chisholm, Miley Massey, Hudson Emrich, Edyn Guidroz, Mollie Murphy, Aniston Bass, Paxtyn Collins, Abra Presley, Mallory Duncan, Brecken Collins, Maggie Duncan, Madisyn Hankins, Caroline Lewis, Ava Kate Maddox, Sydnie Bass, Jayda Smith, Stella Steed and Annmarie Blakeney; and

     WHEREAS, the team was led by Head Coach Matt Harrison and Assistant Coaches Ben Coney and Michael Harrison, whose leadership, preparation and emphasis on discipline and teamwork were instrumental in guiding the Lady Cougars to a championship finish; and

     WHEREAS, the Simpson Academy Lady Cougars represented their school and community with honor, sportsmanship and pride, inspiring classmates, families and Cougar supporters through their perseverance and competitive excellence; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend the outstanding achievements of student-athletes who, through dedication and teamwork, bring distinction to their schools and communities:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Simpson Academy Lady Cougars Softball Team and Head Coach Matt Harrison upon winning the MAIS Class 4A Division I State Softball Championship, and extend best wishes for continued success in future seasons.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Head Coach Matt Harrison, the Simpson Academy administration and Board of Trustees, and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
