SR 29 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Senator(s) Tate, Simmons (12th) Senate Resolution 29 A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING DANIEL WILE OF MERIDIAN, MISSISSIPPI, AS THE STATE GAMES OF MISSISSIPPI 2025 MALE ATHLETE OF THE YEAR. WHEREAS, the State Games of Mississippi 2025 Ochsner Rush Health Athletes of the Year were recognized by the Mississippi State Legislature and Governor Tate Reeves during a visit to the Capitol in March; and WHEREAS, the 2025 Games featured more than 5,200 athletes competing in more than 40 events across 18 counties, and three athletes personified the ideals of the State Games in participation, sportsmanship, special achievements and positive attitudes; and WHEREAS, the three athletes, who each competed in the 2025 games, were unique contributors to their individual sports, and thus were named Ochsner Rush Health Athletes of the Year:  Daniel Wile of Meridian (Male Athlete of the Year), Aubrey Lynn Atchison of Biloxi (Youth Athlete of the Year), and Kaytelyn Harris of Quitman (Female Athlete of the Year); and WHEREAS, "It is a great honor to be recognized by our statewide lawmakers and Governor Reeves," said Missye Dozier, Executive Director of the Games.  "State Games holds sporting events throughout the state, creating multiple opportunities for amateur athletes and contributing to the economies of each city in which they are held"; and WHEREAS, Daniel Wile of Meridian, Mississippi, has been involved in endurance sports for more than a decade.  He has participated in the State Games of Mississippi twice, earning one Gold and one Silver Medal in cycling, as well as two Gold and two Silver Medals in Masters Swimming; and WHEREAS, a passionate cyclist, Daniel posted a remarkable time of 00:55:14.76 in this year's cycling event  just milliseconds behind the gold medalist.  His love of the sport began through triathlon training, and over the years he has completed three Ironman races, including the prestigious 2022 Ironman World Championship in Kona, Hawaii.  He was also named the 2021 Heart O' Dixie Triathlon Champion; and WHEREAS, Daniel said he enjoys the opportunity to focus solely on cycling during the State Games: "When you don't have to save your energy for the run, you really get to empty the tank and see what you can do."  Off the course, he balances training with a busy life and credits his wife and family for their continued support and encouragement; and WHEREAS, it is with great pride that we recognize these athletes who have brought honor to their city, their community and to the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate Daniel Wile of Meridian, Mississippi, as the State Games of Mississippi 2025 Male Athlete of the Year, and extend to Daniel and his family our best wishes for future success. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Daniel Wile and his family, forwarded to the State Games of Mississippi, and made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
