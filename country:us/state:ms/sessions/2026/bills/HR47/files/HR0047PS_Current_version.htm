MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Representatives Lott, Ladner

House Resolution 47

(As Adopted by House)

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING STONE COUNTY ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT PARTNERSHIP AND ELECTED CITY AND COUNTY LEADERS AND BUSINESS LEADERS OF STONE COUNTY UPON THE COUNTY'S ECONOMIC SUCCESS.

     WHEREAS, the Stone County Economic Development Partnership (SCEDP) as well as the county's elected city and county leaders and business leaders are being honored upon the county becoming "a hidden gem that awaits all" as it is a beacon of innovation, opportunity and community pride that has catapulted the county into economic success; and

     WHEREAS, with the assistance of the Mississippi Development Authority and other state resources, Stone County has attracted a world-class innovator in the aerospace and defense section with rocket motors manufacturer, Anduril; and

     WHEREAS, notably, in the upcoming months, with the help of Gulf Coast Restoration Funds, Stone County is committed to developing an industrial site that will bring even more innovators to the area while supporting small businesses and entrepreneurs, who form the backbone of the county's local economy; and

     WHEREAS, as evidence of the success of Stone County, the county was selected and honored as the 2025 Destination Marketing Organization (DMO) of the Year for its "Explore Stone County. . . Hidden Gems Await" tourism initiative at the Governor's Conference of Tourism in Vicksburg; and

     WHEREAS, as the 2025 DMO of the Year recipient, Stone County received this prestigious award due to its outstanding efforts in highlighting outdoor recreation offerings, magnificent places to eat and shop and events that truly make the county great; and

     WHEREAS, SCEDP has also invested thousands of dollars in the design, implementation and construction of welcome signs at the gateways into Stone County, and now Wiggins, over the past year as these signs speak to the county's vision as it is a place where businesses succeed, families thrive and innovation meets tradition; and

     WHEREAS, also, Stone County, in partnership with Mississippi Power, has offered nearly $100,000 in facade grant investments to downtown business owners, to help attract more people to eat, play and shop in downtown, and through alliances with colleges such as Mississippi Gulf Coast Community College, the county is teaming with Accelerate Mississippi to invest in initiatives that will enhance the local workforce; and

     WHEREAS, Stone County has been designated by the Legislature as the Mural County of Mississippi due to its artistic mosaics and art pieces countywide and through events like Pine Hill Festival that welcomes thousands to the county the last weekend of April every year - all of which truly exude the county's creative economy; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and honor outstanding economic success that has been exhibited by Stone County as it is a place where industry and innovation flourish, tourism is valued, partnerships are yielding results and creative economy is key:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate Stone County upon its economic success and wish the county and its leadership continued success.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
