HR 20 (As Adopted by House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Porter House Resolution 20 (As Adopted by House) A RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING AND COMMENDING ALANA JOHNSON FOR HER OUTSTANDING SOFTBALL ACCOMPLISHMENTS. WHEREAS, Alana Johnson, of Summit, Mississippi, has achieved numerous extraordinary milestones throughout her softball career; and WHEREAS, as a Texas Tech University Red Raider, Ms. Johnson's achievements include:  2026 SAAC Campus Relations Representative; 2025 All-Big 12 First Team 2025; 2025 All-Big 12 Tournament Team; CSC Academic All-District 2025; SAAC Representative for softball 2025; NFCA All-Pacific Region Third Team 2024; All-Pac-12 Second Team 2024; CSC Academic All-District 2024; Pac-12 Freshman of the Week (March 27); and NFCA All-America Scholar-Athlete 2023; and WHEREAS, in her tireless dedication to the sport, Ms. Johnson has developed a winning, cooperative spirit and a high degree of sportsmanship, and her actions, both on the field and off, reflects great credit upon her, and the key to her consistent success has been her year-round hard work and the true love she possesses for the game; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend the exceptional accomplishments of student-athletes who represent the spirit of excellence, especially those such as Alana Johnson, whose achievements bring honor to Texas Tech University, her community and to the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby recognize and commend Alana Johnson for her outstanding softball accomplishments, and extend best wishes for continued success in all her future endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Alana Johnson, and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
