MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Medicaid

By: Senator(s) Bryan

Senate Bill 2923

AN ACT TO PROHIBIT THE DIVISION OF MEDICAID FROM COLLECTING OR TAKING ANY STEPS TO COLLECT MONIES OWED DUE TO OVERPAYMENTS RECEIVED DURING THE 2024 FISCAL YEAR FROM A HOSPITAL IN GREENWOOD UNTIL AFTER MAY 1, 2026, IN ORDER TO ENSURE THAT ACCESS TO CRITICAL HEALTH CARE SERVICES WILL NOT BE ERODED OR ELIMINATED FOR CENTRAL AND EASTERN REGIONS OF THE MISSISSIPPI DELTA; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES.

     BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI:

     SECTION 1.  In order to ensure that access to critical health care services will not be eroded or eliminated for residents and nonresidents within the central and eastern regions of the Mississippi Delta, the Division of Medicaid shall not collect or take any steps to collect monies owed due to overpayments received during the 2024 fiscal year from a hospital in Greenwood until after May 1, 2026.

     SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage.
