2026 Regular Session

House Concurrent Resolution 21

A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR A JOINT SESSION OF THE MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE TO HEAR THE STATE OF THE STATE ADDRESS OF GOVERNOR TATE REEVES.

     BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, THE SENATE CONCURRING THEREIN, That a joint session of the House of Representatives and the Senate shall be held in the Second Floor Rotunda of the New Capitol Building, on Tuesday, January 27, 2026, at 5:00 p.m., to hear the State of the State address of Governor Tate Reeves.
