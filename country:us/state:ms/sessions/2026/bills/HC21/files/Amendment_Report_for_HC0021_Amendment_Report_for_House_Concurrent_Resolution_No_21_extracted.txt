Amendment_Report_for_HC0021 Senate Amendments to House Concurrent Resolution No. 21 TO THE CLERK OF THE HOUSE: THIS IS TO INFORM YOU THAT THE SENATE HAS ADOPTED THE AMENDMENTS SET OUT BELOW: AMENDMENT NO. 1 Amend by striking all after line 3 and inserting in lieu thereof the following: BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, THE SENATE CONCURRING THEREIN, That a joint session of the House of Representatives and the Senate shall be held in the Second Floor Rotunda of the New Capitol Building, on Wednesday, February 4, 2026, at 5:00 p.m., to hear the State of the State address of Governor Tate Reeves. Further, amend by striking the title in its entirety and inserting in lieu thereof the following: A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION PROVIDING FOR A JOINT SESSION OF THE MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE TO HEAR THE STATE OF THE STATE ADDRESS OF GOVERNOR TATE REEVES. SS36\HC21A.J Amanda White Secretary of the Senate
