HR 93 (As Adopted by House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Zuber House Resolution 93 (As Adopted by House) A RESOLUTION DESIGNATING APRIL 2026 AS PARLIAMENTARY LAW MONTH IN THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI. WHEREAS, it is timely to reflect on the importance of parliamentary procedure in meetings in providing for civil discourse, ensuring fairness, and maintaining order; and WHEREAS, parliamentary law is an essential component of  ensuring that meetings, discussions, and decision-making processes are conducted in a fair, orderly, and efficient manner, and Parliamentary Law Month recognizes the importance of these structured procedures in organizations ranging from legislative bodies to student groups and faith-based institutions; and WHEREAS, parliamentary procedures provide a structured system for conducting meetings and play a vital role in fostering leadership, decision-making and orderly meetings, and these rules help facilitate discussions, make collective decisions and ensure that all voices are heard while maintaining efficiency; and WHEREAS, Parliamentary Law Month serves as a reminder of the significance of structured governance and decision-making, and celebrating this month presents an opportunity to educate members on proper parliamentary procedures, promote active participation and engagement in organizational meetings, and enhance the overall effectiveness of organizations; and WHEREAS, parliamentary procedures are not just about rules, they are about empowerment, inclusion and effectiveness, and during Parliamentary Law Month, it is important to recognize the value of these procedures and continue fostering environments where order, respect and progress prevail: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby designate April 2026 as Parliamentary Law Month. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
