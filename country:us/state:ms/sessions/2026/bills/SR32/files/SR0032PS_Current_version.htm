Senate Resolution 32

(As Adopted by Senate)

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE SIMPSON ACADEMY LADY COUGARS SOFTBALL TEAM AND COACH MATT HARRISON FOR WINNING THE MAIS 4A DIVISION I STATE SOFTBALL CHAMPIONSHIP.

     WHEREAS, on October 12, 2025, the Simpson Academy Lady Cougars defeated Parklane Academy 6-4 in the deciding Game 3 of the best-of-three series for the MAIS Class 4A Division 1 State Softball Championship at the Magee Sportsplex; and

     WHEREAS, after an incredible run through the playoffs, the Lady Cougars earned their shot at the title with two dominant victories:  An 8-1 win over Bayou Academy and a commanding 12-0 shutout over Parklane Academy.  Their determination and teamwork secured them home-field advantage for Game 1 of the championship series; and

     WHEREAS, the road to the finals was paved with grit, heart, and relentless effort.  The Lady Cougars showed what happens when talent meets tenacity, playing their best softball when it matters most; and

     WHEREAS, the 2025 Simpson Academy Lady Cougars Softball Team Roster:  Danni Cooper, Tinsley Aldridge, Gracie Pinkerton, Lucy Walker, Jadyn Sanford, Kylin Chisholm, Miley Massey, Hudson Emrich, Edyn Guidroz, Mollie Murphy, Aniston Bass, Paxtyn Collins, Abra Presley, Mallory Duncan, Brecken Collins, Maggie Duncan, Madisyn Hankins, Caroline Lewis, Ava Kate Maddox, Sydnie Bass, Jayda Smith, Stella Steed and Annmarie Blakeney; Head Coach Matt Harrison; and Assistant Coaches Ben Coney and Michael Harrison; and

     WHEREAS, their focus and fire on the field inspired Cougar fans and demonstrated why this team is among the best in the MAIS.  Coaches and staff emphasized playing with confidence and unity, and the players responded in dominant fashion.  This remarkable group of young women battled their way to the top, representing Simpson Academy with pride, sportsmanship and Cougar spirit:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Simpson Academy Lady Cougars Softball Team and Coach Matt Harrison for winning the MAIS 4A Division I State Softball Championship, and extend our best wishes to the team, coaches, parents and sponsors for future success.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Coach Matt Harrison for the team, forwarded to Simpson Academy Headmaster John Shows and the Simpson Academy Board of Trustees, and made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
