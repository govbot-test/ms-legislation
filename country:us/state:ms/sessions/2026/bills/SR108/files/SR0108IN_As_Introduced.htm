MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

By: Senator(s) Barrett

Senate Resolution 108

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE BROOKHAVEN ACADEMY LADY COUGARS GIRLS FAST-PITCH SOFTBALL TEAM AND COACH LISA COVINGTON FOR SWEEPING RIVAL SILLIMAN FOR THE 2026 MAIS 4A-D3 GIRLS FAST-PITCH SOFTBALL TITLE.

     WHEREAS, the Brookhaven Academy Lady Cougars are the MAIS 4A-D3 State Champions.  The Lady Cougars finished with a dominate 7-0 win over Silliman in Game 2, bringing home the title once again; and

     WHEREAS, Brookhaven Academy junior pitcher Madelynn Lambert was the star in Brookhaven Academy's title sweep of Silliman, going 5-for-7 at the plate over the course of two nights with a pair of RBIs and two runs.  After the final game 10-0 mercy-rule, Lambert took over in the circle for the game final 7-0 series-ender, facing 23 batters and throwing 52 strikes on 93 pitches; and

     WHEREAS, all the Lady Cougars were locked in, all nine players in the lineup recorded a hit, and the final stat line was seven runs on 15 hits with one error.  Behind Madelynn Lambert, Caroline Case, Peyton Davis, Swayze Poole and Ryleigh Purdy shared a 2-for-4 stat line.  Alayna Daigle, Madelynn Lambert, Swayze Poole, Mirriam Wallace and Whitney Grace Whittington drove in runs.  Six seniors played their last softball game in Cougar Blue:  Morgan Chemin, Alayna Daigle, Annabel Gaskin, Ryleigh Purdy, Mirriam Wallace and Whitney Grace Whittington.  "I'm happy we achieved this, but I am a little sad I have to leave my new friends, my new family.  The whole team is just a family, and I really enjoyed it," said Ryleigh Purdy; and

     WHEREAS, the Brookhaven Academy Lady Cougars Fast-Pitch Softball Team Roster:  Ryleigh Purdy, Miriam Wallace, Madelynn Lambert, Charlee Haire, Breece Tullos, Anniston Brown, Ainsleigh Evans, Morgan Chemin, Alayna Daigle, Annabel Gaskin, Whitney Grace Whittington, Caroline Case, Peyton Davis, Swayze Poole, Matti Kate Gaudet, Kyndal Spencer, Joleigh Langham, Ella Edwards, Kailey Beth Watts, Finley Ybarra, Kinsley Fuller, Jessi Covington, Mattie Leggett, Tinsley Rogers, Laci Brooke Leggett, Maggie Ray, Atlee Ruth Goodson, Autumn Brown, Presley Ballard and Abby Wilson; and Coach Lisa Covington; and

     WHEREAS, all the things that happen when a team wins a state title happened on the field at the championship game.  The Lady Cougars fired off hand-held confetti cannons.  There were high-fives, cheers, proud families.  The Brookhaven Academy mom squad lined up across the circle in an orderly fashion and directed a half-hour of team photography.  Banners unfurled, and a trophy was distributed; and

     WHEREAS, it is with great pride that we recognize these student-athletes who have brought honor to their school, their community and to the State of Mississippi:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Brookhaven Academy Lady Cougars Girls Fast-Pitch Softball Team and Coach Lisa Covington for sweeping rival Silliman for the 2026 MAIS 4A-D3 Girls Fast-Pitch Softball Title, and extend to the team, coaches, faculty, parents and sponsors our best wishes for future championships.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Head Coach Lisa Covington for the team, forwarded to Brookhaven Academy Head of School Jason Case and the Board of Trustees, and made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
