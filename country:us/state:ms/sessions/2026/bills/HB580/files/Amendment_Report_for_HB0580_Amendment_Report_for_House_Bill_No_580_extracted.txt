Section 1:
Amendment_Report_for_HB0580 Senate Amendments to House Bill No. 580 TO THE CLERK OF THE HOUSE: THIS IS TO INFORM YOU THAT THE SENATE HAS ADOPTED THE AMENDMENTS SET OUT BELOW: AMENDMENT NO. 1 AMEND by deleting SECTION 5 in its entirety and inserting the following as a new SECTION 5 in lieu thereof: SECTION 5. (1)  Notwithstanding any other provision of general law, any candidate for the offices of circuit judge for the Fourth Circuit Court District whose term of office begins January 1, 2027, shall file his or her intent to be a candidate no later than 5:00 p.m. on the fifth day after the effective date of this act and qualify as otherwise provided in Section 23-15-977 for candidates for judicial office.  If the fifth day after the effective date of this act falls on a weekend or a holiday, the candidate shall file his or her intent to be a candidate no later than 5:00 p.m. on the next business day following the fifth day after the effective date of this act. (2) Notwithstanding any other provision of general law, any candidate for the offices of circuit judge for the Seventh Circuit Court District whose term of office begins January 1, 2027, shall file his or her intent to be a candidate no later than 5:00 p.m. on fifth day after the effective date of this act and qualify as otherwise provided in Section 23-15-977 for candidates for judicial office.  If the fifth day after the effective date of this act falls on a weekend or a holiday, the candidate shall file his or her intent to be a candidate no later than 5:00 p.m. on the next business day following the fifth day after the effective date of this act. SS26\HB580A.1J Amanda White Secretary of the Senate
