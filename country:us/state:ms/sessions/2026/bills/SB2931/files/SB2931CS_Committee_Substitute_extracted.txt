Section 1:
SB2931 (COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Local and Private By: Senator(s) Butler, Brumfield Senate Bill 2931 (COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE) AN ACT TO AMEND CHAPTER 901, LOCAL AND PRIVATE LAWS OF 2022, BY EXTENDING THE DATE OF REPEAL FROM JULY 1, 2026, TO JULY 1, 2030, ON THE PROVISION OF LAW THAT AUTHORIZES THE GOVERNING AUTHORITIES OF THE CITY OF NATCHEZ, MISSISSIPPI, AND THE BOARD OF SUPERVISORS OF ADAMS COUNTY, MISSISSIPPI, TO CONTRIBUTE FUNDS TO NATCHEZ, INC., A MISSISSIPPI NONPROFIT CORPORATION ORGANIZED FOR ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT PURPOSES; TO CLARIFY CONTRIBUTION LIMITS; TO REVISE THE CONTRIBUTION AMOUNT THAT THE GOVERNING AUTHORITIES OF THE CITY OF NATCHEZ MAY MAKE TO NATCHEZ, INC.; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  Chapter 901, Local and Private Laws of 2022, is amended as follows: Section 1.  The governing authorities of the City of Natchez, Mississippi, are authorized and empowered, in their discretion, to contribute an amount not * * * less more than * * * One Hundred Thousand Dollars ($100,000.00) One Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars ($150,000.00) each fiscal year to Natchez, Inc., a Mississippi nonprofit corporation organized for economic development purposes. Section 2.  The Board of Supervisors of Adams County, Mississippi, is authorized and empowered, in its discretion, to contribute an amount not * * * less more than One Hundred Sixty-five Thousand Dollars ($165,000.00) each fiscal year to Natchez, Inc., a Mississippi nonprofit corporation organized for economic development purposes. Section 3.  This act shall be repealed from and after July 1, * * * 2026 2030. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage.
