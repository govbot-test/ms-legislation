Section 1:
SB2766 (As Passed the Senate) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Senator(s) Tate Senate Bill 2766 (As Passed the Senate) AN ACT TO PROVIDE THAT THE FRIDAY BEFORE MEMORIAL DAY IS TO BE DESIGNATED ANNUALLY AS "BUDDY POPPY DAY" IN MISSISSIPPI IN HONOR OF THE OFFICIAL MEMORIAL FLOWER OF THE VETERANS OF FOREIGN WARS (VFW); AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The Friday before Memorial Day of each year is designated as "Buddy Poppy Day" in Mississippi to recognize the official memorial flower of the Veterans of Foreign Wars (VFW) which honors fallen service members. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage.
