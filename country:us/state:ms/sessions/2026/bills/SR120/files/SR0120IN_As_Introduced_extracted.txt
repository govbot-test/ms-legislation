SR 120 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Senator(s) Whaley Senate Resolution 120 A RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING THE MAGNOLIA HEIGHTS SCHOOL CHIEFS BASEBALL TEAM AND HEAD COACH CHRIS MCMINN FOR THEIR SEVENTH CONSECUTIVE STATE CHAMPIONSHIP AND NO. 1 RANKING IN 2026. WHEREAS, Magnolia Heights School Chiefs Baseball Team in Senatobia, Mississippi, has established a premier baseball dynasty in the Mississippi Association of Independent Schools (MAIS), winning seven consecutive MAIS Class 5A Championships as of May 2025. The Chiefs, consistently ranked as a top Mississippi team under Coach Chris McMinn, have continued their winning streak in 2026; and WHEREAS, as of March 2026, the Chiefs are ranked No. 1 in The Clarion-Ledger Mississippi High School Baseball Super 25 rankings.  Key wins included a 15-12 victory over ranked Jackson Academy and a 9-3 win against Lockport Porters; and WHEREAS, key players for 2026 include Cole Prosek, 2025 MAIS 5A Player of the Year, and Christian Doty; and WHEREAS, the team, led by Head Coach Chris McMinn, has maintained a high level of success for over a decade; and WHEREAS, the Magnolia Heights School Chiefs Baseball Team Roster:  Jett Pratt, Cayden Prestage, Cole Prosek, Christian Hare, Bradley Carroll, Christian Doty, Leland Weldon, Colton Robbins, Quincy Pratt, Michael Fudge, Tre Miller, Kaleb Dunn, Forest Brawner, Jack Price, Landry Garner, Ford Lamar, Hunter Stevens, Ray Glasgow, Carter Drumheller, Brannan Overstreet, Griff Hill, Dane Treadway, Brody Childers, Brantley Rogers and Ty Lucius; Head Coach Chris McMinn; and Coaches Russ Pratt, Jacob Melendez and Hunter Lacefield; and WHEREAS, it is with great pride that we recognize this team of student-athletes whose baseball dynasty has brought honor to its school, its community and to the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby recognize the Magnolia Heights High School Chiefs Baseball Team and Head Coach Chris McMinn for their seventh consecutive State Championship and No. 1 Ranking in 2026, and extend to the team, players, coaches, parents and sponsors our best wishes for future championships. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Head Coach Chris McMinn and Magnolia Heights School Head of School Cliff Johnston for the team, forwarded to the Magnolia Heights Board of Trustees, and made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
