Amendment_Report_for_HB1153 Senate Amendments to House Bill No. 1153 TO THE CLERK OF THE HOUSE: THIS IS TO INFORM YOU THAT THE SENATE HAS ADOPTED THE AMENDMENTS SET OUT BELOW: AMENDMENT NO. 1 AMEND on line 170 by inserting the following language after the comma: a food product is not misbranded FURTHER, AMEND by striking the language on lines 200 through 204, beginning at the word "If".
