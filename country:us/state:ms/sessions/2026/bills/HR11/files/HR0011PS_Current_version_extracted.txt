Section 1:
HR 11 (As Adopted by House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representatives Butler-Washington, Crudup, Nelson House Resolution 11 (As Adopted by House) A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE REIMAGINE PREP LIONS FOOTBALL TEAM AND COACH JAMYE HORTON UPON WINNING THE 2024 AND 2025 LITTLE SWAC FOOTBALL CHAMPIONSHIPS AND FOR THEIR OUTSTANDING AND MEMORABLE SEASONS. WHEREAS, the Reimagine Prep Lions Football Team captured the 2024 and 2025 Little SWAC Football Championships due to their demonstrated expectational athletic ability, dedication, discipline and team work throughout their back-to-back winning seasons; and WHEREAS, under the incomparable leadership of Coach Jamye Horton, the Lions have continued to grow in skill, sportsmanship, character, and academic excellence, and as a result, they represent their school and community with great pride and integrity; and WHEREAS, through hard work, sacrifice and commitment to excellence, the Reimagine Prep Football Team achieved the outstanding honor of becoming Little SWAC Foot Champions in 2024; and WHEREAS, this inspirational team maintained its momentum and competitive spirit and secured a second consecutive title as Little SWAC Football Champions in 2025 - accomplishing remarkable back-to-back championship victories; and WHEREAS, these back-to-back championship victories are a true testament not only to their athletic skill, but to their leadership, perseverance, unity, and the belief that success is earned through effort, preparation and teamwork; and WHEREAS, additionally, Coach Jamye Horton has exemplified what it means to be a mentor, educator and leader by instilling confidence, responsibility, sportsmanship and character both on and off the football field; and WHEREAS, the Reimagine Prep Football Team has brought great honor to its school, families and community and as such, they continue to inspire others to dream big, work hard and strive for excellence; and WHEREAS, the Lions have developed a winning and cooperative spirit and a high degree of sportsmanship, and their activities and attitude reflect great credit upon the individual players and Coach Horton and bring honor to Reimagine Prep; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend excellence, especially when evidenced by the youth of our state, such as the Reimagine Prep Lions Football Team, who are its future: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Reimagine Prep Football Team and Coach Jamye Horton for their winning back-to-back Little SWAC Football Championships for 2024 and 2025 and for displaying tireless devotion to their athletic endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Reimagine Prep, Coach Jamye Horton and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
