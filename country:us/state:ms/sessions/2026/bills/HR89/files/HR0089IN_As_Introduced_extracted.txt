HR 89 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Varner House Resolution 89 A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE FLORENCE HIGH SCHOOL GIRLS SOCCER TEAM FOR WINNING THE MISSISSIPPI HIGH SCHOOL ACTIVITIES ASSOCIATION (MHSAA) CLASS 5A STATE CHAMPIONSHIP. WHEREAS, the Florence High School Girls Soccer Team reigned supreme at the Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class 5A State Championship, bringing home the Gold Ball in their third consecutive title game appearance and marking the team's second state championship win in the last three years; and WHEREAS, fans crowded the stands to watch the Lady Eagles showcase their exemplary skill and grit on the pitch, and the team's spectacular performance did not disappoint as they clinched the championship title in a 4-1 victory over the East Central Lady Hornets; and WHEREAS, Florence took an early lead as Mallory Jackson netted the opening goal in the 5th minute of the championship game, setting the team up for success, and though the Lady Hornets quickly managed to put one past the keeper to momentarily even the score, the Lady Eagles never waivered in determination and stamina as they fought to regain control of the game; and WHEREAS, relying on their elite offense, the Lady Eagles quickly bounced back as Mary Elise Taylor and Jordyn Quick both managed to find the back of the net before halftime, and the team's defense maintained dominance in the second half of the game, refusing to let the Lady Hornets score again; and WHEREAS, Jordyn Quick landed the final blow of the game with another goal and secured the well-earned win for the Lady Eagles in a triumphant finale to their incredible season; and WHEREAS, the team's previous loss in penalty kicks last year lit a fire under the returning competitors, motivating them to train even harder and return to the championship better than before, and their hard work paid off in spades, making their comeback victory that much sweeter; and WHEREAS, after winning four state titles and reaching the state championship for the fifth time, the senior members of the Lady Eagles will surely carry the unforgettable memories they have made during their time on this dynamic team, as well as the lessons of teamwork, dedication and perseverance they have learned, throughout their lives as they remember the feeling of greatness that accompanies being a champion; and WHEREAS, the Lady Eagles have been superbly coached and inspired by Head Coach Bill Hood, who has broadened the team's knowledge of the sport and refined their skills in execution, producing high caliber players and plays; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend outstanding Mississippi student-athletes, especially those such as the Florence High School Girls Soccer Team, and we congratulate this elite group of student-athletes, whose talent and drive have brought great pride to their school, their community and the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Florence High School Girls Soccer Team upon winning the Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class 5A State Championship, and extend best wishes to the team for continued success in all their future endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to the Florence High School Girls Soccer Team and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
