Senate Resolution 87

(As Adopted by Senate)

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE 2025-2026 PICAYUNE MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL MAROON TIDE VARSITY CHEER TEAM AND COACH JESSICA SMITH FOR THEIR HISTORIC DOUBLE TITLE NATIONAL AND STATE ACHIEVEMENTS.

     WHEREAS, we join the students, faculty, parents and alumni of Picayune Memorial High School in recognizing the historic Double Title achievement by the 2025-2026 Maroon Tide Varsity Cheer Team; and

     WHEREAS, the 2025-2026 Picayune Memorial High School (PMHS) Varsity Cheerleading squad, coached by Jessica Smith and Caroline Nichelson, achieved major success as 2025 MHSAA State Champions and 2026 UCA Small Varsity Non-Tumbling D2 National Champions.  The 18-member team, which competed in Orlando, Florida, includes Lauren Ball, Kaidyn Collins, Zarriah Daniels, Shemori Felton, Calya Harris, Rikyia Henderson, Anaviia Hille, Zoey Hodges, Kaylin Hunter, Lyric Jackson, Abigail Kimbrough, Ca'miya Mays, Kennedi McCormick, Skylar Rice, London Simpson, Laila Smith, Gabrielle Stevenson, and Amelia Trieu; and

     WHEREAS, the Cheer Team won:  National Title:  2026 UCA (Universal Cheerleaders Association) National Champions.  Secured February 8, 2026; Division:  Small Varsity D2 Non-Tumbling Game Day; Field:  Earned 1st place in a competitive field of over 80 programs from across the United States, and State Title:  2025 MHSAA State Champions; and

     WHEREAS, the team secured the 1st place finish in the Varsity Game Day 6A Division at the Mississippi Coliseum in Jackson on December 13, 2025.  Their routine was highlighted by a Band Chant, a Crowd-Leading section, and the PMHS Fight Song, specifically designed to showcase authentic game-day spirit; and

     WHEREAS, this marks the first time in Picayune Memorial High School history that the varsity cheerleading program has secured both the State and National titles in the same competitive season; and

     WHEREAS, the squad competed at the highest national level, bringing home the gold medal and national prestige to the State of Mississippi; and

     WHEREAS, in addition to their athletic commitment, the team members are recognized as dedicated student-athletes.  Each member maintains full academic eligibility under the rigorous standards set by the Picayune School District and the MHSAA, balancing a year-round practice schedule with her classroom responsibilities.  The program emphasizes discipline, character, and leadership both on or off the mat; and

     WHEREAS, the City of Picayune, along with Picayune Memorial High School hosted a formal community celebration for these champions on March 12, 2026, at Crosby Commons in Picayune:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the 2025-2026 Picayune Memorial High School Maroon Tide Varsity Cheer Team and Coach Jessica Smith for their historic Double Title National and State achievements, and extend to the team, coaches, parents and sponsors our best wishes for future success.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Coach Jesssica Smith, and Principal Kristi Mitchell for the team, forwarded to the Picayune School District Board of Trustees and made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
