Adopted

 

AMENDMENT NO 1 PROPOSED TO

 

Senate Bill No. 2594

 

BY: Senator(s) Williams

 

     AMEND on lines 56 through 80 by striking the definition for "blight" in its entirety and replacing it with the following:

          (h)  (i)  "Blight" means a building or area which by reason of dilapidation, deterioration, age or obsolescence; inadequate provision for ventilation, light, air, sanitation or open spaces; high density of population or overcrowding; existence of conditions which endanger life or property by fire and other causes; or any combination of such factors, is conducive to ill health, transmission of disease, infant mortality, juvenile delinquency or crime and is detrimental to the public health, safety, morals or welfare.  "Blight" also means any combination of factors which substantially impair or arrest the sound growth of a county, city, public university or community college, or any other political subdivision of the state, inhibit the provisions of housing accommodations, or constitute an economic or social liability and are a menace to the public health, safety, morals or welfare.

              (ii)  For the purposes of this paragraph (h), factors considered detrimental to the public health, safety, morals or welfare shall include, but are not limited to including, prostitution, trafficking and substance abuse.

     FURTHER, AMEND on line 106 by inserting the following after the word "board":

of trustees of the Mississippi Department of Archives and History
