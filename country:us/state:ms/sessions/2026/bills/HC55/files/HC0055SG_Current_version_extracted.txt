HC 55 (As Adopted by House and Senate) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Lamar House Concurrent Resolution 55 (As Adopted by House and Senate) A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE NORTHWEST MISSISSIPPI COMMUNITY COLLEGE DANCE TEAM FOR WINNING A NATIONAL CHAMPIONSHIP AT THE 2026 UDA COLLEGE NATIONALS AND CONTINUING A CHAMPIONSHIP LEGACY WITH A THREE-PEAT VICTORY IN THE OPEN HIP-HOP DIVISION. WHEREAS, the Northwest Mississippi Community College Dance Team, under the outstanding leadership of Head Coach Patrice Christian, has once again demonstrated elite skill and ability by earning top national honors at the 2026 Universal Dance Association (UDA) College Dance Team National Championships in Orlando, Florida; and WHEREAS, at this year's national competition, the Northwest Dance Team captured a first place victory in the Open Hip-Hop division, securing their third consecutive championship title in the division and establishing a historic three-peat triumph at the national level; and WHEREAS, the Northwest Dance Team also earned a third place national finish in the Open Game Day Dance division, showcasing versatility, depth and competitive distinction in a highly talented national field; and WHEREAS, the key to this dynamic team's championship success has been the countless hours of preparation, discipline, sportsmanship and dedication of each team member, culminating in exceptional routines that exemplify the highest standards of collegiate athletics and artistic performance; and WHEREAS, guided by the vision and leadership of Head Coach Patrice Christian, the Northwest Mississippi Community College Dance program has earned three national championships in the past four years, marking a period of unprecedented achievement and a rapid ascent to national prominence; and WHEREAS, the Northwest Dance Team roster represents a diverse and gifted group of student-athletes from across Mississippi and neighboring states, reflecting the broad reach, appeal and competitive spirit of the program; and WHEREAS, the continued success of the Northwest Dance Team is made possible through the unwavering support of the team's coaches, families, college administration and the entire Northwest Ranger community; and WHEREAS, the members of this team will surely carry the unforgettable memories they have made, as well as the lessons of teamwork, commitment and perseverance they have learned, throughout their lives as they remember the feeling of greatness that accompanies being a champion; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of this Legislature to recognize and commend excellence in collegiate athletics, especially that displayed by the Northwest Mississippi Community College Dance Team, whose accomplished members bring distinction, honor and national recognition to their institution and to the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, THE SENATE CONCURRING THEREIN, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Northwest Mississippi Community College Dance Team for their national championship performance at the 2026 Universal Dance Association (UDA) College Nationals, including a three-peat national title in the Open Hip-Hop division, and extend our best wishes for continued success in all future competitions and endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Head Coach Patrice Christian, Northwest Mississippi Community College President Dr. Michael Heindl, and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
