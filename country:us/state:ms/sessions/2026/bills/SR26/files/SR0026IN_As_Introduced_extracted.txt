SR 26 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Senator(s) Hopson, DuPree Senate Resolution 26 A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE WARREN CENTRAL HIGH SCHOOL VIKINGS FOOTBALL TEAM AND HEAD COACH JOSH MORGAN FOR WINNING THE 2025 CLASS 6A STATE FOOTBALL CHAMPIONSHIP. WHEREAS, we join the students, faculty and fans of Warren Central High School in recognizing the football team for winning the 2025 Class 6A State Championship; and WHEREAS, at the State Championship on December 5, 2025, in Starkville, Mississippi, Warren Central used a dominant rushing attack and scored 28 unanswered points in the second half to defeat Hattiesburg 5634 at Davis Wade Stadium; and WHEREAS, the teams were tied 2121 at halftime before Warren Central took the lead for good early in the third quarter.  Xavier Jackson broke a 40-yard touchdown run at 9:17 of the third, and after Hattiesburg tied the game again on a nine-yard touchdown pass, the Vikings responded with a 60-yard touchdown pass from Nash Morgan to Michael Hall with 47 seconds left in the quarter.  Warren Central extended the lead in the fourth quarter with three touchdown runs of 33 and 52 yards by Morgan and a 45-yard run by Jackson; and WHEREAS, Warren Central finished with 487 total yards, including 360 rushing yards on 34 carries.  Nash Morgan ran for 184 yards and three touchdowns on 17 carries and completed a 60-yard touchdown pass.  Jackson Shaw added two rushing touchdowns.  Michael Hall had 84 receiving yards on four catches.  Fabian Davenport and Micah Moore led Warren Central with nine tackles each; and WHEREAS, the Warren Central Championship Football roster:  Caleb Lane, Michael Hall, Landon Green, Tristan Jamison, Javid Scott, Fabian Davenport, Keanthony Wynn, Micah Moore, Blake Caruthers, Nash Morgan, Chaka Walker, Jr., Landon Lumpkin, Jonah Artman, Graham Streif, Trystan Fuller, Braylon Jenkins, Kourtland Dillard, Doss Morgan, Evan Mallard, Cordero Reed, Jonas Green, Robert Williams, Paxton Lynch, Edward Mazie, Aiden Malone, Aaron Jenkins, Mack Bell, Logan Ables, Aidan Lumpkin, Quentin King, Deshawn Williams, Devin Demby, Mitchell Lewis, Jeremiah Brown, Odarion Williams, Kamren Bailey, Rodney Ford, Jr., Jaxon Boyd, Samuel Ross, Anthony Streif, Thomas Antwine, Nathaniel Moore, Kenny Woods, Kemarrion Shepard, Aiden Allen, Kemariyun Coffie, Tkylan Wright, Deontae Stowers, Crishun Brown, Kameron Reed, Trey Smith, Nick Price, Derrius Nicholson, Jackson Shaw, Marquise Overton, Kullen Kistler, Daniel Pitzer, Cameron Hall, Braden Cooper, Carmelo Clark, Deandre Flowers, Eddie London, Jordan Woods, Patrick Bowman, Layton Lee, Spencer Williams, Jaxin Fulgham, Simon Lenzinger, Ashton Saleh, James Jones, MacKaveon Bell, Kaiden Lindsey, Arva Foster, Jalen Dates, Cameron Kelly, Ja'mari Gaines, Nick Lee and Jaiden Smith; and Head Coach Josh Morgan and Assistant Coaches Rob Morgan, Corey Wilson, Matt Williams, Kacey Myers, Tim Hughes, Craig Crews, Wayne Lynch, Jeff Scott and Matt Brewer; and WHEREAS, it is with great pride that we recognize this group of student-athletes who have brought honor to their school, their community and to the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we hereby commend and congratulate the Warren Central High School Vikings Football Team and Head Coach Josh Morgan for winning the 2025 Class 6A State Football Championship, and extend to the team, coaches, parents and sponsors our best wishes for future success. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Head Coach Josh Morgan for the team, forwarded to Warren Central Principal Drew Rainer and the Vicksburg-Warren School District Board of Education, and be made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
