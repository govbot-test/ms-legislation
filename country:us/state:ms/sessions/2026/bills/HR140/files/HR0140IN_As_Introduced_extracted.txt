HR 140 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Bell (65th) House Resolution 140 A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND EXPRESSING GRATITUDE TO DR. LORI HILL MARSHALL, MD, FASA, FOR HER OUTSTANDING CONTRIBUTIONS IN THE FIELD OF HEALTHCARE AND TO HER COMMUNITY. WHEREAS, Dr. Lori Hill Marshall, MD, FASA, is an esteemed Mississippi physician who is known throughout the state and beyond for the immeasurable contributions she is making not only as a highly respected anesthesiologist and interventional pain medicine specialist, but as a passionate philanthropist, transformational leader and trailblazing woman committed to making a difference in the lives of others; and WHEREAS, a proud native of the Magnolia State, Dr. Marshall exhibited academic excellence and leadership from an early age, graduating as valedictorian from Madison Central High School, where she was selected for membership in the school's Hall of Fame, before attending the University of Southern Mississippi and being inducted into the Honor Society of Phi Kappa Phi; and WHEREAS, after graduating from USM, Dr. Marshall continued her education at the University of Mississippi School of Medicine, where her outstanding academic performance and clinical acumen earned her several prestigious honors, including the Don Q. Mitchell Barksdale Scholarship for academic excellence, the Leonard Fabian Award for excellence in Anesthesiology, and the Neuroscience Critical Care Award; and WHEREAS, Dr. Marshall remained at UMMC after completing her medical degree and did her residency in Anesthesiology, serving as Chief Resident during her CA-3 year, before furthering her training by completing a fellowship in Interventional Pain Medicine; and WHEREAS, since 2012, Dr. Marshall, board-certified as an anesthesiologist and pain medicine specialist, has served as President, CEO and Medical Director of Premier Pain Care, P.C., and today she also serves as the Chief of Anesthesiology at Methodist Le Bonheur Healthcare Olive Branch and North Memphis Hospitals; and WHEREAS, believing that she can help make a greater impact and achieve more success for the common good when working collaboratively with others, Dr. Marshall actively serves in leadership roles on numerous professional organizations, including:  Vice-chair of the Board of Trustees of the Mississippi State Medical Association; Director of the Mississippi Society of Anesthesiologists; and member of the Board of Directors of the American Society of Anesthesiologists (ASA), where she has served on various committees such as the Committee on Economics, Committee on Practice Management, Committee on Professional Development, Committee on Membership and the Section on Fiscal Affairs; and WHEREAS, in addition to championing healthcare issues, Dr. Marshall works tirelessly through noteworthy civic organizations to better her community, including as:  Vice President and Membership Chair of the LeFleur's Bluff Chapter of the Links, Inc.; Immediate Past President of the Jackson Chapter of Jack and Jill of America, Inc.; life member of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc.; and longstanding member of the Junior League of Jackson, where she has served as the 2020 Mistletoe Marketplace Chair, 2019 Connecting Communities Co-chair, Bargain Boutique Acquisitions Chair, Junior League of Jackson Publications Office Manager, 2015 Mistletoe Marketplace Staging Chair, and Nominating Committee member; and WHEREAS, in recognition of her "interminable quest to be a constructive force for sustainable and determinative change," Dr. Marshall was honored to be named a laureate of the Class of 2025 Women of Distinction, a national Girl Scout program that celebrates the achievements of women whose personal and professional accomplishments make them positive role models who inspire girls to achieve their goals and dreams, exemplifying ethical leadership and community service; and WHEREAS, the Mississippi State Medical Association also celebrated Dr. Marshall during the September 2025 Women in Medicine Month, honoring her dedication to advancing care, empowering physicians and strengthening medicine in Mississippi; and WHEREAS, whether by training, mentoring or inspiring the next generation of physicians, working toward solutions to healthcare issues affecting all populations, especially the underrepresented, marginalized, disadvantaged and rural populations, or generously volunteering her time and resources to spearhead projects aimed at bettering her fellows, Dr. Marshall has been a trailblazer every step of the way whose presence in the medical profession and many organizations reflects a career and life built on service to others, excellence and advocacy; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to commend outstanding Mississippians such as Dr. Lori Hill Marshall, MD, FASA, a woman of courage, confidence and character who has selflessly devoted her entire life to making the world a better place everyday: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend Dr. Lori Hill Marshall, MD, FASA, on her remarkable medical career and express heartfelt gratitude to her for outstanding contributions in the field of healthcare and to her community.  FURTHER, we do hereby extend sincere wishes for continued success in all her future endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Dr. Lori Hill Marshall, MD, FASA, and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
