Section 1:
HB 511 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Business and Commerce By: Representative Porter House Bill 511 AN ACT TO REQUIRE BUSINESSES THAT PROVIDE RECURRING SUBSCRIPTION OR MEMBERSHIP SERVICES TO CUSTOMERS TO NOTIFY CUSTOMERS AT LEAST TWENTY-FOUR HOURS BEFORE CHARGING CUSTOMERS FOR SUCH RECURRING SUBSCRIPTIONS OR MEMBERSHIPS; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  In addition to and subject to federal law, any business or entity that provides recurring subscription or membership services to customers shall provide notice to customers at least twenty-four (24) hours before charging customers for such  recurring subscriptions or memberships. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
