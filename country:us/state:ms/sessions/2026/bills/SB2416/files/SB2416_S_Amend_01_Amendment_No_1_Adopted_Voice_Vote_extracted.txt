Section 1:
SB2416_S_Amend_01 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 1 PROPOSED TO Senate Bill No. 2416 BY: Senator(s) Blackmon, Sparks AMEND on lines 14 and 15 by deleting the following: or is suspended without pay FURTHER, AMEND on line 19 by changing "may" to "shall" and "with" to "without". FURTHER, AMEND on line 37 by adding the following before the period: and shall receive back pay from the date of the officer's suspension FURTHER, AMEND on line 51 by adding the following before the comma: unless the person is lawfully reinstated FURTHER, AMEND on lines 52 and 53 by deleting the underlined language. FURTHER, AMEND the title to conform.
