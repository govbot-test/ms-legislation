Section 1:
SB2152 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations By: Senator(s) Thomas Senate Bill 2152 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE MIDWAY COMMUNITY WATER ASSOCIATION FOR THE PURPOSE OF DEFRAYING COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH REPAIRS, UPGRADES, AND IMPROVEMENTS TO ITS WATER SYSTEM FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1. The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the Capital Expense Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the Midway Community Water Association for the purpose of defraying costs associated with repairs, upgrades, and improvements to its water system for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027.................................. .................................................. $ 400,000.00. SECTION 2.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the Capital Expense Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 3.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
