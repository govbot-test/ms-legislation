Section 1:
HB1972 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations A By: Representative Bailey (23rd) House Bill 1972 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE GOVERNING AUTHORITIES OF THE TOWN OF BRUCE TO ASSIST IN PAYING COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH VARIOUS IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the governing authorities of the Town of Bruce, Mississippi, to assist in paying costs associated with various improvement projects as described in Section 2 of this act for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027.............................$1,650,000.00 SECTION 2.  The money appropriated to the governing authorities of the Town of Bruce under Section 1 of this act shall be allocated as follows: (a)  One Hundred Twenty-five Thousand Dollars ($125,000.00) for costs associated with renovations to the town square, including replacing the sprinkler system and making lot improvements; (b)  Two Hundred Thousand Dollars ($200,000.00) for paying costs associated with water shut-off replacements; (c)  Seven Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars ($750,000.00) for paying costs associated with the acquisition of a fire truck; (d)  Seventy-five Thousand Dollars ($75,000.00) for paying costs associated with fencing improvements at the town park, fencing improvements at the old National Guard Armory, and lighting for soccer fields; (e)  Two Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars ($250,000.00) for paying costs associated with general water system line repairs; and (f)  Two Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars ($250,000.00) for paying costs associated with sewer system repairs, upgrades and improvements. SECTION 3.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 4.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
