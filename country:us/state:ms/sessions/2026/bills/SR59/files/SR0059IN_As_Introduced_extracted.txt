SR 59 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Senator(s) Carter, Hill, Wiggins, DeBar, Thompson, England, Seymour, Harkins, Hopson, DeLano, Ladner Senate Resolution 59 A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE 2026 ST. PATRICK LADY IRISH GIRLS SOCCER TEAM AND STANDOUT MEREDITH TINDELL FOR THEIR FOUR-PEAT STATE CHAMPIONSHIP. WHEREAS, on February 20, 2026, the St. Patrick High School Girls Lady Irish Soccer Team from Biloxi, Mississippi, claimed revenge for the 2021 squad, clinching a four-peat with a 2-0 Class 1 win; and WHEREAS, before the boys State Championship game, the Lady Irish claimed their fourth straight state title as they defeated the Lady Saints 2-0.  A loss would have been a first for St. Patrick since November 11 against Ocean Springs, which saw them drop 1-0. Since then, the Lady Irish posted an undefeated record of 17-0-4.  The four ties came against Gulfport, Petal, Clinton, and Madison Central, all 7A competition; and WHEREAS, it can be hard to translate regular season success to the playoffs, but St. Patrick had no problem doing just that.  Their defense stepped up to hand the Our Lady Academy Crescents a 2-0 shutout.  Winning may never get old, but the St. Patrick Lady Irish Girls Soccer Team is getting used to it with its fifth in a row; and WHEREAS, standouts, Meredith Tindell and Bella Jordan scored both of St. Patrick's goals, bringing their combined season tally to 40 goals.  St. Patrick's victory bumped their record up to 18-4-4; and WHEREAS, the St. Patrick High School Lady Irish 2025-2026 Varsity Girls Soccer Roster:  Victoria Pickens, Charlotte Saucier, Lila Miller, Madilynn Bentz, Gianna Encrapera, Brooklyn Guymon, Bella Jordan, Katerine Adams, Sonora Ritchie, Tenley Helton, Grace Baker, Olivia Jones, Avery Stolarski, Ellie Saucier, Nora Mestayer, Meredith Tindell, Ava Simnicht, Ella Adams, Samantha Johnson, Mary Weeks, Gianna Bull, Brianna Lowe, Olivia Codella, Emma Trosclair, Lily Viglianco, Kate O'Grady, Abby Warburton, Josie Vann, Ella Warburton, and Amberlynn Lowe; Head Coach, Angela McClure; Assistant Coaches, Carissa Harrison and Courtney Head; and WHEREAS, it is with great pride that we recognize these student-athletes who have brought honor to their school, community, and to the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the 2026 St. Patrick Lady Irish Girls Soccer Team and standout Meredith Tindell for their four-peat State Championship, and extend to the team, coaches, administration, parents, and sponsors our best wishes for future success. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Head Coach Angela McClure, Meredith Tindell and Principal Paul Knapstein, forwarded to the Board of Directors for St. Patrick High School and made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
