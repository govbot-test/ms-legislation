Section 1:
SB2526_S_(Cmte_Sub)_Amend_01 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 1 PROPOSED TO Cmte Sub for Senate Bill No. 2526 BY: Senator(s) Hill, Wiggins AMEND under line 227 by inserting the following language as a new section and renumbering subsequent sections accordingly: SECTION *  Upon a finding of inadequate service of any rural water system, the Public Service Commission shall have the authority to reconstitute the board of any such system on a temporary basis until the next annual meeting at which a new board shall be constituted.
