Section 1:
SB2640 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Public Property By: Senator(s) Whaley, Younger Senate Bill 2640 AN ACT TO AMEND SECTION 69-5-114, MISSISSIPPI CODE OF 1972, TO EXPAND THE LIST OF FACILITIES AVAILABLE TO CERTAIN ORGANIZATIONS FOR LIMITED USE AT NO CHARGE; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  Section 69-5-114, Mississippi Code of 1972, is amended as follows: 69-5-114.  In any livestock facility constructed, renovated or expanded with funds from the grant program authorized under Section 18, Chapter 530, Laws of 1995, and the Mississippi Trade Mart, Mississippi Coliseum, Kirk Fordice Equine Center, Mississippi State Fairgrounds and related livestock and equine barns, stalls and all other facilities, the members of the Future Farmers of America, the 4-H Club, the Junior Livestock Association, the Mississippi Cattlemen's Association and the United States Pony Club, Inc., may hold up to three (3) animal or livestock shows * * * or, sales or other events per year per facility at no charge for use of the facility or for utilities. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
