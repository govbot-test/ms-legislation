SR 96 (As Adopted by Senate) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Senator(s) Williams Senate Resolution 96 (As Adopted by Senate) A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE EUPORA HIGH SCHOOL EAGLES CHEERLEADERS AND CHEER COACHES JAMIE WALKER AND KRISTY TINDALL ON WINNING THE 2025 MISSISSIPPI HIGH SCHOOL ACTIVITIES ASSOCIATION (MHSAA) 2A STATE CHAMPIONSHIP. WHEREAS, on December 13, 2025, the Eupora High School Eagles Cheerleading Team reigned supreme when the cheerleaders captured the 2025 Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) 2A Game Day Division Cheer State Championship at the Mississippi Coliseum in Jackson, Mississippi; and WHEREAS, the Eupora Eagles' state title solidifies the squad's high-performing reputation as a consistent program known for its precise movements appearing effortless as the athletically fit members of the team safely performed complex routines as a single, cohesive unit throughout the season; and WHEREAS, through the Eupora High School cheerleaders' dedication and excellence in performance, members of the team have demonstrated exceptional character and discipline, whether in the classroom or on the sidelines or the mat, and have developed unparalleled teamwork representing the highest standards of sportsmanship and leadership through school spirit; and WHEREAS, the extraordinary success of the Eupora High School Eagles would not have been possible without the countless hours of intense practice, positive attitudes and enthusiasm of each of the following talented student-athletes who comprise the 2025 cheerleading squad:  Chloe Bass, Gwendolyn Browder, Mallory Collum, Gracie Dean, Caleigh Fason, Olivia Frank, Kinley Gilbert, Emma Gray, Adalee Hardin, Swayze Hodges, Kinsley Huffman, Madison Johnson, Brinkley Lee, Ansley Mason, Avery McCluskey, Taylor Payton, Bentley Ray, Tinsley Richardson, Jamesha Rush, Cydney Smith and Corinne Walker; and WHEREAS, the cheerleaders have been superbly coached and inspired by Cheer Coaches Jamie Walker and Kristy Tindall, who together have combined their considerable talents and years of experience to refine the squad's skills and precision in execution; and WHEREAS, it is with great pride that we recognize and honor the achievements of these outstanding student-athletes, who have brought honor to their families, school, community and the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Eupora High School Eagles Cheerleaders and Cheer Coaches Jamie Walker and Kristy Tindall on winning the Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class 2A Game Day Division Cheer State Championship, and extend best wishes for continued success in all their future athletic and academic endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Cheer Coaches Jamie Walker and Kristy Tindall for the Eupora High School Eagles Cheerleading Team and made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
