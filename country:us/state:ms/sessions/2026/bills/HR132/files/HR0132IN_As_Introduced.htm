MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Representative Holloway (27th)

House Resolution 132

A RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING AND COMMENDING THE MORTON HIGH SCHOOL PANTHERS BOYS BASKETBALL TEAM FOR WINNING THE MISSISSIPPI HIGH SCHOOL ACTIVITIES ASSOCIATION (MHSAA) CLASS 4A STATE CHAMPIONSHIP.

     WHEREAS, the Morton High School Panthers Boys Basketball Team  delivered an extraordinary performance during the 2025-2026 basketball season, culminating the capture of the Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class 4A State Championship and securing their third state title and their first since 1998; and

     WHEREAS, the Panthers have demonstrated outstanding athletic excellence, discipline, and perseverance throughout the 20252026 season, compiling an impressive overall record of 22 wins and eight losses, showcasing their consistency, resilience and competitive spirit; and

     WHEREAS, the team excelled in district competition with a strong 73 record, proving themselves as leaders among their peers, and they earned a statewide ranking of 31, reflecting their elite level of play and dedication to excellence across Mississippi; and

     WHEREAS, outstandingly, the team concluded their remarkable season with a decisive 67-55 victory over Leake Central High School, before a crowd of enthusiastic supporters, demonstrating exceptional skill, teamwork and determination on the court; and

     WHEREAS, throughout their season, the Panthers played with clear eyes, full hearts and unimaginable amounts of fortitude, and during the state championship game, their mental stamina and athletic prowess never wavered from the moment they first hustled onto the court until the final buzzer buzzed, as they outlasted, outplayed and ultimately overpowered Leake Central; and

     WHEREAS, this elite team is comprised of the following talented student-athletes:  Justice Anderson, Bryson Bounds, Tristian Ealy, Tristan Hobson, Desmand Jenkins, Dannirius Louis, Dolph McDonald, Xavier McDonald, Avonte Reese and Jaylin Stowers; and

     WHEREAS, the Panthers have been superbly coached and inspired by Head Coach Maurice Bowie and Assistant Coaches Roderick Loper, Mario Mapp and Ashley McDougle, who have broadened the players' knowledge of the game and refined their skills in execution, and they continue to build upon the legacy of superior play and true sportsmanship forged by the tenacious and undaunted spirit of each team member; and

     WHEREAS, this recognition stands as a testament to the hard work, determination, and unity displayed by this exceptional team, and their legacy will continue to inspire excellence both on and off the court; and

     WHEREAS, through teamwork, commitment and leadership from players, coaches, and staff, the Panthers rose to the pinnacle of success by capturing the 20252026 MHSAA Class 4A Boys Basketball State Championship, serving as an inspiration to young athletes everywhere; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend the outstanding achievements of Mississippi student-athletes, especially those such as the Morton Panthers, whose hard work, determination, and championship spirit bring honor to Morton High School, the Morton community and the State of Mississippi:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Morton High School Panthers Boys Basketball Team for winning the Mississippi High School Activities Association Class (MHSAA) 4A State Championship, and extend best wishes for continued success both on and off the court.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Head Coach Maurice Bowie and the Morton High School Panthers Boys Basketball Team, and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
