Section 1:
SB2142 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Judiciary, Division A By: Senator(s) Rhodes Senate Bill 2142 AN ACT TO PROVIDE THAT A PERSON OPERATING A MOTOR VEHICLE INVOLVED IN A MOTOR VEHICLE ACCIDENT SHALL NOT RECOVER DAMAGES RELATED TO THE ACCIDENT IF THE OPERATOR DID NOT HAVE THE MINIMUM AMOUNTS OF MOTOR VEHICLE LIABILITY INSURANCE REQUIRED BY CHAPTER 15 OF TITLE 63, MISSISSIPPI CODE OF 1972, WHEN THE ACCIDENT OCCURRED; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  A person operating a motor vehicle involved in a motor vehicle accident shall not recover damages related to the accident if the operator did not have the minimum amounts of motor vehicle liability insurance required by Chapter 15 of Title 63, Mississippi Code of 1972, when the accident occurred. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
