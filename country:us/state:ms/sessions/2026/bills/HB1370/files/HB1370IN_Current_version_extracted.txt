Section 1:
HB1370 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Public Health and Human Services By: Representative Waldo House Bill 1370 AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE NURSE PRACTITIONERS TO SERVE AS THE CERTIFYING PHYSICIAN FOR THE PURPOSE OF COMPREHENSIVE MANAGEMENT OF A PATIENT'S DIABETES IF THE NURSE PRACTITIONER IS PRACTICING INCIDENT TO THE PHYSICIAN WITH WHOM THE NURSE PRACTITIONER HAS A COLLABORATIVE RELATIONSHIP, AND TO PRESCRIBE THERAPEUTIC SHOES, MODIFICATIONS AND INSERTS FOR PATIENTS WITH DIABETES, IN ACCORDANCE WITH RULES AND OTHER GUIDELINES FROM THE CENTERS FOR MEDICARE AND MEDICAID SERVICES; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  In accordance with rules and other guidelines from the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services: (a)  Nurse practitioners are authorized to serve as the certifying physician for the purpose of comprehensive management of a patient's diabetes if the nurse practitioner is practicing incident to the physician with whom the nurse practitioner has a collaborative relationship; and (b)  Nurse practitioners are authorized to prescribe therapeutic shoes, modifications and inserts for patients with diabetes. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
