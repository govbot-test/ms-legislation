Section 1:
HB1314_H_(Cmte_Sub)_Amend_01 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 1 PROPOSED TO Cmte Sub for House Bill No. 1314 BY: Representatives Ford (54th), Remak AMEND on lines 1271 and 1272 and on line 1291 by deleting "Twenty-five Thousand Dollars ($25,000.00)" and inserting in lieu thereof "Seventy-five Thousand Dollars ($75,000.00)". AMEND FURTHER, on line 1296 by inserting the following after the period: "For any such contract, if a subcontractor of the general contractor for the contract does not have general liability insurance coverage in the minimum amount required by this subsection, then the general contractor shall be liable for any amount under such minimum amount of coverage for which the subcontractor is underinsured or not insured." AMEND title to conform.
