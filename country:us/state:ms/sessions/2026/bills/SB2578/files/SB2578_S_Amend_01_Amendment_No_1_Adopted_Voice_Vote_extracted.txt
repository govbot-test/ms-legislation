Section 1:
SB2578_S_Amend_01 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 1 PROPOSED TO Senate Bill No. 2578 BY: Senator(s) Simmons (13th), Chassaniol AMEND on line 34 by inserting the words "in any combination of either state funds or" after "(20%)". FURTHER, AMEND on line 35 by inserting the following after the period: A municipality shall not receive more than Two Hundred Thousand Dollars ($200,000.00) under this section within a single fiscal year. FURTHER, AMEND on line 39 by striking "Mississippi Development Authority" and inserting "Department of Finance and Administration" in lieu thereof. FURTHER, AMEND the title to conform.
