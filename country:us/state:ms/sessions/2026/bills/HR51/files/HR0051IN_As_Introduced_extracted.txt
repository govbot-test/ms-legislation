HR 51 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Tullos House Resolution 51 A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE RALEIGH HIGH SCHOOL FOOTBALL TEAM FOR WINNING THE MISSISSIPPI HIGH SCHOOL ACTIVITIES ASSOCIATION (MHSAA) CLASS 3A STATE CHAMPIONSHIP. WHEREAS, the Raleigh High School Football Team reigned supreme at the Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class 3A State Championship, a truly impressive feat; and WHEREAS, on December 6, 2025, fans crowded Davis Wade Stadium in Starkville, Mississippi, to watch the Lions showcase their exemplary skill and tenacity on the field, and the team's remarkable performance did not disappoint as they clinched the championship title in a 12-6 victory over Noxubee County; and WHEREAS, both of Raleigh's touchdowns were scored by senior Jamarcus McCullum, and the quarterback and linebacker was deservedly given the MVP title for the championship game; and WHEREAS, rallied by the support of their fans and their coaches, the Lions never waivered in determination and mental stamina, and the team's impenetrable defense successfully stopped Noxubee County on multiple pass attempts and rushes in the red zone with four seconds remaining to secure the win; and WHEREAS, the Lions have earned a reputation for greatness over the last four years, and after winning their first state championship in 2022, the team has continued to build on their extraordinary efforts to achieve victory once again; and WHEREAS, throughout the year, the Lions relied on their swift feet, strategic maneuvering and athletic prowess to outlast and outplay their opponents, closing the season with an outstanding record of 14-1; and WHEREAS, the Lions have ascended to the pinnacle of football excellence this season thanks to the hard work and unmatched spirit of the team's student-athletes:  Brendan Harper, Deuce Carter, Kentravis Page, Marquis Perkins, Jamarcus McCullum, Jaylin Owens, Dariyun Moses, Damonta Keyes, Julian McCullum, Jordan Williamson, Strick Strickland, Jabrinyt Clark, Tyler Jones, Kaden Ashley, Brandon Laird, Mason Smith, Jordan Smith, Ollie Owens, Davion Hicks, Royce Howze, Will Ethridge, D'travion Franks, Zyrian Malone, Buck Fails, Jordan Ellis, Nick Morgan, Braylon Pace, Clyde Jones, Jace Dixon, Noah Westbrook, Jaden Purvis, Zhaiden Malone, JaMarion Owens, Kaleb Saterfiel, Kolten Risher, Jayden Wilbon, Jalik Jones, Jeffery Allen, Brynn Boykin, Jason Page, Jerry Harvey, Harlon Dove, Landon Allen and Will Higdon, with the support of the team's managers:  Hunter Watts, Ethan Watts, Quentrez McKee, Landon Ellis, Jack Means, LanDon Byrd, James Smith, Rylan Dearman, Jasper Barnes and Deon Fairley, as well as the team's filmers, Holden Buffington and Kylen Smith; and WHEREAS, this dynamic team has been superbly coached and inspired by Head Coach Ryan Higdon and Assistant Coaches Jared Smith, Larry Means, La-Dean Byrd and Ronald Smith, who have broadened the players' knowledge of the sport and refined their skills in execution; and WHEREAS, the members of this team will surely carry the unforgettable memories they have made, as well as the lessons of teamwork, dedication and perseverance they have learned, throughout their lives as they remember the feeling of accomplishment that accompanies being a champion; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend Mississippi's talented student-athletes, especially those such as the Raleigh High School Football Team, and it is with great pride that we congratulate the members of this elite team, who have brought honor and pride to their school, their community and the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Raleigh High School Football Team upon winning the Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) Class 3A State Championship, and extend best wishes to the team for continued success in all their future endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to the Raleigh High School Football Team and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
