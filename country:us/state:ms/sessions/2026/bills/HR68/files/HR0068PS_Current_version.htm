House Resolution 68

(As Adopted by House)

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING CYRUS R. FLAHERTY UPON THE IMPRESSIVE OCCASION OF BEING ACCEPTED INTO WEST POINT ACADEMY.

     WHEREAS, Cyrus R. Flaherty has earned admission into the distinguished and high revered institution of West Point Academy, much to the joy and excitement of his parents; and

     WHEREAS, this Tupelo High School senior is a task-oriented member of the National Honor Society whose intellectual acumen and impressive work ethic have placed him consecutively on the Superintendent's list since 2014, and earned him the title of Rotary Scholar in 2021 through 2024; and

     WHEREAS, a passionate officer of the Teenage Republicans, Cyrus is also a member of the Sincerely Seniors Club and the manager of the Golden Wave Varsity Football Team; and

     WHEREAS, Cyrus is a four-year cadet who began his journey in 2021 and has served as a Cadet Major, Staff Officer who led the Program Operations and Cadet Training, has been a participant of the All-Star Junior Cadet Leadership Challenge; and

     WHEREAS, a leader in spirit and in truth, Cyrus is an honorable member of Scouting America Troop 85 in Tupelo, where he has earned the coveted rank of Eagle Scout, was a former Senior Patrol Leader, former order of the Arrow Chapter Chief and Order of the Arrow-Brotherhood; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to celebrate the accomplishments of our youth because they are Mississippi's future:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate Cyrus R. Flaherty upon the impressive occasion of being accepted into West Point Academy.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Cyrus R. Flaherty and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
