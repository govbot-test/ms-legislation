To: Local and Private Legislation

By: Representative Fondren

House Bill 4080

(As Sent to Governor)

AN ACT TO AMEND CHAPTER 941, LOCAL AND PRIVATE LAWS OF 2022, BY EXTENDING THE DATE OF REPEAL FROM JULY 1, 2026, TO JULY 1, 2030, ON THE PROVISION OF LAW THAT AUTHORIZES THE BOARD OF SUPERVISORS OF JACKSON COUNTY, MISSISSIPPI, TO CONTRIBUTE FUNDS TO THE JUNIOR AUXILIARY OF PASCAGOULA-MOSS POINT; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES.

     BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI:

     SECTION 1.  Chapter 941, Local and Private Laws of 2022, is amended as follows:

     Section 1.  (1)  The Board of Supervisors of Jackson County, Mississippi, in its discretion, may contribute funds annually in an amount not to exceed Five Thousand Dollars ($5,000.00), out of any available county funds, to the Junior Auxiliary of Pascagoula-Moss Point, a nonprofit organization that is dedicated to community improvement by meeting the needs of children, youth and families.

     (2)  The provisions of this section shall be repealed on July 1, * * *2026 2030.

     SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage.
