Section 1:
HB4080 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Local and Private Legislation By: Representative Fondren House Bill 4080 AN ACT TO AUTHORIZE THE BOARD OF SUPERVISORS OF JACKSON COUNTY, MISSISSIPPI, TO CONTRIBUTE FUNDS TO THE JUNIOR AUXILIARY OF PASCAGOULA-MOSS POINT; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  (1)  The Board of Supervisors of Jackson County, Mississippi, in its discretion, may contribute funds annually in an amount not to exceed Five Thousand Dollars ($5,000.00), out of any available county funds, to the Junior Auxiliary of Pascagoula-Moss Point, a nonprofit organization that is dedicated to community improvement by meeting the needs of children, youth and families. (2)  The provisions of this section shall be repealed on July 1, 2030. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage.
