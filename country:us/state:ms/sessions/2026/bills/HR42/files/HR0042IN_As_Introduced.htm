MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Representatives Mims, Mangold

House Resolution 42

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE PARKLANE ACADEMY PIONEERS FOOTBALL TEAM AND HEAD COACH RON RUSHING ON WINNING THEIR SECOND CONSECUTIVE MIDSOUTH ASSOCIATION OF INDEPENDENT SCHOOLS STATE FOOTBALL CHAMPIONSHIP.

     WHEREAS, on November 22, 2025, in a landslide victory that will surely be remembered for years to come, the Parklane Academy Football Team reigned supreme yet again when the unstoppable Pioneers routed the Heritage Academy Patriots 42-0 to win the Midsouth Association of Independent Schools (MAIS) Class 4A, Division II, State Championship; and

     WHEREAS, having brought home the MAIS Class 5A state title just a year earlier, the 11-1 Pioneers solidified the team's high-performing reputation as a continuously successful program with their incredible back-to-back championship wins; and

     WHEREAS, at the 2025 title matchup, Parklane's early burst of explosive plays gave the Pioneers a comfortable 20-0 lead when the halftime buzzer sounded, yet the squad remained relentless, more than doubling the final score with 22 points added to the board in the second half while the overpowering Pioneers' defense remained unbearable and gave the Patriots not a single chance to prevent the shutout; and

     WHEREAS, in recognition of his phenomenal play for the Pioneers in the championship game and throughout his senior year, Arrocus "Roc" Baskin, Jr., was named the 2025 Y'all Sports Offensive Lineman of the Year; and

     WHEREAS, the team's overwhelming success in the 2025 championship game and throughout the season would not have been possible without the endless training and hard work of each of the following talented student-athletes who comprise the 2025 Pioneers squad:  Jay Alldredge, Kardez Copeland, Kyle Rushing, Nick Young, Kaydon Jackson, Slade Steele, Brennan Ellison, Crews Marchbanks, Telvin Jefferson, Paw Forrest, Braxton Hughes, Eli Chandler, Wick Lindsey, Reece Robinson, Cayden Sandifer, Andrew Lott, Davis Carruth, Hayden Williams, Parker Winge, Brody Rimes, Jace Jones, Ethan Jackson, Drake Hughes, Henry Williams, Watts Newman, DJ Brown, Miles Carter, Dawson Hughes, Tanner Kyzar, Tylar Smith, Sawyer Steele, Tripp Stults, Wilks Carter, TK Jackson, Hayden McCurley, Bennett Williams, Jermaine Gatlin, Owen Webb, Arrocus Baskin, Rantley Rimes, Parker Robinson, Aaron Holmes, Jamie Parker, Nathan Tinnon, Christopher Brown, James Smith, Mason Steele, Alston Davidson, Cannon Perkins, Case Taylor and Kai LaFontaine; manager Bailey Brown; and ball boys Gatlin Lott, Collin Reid and Sam House; and

     WHEREAS, the Parklane Academy Pioneers have been superbly coached and motivated by Head Coach and Athletic Director Ron Rushing and Assistant Coaches Robert Young, Ryan Bradford, Ken Callendar, Austin Helus, Brinson Wall, Grayson Hewitt, Austin Stults, Ryan Wallace and Henry Brewer, all of whom have broadened the players' knowledge of the game while refining their skills in execution; and

     WHEREAS, the Pioneers' phenomenal achievement of winning the MAIS State Championship in their class for a second consecutive year is a testament to the steadfast support of Parklane Academy Headmaster Jack Henderson, the Parklane family and the entire McComb community, whose encouragement and unwavering support of the team continue to inspire success on and off the field; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and honor the achievements of student-athletes such as the 2025 Parklane Academy Pioneers Football Team, who have brought honor to their families, school and community and the State of Mississippi:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Parklane Academy Pioneers Football Team and Head Coach Ron Rushing on winning the 2025 Midsouth Association of Independent Schools State Football Championship and extend best wishes for continued success in all their future athletic and academic endeavors.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Parklane Academy Head Coach Ron Rushing and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
