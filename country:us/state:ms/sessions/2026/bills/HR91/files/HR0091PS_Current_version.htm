MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE

2026 Regular Session

To: Rules

By: Representative Eubanks

House Resolution 91

(As Adopted by House)

A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING LADY CORMORANT HIGH SCHOOL VOLLEYBALL HEAD COACH CHRISTINA MORGAN UPON HER EXTRAORDINARY COACHING SUCCESS AND HER CONTRIBUTIONS TO ONE OF THE MOST DOMINANT VOLLEYBALL PROGRAMS IN MISSISSIPPI HIGH SCHOOL ATHLETICS.

     WHEREAS, the Mississippi House of Representatives takes great pride in recognizing and honoring individuals whose leadership and dedication have elevated the standard of excellence in athletics, education and mentorship throughout the State of Mississippi; and

     WHEREAS, Lady Cormorant High School Volleyball Head Coach Christina Morgan has established herself as one of the most accomplished and respected coaches in Mississippi high school athletics, leading the Lady Gators volleyball program with vision, discipline and unwavering commitment since the school's opening; and

     WHEREAS, under Coach Morgan's leadership, the Lady Gators have built one of the most dominant volleyball dynasties in Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) history, capturing an impressive eight state championships in 2009, 2012, 2013, 2020, 2022, 2023, 2024 and 2025; and

     WHEREAS, the Lady Gators achieved a historic milestone in November 2025, when they completed an extraordinary fourth consecutive state championship, defeating George County to secure a remarkable "four-peat," an achievement that firmly cements the program among the most successful in the history of Mississippi high school sports; and

     WHEREAS, Coach Morgan's career is marked by remarkable longevity and success, having surpassed 450 career coaching victories during the 2025 season, a milestone that reflects decades of dedication to the development of student-athletes both on and off the court; and

     WHEREAS, her coaching excellence has earned national recognition, as she was named the American Volleyball Coaches Association (AVCA) Region 4 Coach of the Year in 2025, an honor bestowed upon only a select few coaches whose programs demonstrate exceptional performance and leadership; and

     WHEREAS, the Lady Gators' sustained dominance was highlighted during the historic 2024 season, in which the team compiled a 39-win record, the most victories in school history, and did not lose a single set to a Class 6A opponent, demonstrating a level of competitive excellence rarely seen in high school athletics; and

     WHEREAS, since 2022, Coach Morgan has guided the Lady Gators through an unprecedented era of success, including a 2022 Class 5A State Championship victory over Long Beach, a 2023 Class 6A State Championship victory over Hancock, a 2024 Class 6A title in a record-setting season, and a 2025 championship victory over George County, culminating in the historic four-year championship run; and

     WHEREAS, the program's achievements have also been fueled by a pipeline of elite student-athletes who flourished under Coach Morgan's mentorship, including standout player Sofia Gonzalez, a three-time "Miss Volleyball" honoree and the school's all-time leader in kills, as well as collegiate signees Ma'Kayla Hill and Payton Davis, whose combined contributions helped propel the Lady Gators to championship glory; and

     WHEREAS, beyond the volleyball court, Coach Morgan's impact extends into the classroom and the broader school community, where she has been recognized as the DeSoto County School District Teacher of the Year, exemplifying excellence not only as a coach but also as an educator and mentor; and

     WHEREAS, Coach Morgan's leadership, character and unwavering commitment to excellence have cultivated a culture of discipline, teamwork and perseverance at Lake Cormorant High School, inspiring generations of student-athletes and bringing great pride and honor to DeSoto County and the State of Mississippi; and

     WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and commend individuals whose extraordinary achievements reflect the highest ideals of leadership, mentorship and athletic excellence:

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate Head Coach Christina Morgan for her outstanding coaching success and her leadership in building one of the most dominant volleyball programs in Mississippi high school athletics, and extend best wishes for continued success in all her future endeavors.

     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Head Coach Christina Morgan and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
