HB4061 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations A By: Representative Hines House Bill 4061 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE BOARD OF TRUSTEES OF THE WESTERN LINE SCHOOL DISTRICT TO ASSIST IN PAYING COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH ESTABLISHING A STAR ACADEMY PROGRAM, WHICH IS AN ALL-ENCOMPASSING ACCELERATION PROGRAM FOR SELECTED 7TH AND 8TH GRADE STUDENTS, FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the Board of Trustees of the Western Line School District to assist in paying costs associated with establishing a Star Academy program, which is an all-encompassing acceleration program for selected 7th and 8th grade students that engages students through hands-on project-based learning and blends rigorous, standards-aligned coursework with hands-on, workforce readiness experiences, for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027............ ................................................. $ 1,000,000.00 SECTION 2.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 3.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
