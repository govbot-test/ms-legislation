Section 1:
HB4105 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations A By: Representative Holloway (27th) House Bill 4105 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE GOVERNING AUTHORITIES OF THE TOWN OF LENA TO ASSIST IN PAYING COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH THE PURCHASE OF A FIRE TRUCK AND VARIOUS EQUIPMENT FOR THE LENA VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENT FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the governing authorities of the Town of Lena, Mississippi, to assist in paying costs associated with the purchase of a fire truck and various equipment for the Lena Volunteer Fire Department for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027 ................................................... $ 514,865.00 SECTION 2.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 3.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
