SR 117 (As Adopted by Senate) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Senator(s) Sparks Senate Resolution 117 (As Adopted by Senate) A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING THE BOONEVILLE HIGH SCHOOL LADY BLUE DEVILS GIRLS BASKETBALL TEAM AND HEAD COACH MICHAEL SMITH FOR WINNING BACK-TO-BACK MHSAA CLASS 3A STATE CHAMPIONSHIPS. WHEREAS, on March 7, 2026, the Booneville High School Lady Blue Devils Girls Basketball Team won the MHSAA Class 3A Girls Basketball State Championship in 2026, capping a 25-4 season. This win marks back-to-back state championships for the team, which has secured multiple titles recently; and WHEREAS, Booneville took control in the opening half of the MHSAA Class 3A Girls Basketball Championship and held on to beat Belmont High School 54-31.  Booneville led 13-6 after one quarter, stretched the margin to 31-15 by halftime and never let Belmont get closer than 16 in the second half.  The decisive stretch came in the second quarter.  Booneville turned a 13-7 lead into a 22-7 advantage on baskets from Raylee Jones and Lamaya Southward, plus two free throws from Adler Doran. Southward then hit a 3-pointer late in the half as Booneville carried a 16-point lead to the break.  Southward led Booneville with 20 points and 5 rebounds, while Jones added 11 points and 7 rebounds.  Kellan Johnson scored 5 points and grabbed 9 rebounds, and Booneville finished with a 42-30 rebounding edge while going 19 of 20 from the foul line; and WHEREAS, Lamaya Southward was named 2026 State Championship MVP and MHSAA Miss 3A Basketball; and WHEREAS, Head Coach Michael Smith has led the team to multiple championships.  The team has established themselves as a dominant force in Mississippi 3A basketball, winning four out of five recent championships; and WHEREAS, Booneville High School 2025-2026 Varsity Girls Championship Basketball Roster includes: Ki'raeah Philpot, Adler Doran, Jazz Philpot, Makylah Pippins, Logann Donahue, Kellan Johnson, Lyric Perry, Peyton Nunn, Lamaya Southward, Rayne Alexander, Bella Nunley, Zyria Floyd, Raylee Jones, Cameya Hampton, Kennedi Shelley, Mylee Stacks, Ayla Hinds, and Alexa Lopez; Head Coach Michael Smith, and Assistant Coaches Chip Johnson, Micah Moment, Bryant Tynes, Maria Mauney, and Ava Kathryn Smith; and WHEREAS, it is with great pride that we recognize these student-athletes who have brought honor to their school, their community and to the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate the Booneville High School Lady Blue Devils Girls Basketball Team and Head Coach Michael Smith for winning back-to-back MHSAA Class 3A State Championships, and extend to the team, coaches, parents and sponsors our best wishes for future championships. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That this resolution be presented to Coach Michael Smith and Principal Brian Windham for the team, forwarded to the Prentiss County Board of Education and be made available to the Capitol Press Corps.
