Section 1:
HB1290 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Ways and Means By: Representative Holloway (76th) House Bill 1290 AN ACT TO REPEAL SECTIONS 27-19-21 AND 27-19-23, MISSISSIPPI CODE OF 1972, WHICH IMPOSE AN ANNUAL TAX ON ELECTRIC VEHICLES AND HYBRID VEHICLES IN ADDITION TO OTHER TAXES FOR WHICH THE VEHICLES ARE LIABLE; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  Sections 27-19-21 and 27-19-23, Mississippi Code of 1972, which impose an annual tax on electric vehicles and hybrid vehicles in addition to other taxes for which the vehicles are liable, are repealed. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
