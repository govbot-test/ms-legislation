Section 1:
SB2986 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations By: Senator(s) Simmons (12th) Senate Bill 2986 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE MISSISSIPPI INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER LEARNING FOR THE PURPOSE OF DEFRAYING COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH DESIGN, CREATION, CONSTRUCTION AND EQUIPMENT OF A NATURAL HAIR CARE SCHOOL IN GREENVILLE, MISSISSIPPI, FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1. The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the Mississippi Institutions of Higher Learning for the purpose of defraying costs associated with design, creation, construction and equipment of a natural hair care school in Greenville, Mississippi, for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027 ............................................... $ 10,000,000.00. SECTION 2.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 3.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
