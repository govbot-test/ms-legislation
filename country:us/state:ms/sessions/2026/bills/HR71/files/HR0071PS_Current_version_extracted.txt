HR 71 (As Adopted by House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Creekmore IV House Resolution 71 (As Adopted by House) A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING ADELINE BAILEY OF NEW ALBANY ON WINNING HER THIRD MISSISSIPPI HIGH SCHOOL ACTIVITIES ASSOCIATION (MHSAA) 3A-4A INDIVIDUAL STATE GOLF CHAMPIONSHIP AND HER EMERGING SUCCESS AS A JUNIOR GOLFER. WHEREAS, Adeline Bailey, a phenomenal young golfer from New Albany, Mississippi, has distinguished herself as a championship player who is gaining ever-increasing recognition throughout the state and beyond as she continues to climb in the rankings of youth golf circuits and organizations with each new tournament win; and WHEREAS, while only a sophomore at New Albany High School, Adeline has helped lead the New Albany Lady Bulldogs Golf Team, coached by Shane Sanderson, to an extraordinary four consecutive Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) State Championships; and WHEREAS, although the Lady Bulldogs fell short of the 2025 Class 3A-4A state title by a few strokes, Adeline, shooting 146 1-under at the two-date event held at the Dancing Rabbit Golf Club in Philadelphia, Mississippi, absolutely dominated the championship, winning her third individual state title in just four years and capturing the Overall MHSAA 3A-4A Medalist honors at the state golf meet on October 21, 2025; and WHEREAS, being at the top of the leaderboard is not uncommon for Adeline, who has secured numerous victories in addition to her three MHSAA 3A-4A individual championships, including her most recent 2025 first finishes at the Towhee Club Open, the Southeastern Junior Golf Tournament (SJGT) MSU Golf Course Junior Classic, and the SJGT Reunion Junior Classic; and WHEREAS, Adeline, undisputedly one of North Mississippi's best golfers, has thrice been named to the Mississippi Association of Coaches North All-Star Golfers and was named Most Valuable Player at the 2025 All-Star Match after leading the North team to victory, and she has also been selected as a top junior golfer to participate in the Sanderson Farms Junior Pro-Am; and WHEREAS, an accomplished multi-sport athlete who excels as a cheerleader as well as on the soccer field, Adeline has been honored locally to be named an A+ Athlete of the Week by WTVA and voted the Daily Journal's Female Athlete of the Week; and WHEREAS, Adeline's commitment to being her best is not limited to athletic endeavors, as she maintains a 4.0 GPA while wisely managing her various activities so that she can have time for family and friends and participate in meaningful organizations such as the National Honor Society, Fellowship of Christian Athletes and her church's ACTEENS mission group; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to recognize and honor the accomplishments of talented student-athletes such as Adeline Bailey, whose outstanding achievements and continuous striving for improvement, whether on the golf course, in the classroom or wherever she may be, bring honor and pride to Adeline's family, school, the New Albany community and the entire Magnolia State: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate Adeline Bailey of New Albany, Mississippi, on winning her third Mississippi High School Activities Association (MHSAA) 3A-4A Individual State Golf Championship and her emerging success as a junior golfer and extend best wishes for continued success in all her future athletic and academic endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Adeline Bailey and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
