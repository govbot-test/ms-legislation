Section 1:
HB1310_H_Amend_01 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 1 PROPOSED TO House Bill No. 1310 BY: Representatives Owen, Blackwell Amend on line 52 by inserting the word "this" before the word "subsection". Further, amend on line 102 by inserting the phrase "or after"  before the word "normal". Further, amend by striking lines 103-107, and inserting, in lieu thereof, the following: "(ii) In areas within school facilities or on school premises designated through rules and regulations adopted by the local school board, which govern such activities, provided that the time of use of the designated areas does not interfere with instruction of other programs authorized by the school district during the normal school hours as prescribed in subparagraph (i) of this paragraph (b)."
