Section 1:
HB1852 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations A By: Representative Zuber House Bill 1852 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE INNOVATION TECHNOLOGY FUND FOR THE PURPOSE OF PROVIDING ADDITIONAL MONIES FOR THE FUND FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the Innovation Technology Fund created by Section 25-53-263, Mississippi Code of 1972, for the purpose of providing additional monies for the fund for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027........................................... ................................................. $ 1,000,000.00 SECTION 2.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 3.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
