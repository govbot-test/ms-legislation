Section 1:
HB1224_H_(Cmte_Sub)_Amend_01 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 1 PROPOSED TO Cmte Sub for House Bill No. 1224 BY: Representative Hood HB 1224 AMEND after the period on line 37, by inserting the following: Electronic mail service means a service for the sending or receiving of electronic mail messages as that term is defined in 15 U.S. Code § 7702 (6). AMEND FURTHER at line 40 after Section 230 and before the period, by inserting: ,except that for purposes of this act it does not include a service that is a broadband provider, an electronic mail service, a text message service, or a telephone service AMEND FURTHER after the period at the end of line 107, by inserting the following and re-lettering the succeeding paragraphs: (n)Telephone service means any service for making or receiving voice calls using an internet connection. (o)Text message service means a service for the sending or receiving of text messages as that term is defined in 47 U.S. Code § 227(e)(8). AMEND FURTHER at line 79 after the period, by inserting the following and re-lettering the succeeding paragraphs: (k) Parent means a person who the interactive computer service provider or its agent has determined is a minors parent, through reasonable efforts in light of available technology, including verifying a parents identity by checking a form of government-issued identification against databases of such information, where the parents identification is deleted by the interactive computer service provider or its agent promptly after such verification is complete. AMEND FURTHER  on line 200 by deleting paragraph "(d)" and inserting the following new paragraph "(d)" : "(d) Allows an adult to use the interactive computer service to knowingly engage in a pattern of conduct or communication with a user who is a minor to gain access to, to gain the compliance of, to prepare, to persuade, to induce, or to coerce a child to engage in sexually explicit conduct or human trafficking or to procure the sexual servitude of a child, as those terms are defined in Mississippi Code § 97-5-32." AMEND FURTHER on line 225 by deleting paragraph (b) in its entirety and inserting in lieu thereof: "(b) A search engine or cloud service provider shall not be held to have violated the provisions of this section  solely for providing access or connection to or from a website or other information or content on the Internet or a facility, system, or network not under the search engine or cloud service provider's control, including transmission, downloading, storage, or providing access, to the extent the search engine or cloud service provider is not responsible, in whole or in part, for the creation or development of the content of the communication that constitutes material harmful to minors or grooming of a child as defined in Mississippi Code § 97-5-32." AMEND FURTHER  on line 187 by deleting (4) and inserting in lieu thereof (3). AMEND FURTHER on line  209 by deleting the word "instance" and inserting in lieu thereof: "category" AMEND FURTHER on line 209 by deleting (4) and inserting in lieu thereof: "(2)(a)(c) or (d) " AMEND FURTHER on line 253 after the word "Protecting" by inserting the following: " Online" AMEND FURTHER on line 254 after the period, by inserting the following: " In the event of a conflict between the provisions of this section and any part of the Walker Montgomery Protecting Children Online Act that are legally in force, the in-force provisions of the Walker Montgomery Protecting Children Act will control."
