Section 1:
HB1677_H_(Cmte_Sub)_Amend_02 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 2 PROPOSED TO Cmte Sub for House Bill No. 1677 BY: Representative Yates AMEND on lines 279 through 309 by deleting all language in inserting in lieu thereof the following: "(2)  Two (2) members at large who live or work in the service area to be appointed by the Mayor of the City of Jackson and confirmed by the City Council of the City of Jackson; (3)  Two (2) members at large who live or work in the service area to be appointed by the Governor; (4)  One (1) member at large who lives or works in the service area to be appointed by the Lieutenant Governor; (5)  The President, or his or her designee, of the Greater Jackson Chamber of Commerce; (6)  One (1) member at large who lives or works in the service area to be recommended by the Mayor of the City of Byram and appointed by the Mayor of the City of Jackson and confirmed by the City Council of the City of Jackson; and (7)  One (1) member at large who lives or works in the service area to be recommended by the Mayor of the City of Ridgeland and appointed by the Mayor of the City of Jackson and confirmed by the City Council of the City of Jackson". AMEND further on lines 311 and 312 by deleting ", and all gubernatorial appointees shall be subject to the advice and consent of the Senate". AMEND further on lines 313 through 315 by deleting the following: ", the Mayor of the City of Byram, the Mayor of the City of Ridgeland and the Executive Director of the Department of Finance and Administration each". AMEND further on lines 316 through 317 by deleting "such Mayor or Director, respectively," and inserting "him or her" in lieu thereof. AMEND further on lines 317 and 318 by deleting "such Mayor or Director, as the case may be," and inserting "he or she" in lieu thereof. AMEND further on line 334 by deleting "member 8" and inserting "one (1) member" in lieu thereof. AMEND further on line 335 by deleting "member 9" and inserting "and one (1) member" in lieu thereof. AMEND further on line 336 by deleting ", and member 10 for a term of two (2) years" AMEND further on line 337 by deleting "member 11" and inserting "one (1) member" in lieu thereof. AMEND further on lines 337 and 338 by deleting "and member 12 for a term of three (3) years". AMEND further on lines 346 and 347 by deleting ", subject to the advice and consent of the Senate for the gubernatorial appointees,". AMEND further on line 404 by inserting the following: "(10)  A three-fourths (3/4) vote of the total membership of the board is required for any rate increase or expenditure in excess of Five Million Dollars ($5,000,000.00)."
