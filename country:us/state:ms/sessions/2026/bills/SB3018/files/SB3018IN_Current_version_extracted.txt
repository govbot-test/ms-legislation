Section 1:
SB3018 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations By: Senator(s) Simmons (12th) Senate Bill 3018 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE SUSTAINABLE ENERGY PARTNERSHIP PROGRAM FOR FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much thereof as may be necessary, is hereby appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the Sustainable Energy Partnership Program, an agreement between Jackson State University College of Science, Engineering and Technology and Entergy Mississippi, L.L.C., for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027 $ 4,300,000.00. SECTION 2.  The money herein appropriated shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 3.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
