HR 123 (As Adopted by House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Scott House Resolution 123 (As Adopted by House) A RESOLUTION COMMENDING AND CONGRATULATING KAYLLIS WALKER FOR HER EXCEPTIONAL ATHLETIC ACHIEVEMENTS, ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE AND OUTSTANDING LEADERSHIP AS A MEMBER OF THE LAUREL HIGH SCHOOL LADY GOLDEN TORNADOES 2026 MHSAA CLASS 5A STATE CHAMPIONSHIP BASKETBALL TEAM. WHEREAS, Kayllis Walker, a distinguished senior and standout student-athlete at Laurel High School, has etched her name among the most accomplished players in program history through her unwavering commitment to excellence, leadership and team success, and the Mississippi House of Representatives takes great pride in recognizing young scholars and athletes whose dedication, discipline and perseverance exemplify the highest standards of excellence both on and off the court; and WHEREAS, during her remarkable high school career, Kayllis Walker played an instrumental role in leading the Laurel High School Lady Golden Tornadoes to the pinnacle of success, culminating in the 2026 MHSAA Class 5A State Championship, solidifying her legacy as a cornerstone of one of the program's most celebrated eras; and WHEREAS, her dominance on the court is reflected in an extraordinary list of accolades, including multiple District Championships, Final Four appearances, and back-to-back State Championship titles, while earning repeated recognition as District Most Valuable Player, District Offensive Most Valuable Player and State Championship Most Valuable Player; and WHEREAS, Walker's individual excellence has been further recognized through her selection as a two-time Dandy Dozen honoree, as well as being named 5A Miss Basketball, distinguishing her as one of the premier high school basketball players in the State of Mississippi; and WHEREAS, her impact in championship play was undeniable, earning Most Valuable Player honors in the State Championship game, a testament to her poise, leadership and ability to perform at the highest level when it matters most; and WHEREAS, beyond her athletic achievements, Kayllis has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to academic excellence, earning recognition as a Principal's List scholar while also pursuing dual enrollment studies at Jones College, reflecting her diligence and preparation for future success; and WHEREAS, her well-rounded character is further evidenced by her active involvement in extracurricular and service-oriented organizations, including volleyball, tennis, Student Council, Key Club and HOSA, embodying the principles of leadership, service and community engagement; and WHEREAS, in recognition of her outstanding talents and future potential, Walker has signed a letter of intent to continue her academic and athletic career at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, where she is expected to make an immediate and lasting impact at the collegiate level while pursuing a degree in nursing; and WHEREAS, throughout her journey, Kayllis has exemplified humility, resilience and determination, serving as a role model to her peers and an inspiration to aspiring student-athletes across the State of Mississippi; and WHEREAS, it is the policy of the House of Representatives to honor and recognize young leaders whose achievements bring pride to their schools, communities and the State of Mississippi: NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI, That we do hereby commend and congratulate Kayllis Walker for her exceptional athletic accomplishments, academic excellence and invaluable contributions as a member of the Laurel High School Lady Golden Tornadoes 2026 MHSAA Class 5A State Championship Basketball Team, and extend best wishes for continued success in all her future endeavors. BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, That copies of this resolution be furnished to Kayllis Walker and to the members of the Capitol Press Corps.
