Section 1:
HB1632_S_Amend_02 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 2 PROPOSED TO House Bill No. 1632 BY: Senator(s) Carter AMEND on line 55 by inserting ", whether publicly or privately owned," after the word "system". FURTHER, AMEND under line 62 by inserting the following as a new paragraph: (*) "Commission" means the Mississippi Commission on Environmental Quality. FURTHER, AMEND on lines 79 through 86 by striking the language after the second comma and inserting the following in lieu thereof: compliance with state and federal permits, operation and maintenance performance history and infrastructure condition and compliance. FURTHER, AMEND on line 87 by striking the word "department" and inserting the word "commission" in lieu thereof. FURTHER, AMEND on line 109 by striking the word "department" and inserting the word "commission" in lieu thereof. FURTHER, AMEND on line 1061 by striking the words "each standard set forth in" and inserting "the criteria established in accordance with" in lieu thereof. FURTHER, AMEND on lines 1062 and 1063 by striking the words "and other assessed criteria as determined by the board that is used". FURTHER, AMEND under line 1558 by inserting the following as a new section and renumbering subsequent sections accordingly: SECTION *.  The assessment, scores and letter grades described in this act shall not affect a project's ranking under the department's Water Pollution Control Revolving Loan Fund Program established under Section 49-17-85.  The framework established pursuant to this act is separate and independent of the criteria outlined in the department's Water Pollution Control Revolving Loan Fund Program. FURTHER, AMEND the title to conform.
