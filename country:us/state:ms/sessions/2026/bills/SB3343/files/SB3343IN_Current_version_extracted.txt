Section 1:
SB3343 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations By: Senator(s) Suber Senate Bill 3343 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE CITY OF WATER VALLEY, MISSISSIPPI, FOR THE PURPOSE OF DEFRAYING COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH UPGRADES AND REPAIRS TO ITS CITY ELECTRIC SYSTEM CAUSED BY THE RECENT WINTER STORM, FOR NECESSARY REPAIRS TO ITS CITY LIBRARY, AND FOR NECESSARY UPGRADES TO ITS WATER AND SEWER SYSTEM FOR THE FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1. The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the City of Water Valley, Mississippi, for the purpose of defraying costs associated with certain infrastructure projects for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027 $ 3,275,000.00. SECTION 2.  Of the funds appropriated under the provisions of Section 1 of this act, the following amounts are to be allocated as follows: To defray costs of upgrades and repairs to its city electric system caused by the recent winter storm................. $ 2,500,00.00. To defray costs of necessary repairs to its city library.... ................................................. $ 275,000.00. To defray costs of necessary upgrades to its water and sewer system................................................. $  500,000.00. SECTION 3.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 4.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
