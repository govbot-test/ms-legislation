Section 1:
SB2994 (As Introduced) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Appropriations By: Senator(s) Simmons (12th) Senate Bill 2994 AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION to the MISSISSIPPI Department of Transportation for the Town of Gunnison for the purpose of defraying costs associated with the construction of a lighted, asphalt walking trail FOR FISCAL YEAR 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much of it as may be necessary, is appropriated out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, to the Mississippi Department of Transportation for the Town of Gunnison, Mississippi, for the purpose of defraying costs associated with the construction of a lighted, asphalt walking trail for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2026, and ending June 30, 2027 $ 65,550.00. SECTION 2. It is the intent of the Legislature that the funds appropriated in Section 1 of this act shall be provided for matching funds to draw down federal funds under the Transportation Alternatives Program administered by the Mississippi Department of Transportation. SECTION 3.  The money appropriated by this act shall be paid by the State Treasurer out of any money in the State General Fund not otherwise appropriated, upon warrants issued by the State Fiscal Officer; and the State Fiscal Officer shall issue his or her warrants upon requisitions signed by the proper person, officer or officers in the manner provided by law. SECTION 4.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
