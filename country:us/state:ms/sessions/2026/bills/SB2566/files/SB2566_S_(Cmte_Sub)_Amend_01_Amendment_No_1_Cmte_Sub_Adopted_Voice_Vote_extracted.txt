Section 1:
SB2566_S_(Cmte_Sub)_Amend_01 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 1 PROPOSED TO Cmte Sub for Senate Bill No. 2566 BY: Senator(s) Johnson AMEND on lines 363-364 by deleting "with fifteen (15) years of experience as a cosmetologist" and inserting "or a cosmetologist with at least fifteen (15) years of experience" in lieu thereof. FURTHER, AMEND on line 410 by inserting "in barbering" after "license". FURTHER, AMEND on lines 410-411 by deleting "and fifteen (15) years of experience as a barber" and inserting "or a barber with at least fifteen (15) years of experience" in lieu thereof. FURTHER, AMEND on lines 478, 489, 499 and 510 by deleting "and". FURTHER, AMEND on lines 739-740 by deleting "and fifteen (15) years of experience as an esthetician" and inserting "or an esthetician with at least fifteen (15) years of experience" in lieu thereof. FURTHER, AMEND on lines 834-835 by deleting "with fifteen (15) years of experience as a nail technician" and inserting "or a nail technician with at least fifteen (15) years of experience" in lieu thereof. FURTHER, AMEND by deleting lines 884-894 and renumbering the subsequent subsection(s). FURTHER, AMEND the title to conform.
