Section 1:
SB2208_S_Amend_02 Adopted AMENDMENT NO 2 PROPOSED TO Senate Bill No. 2208 BY: Senator(s) DuPree AMEND below line 185 by inserting the following: (*)  One (1) individual appointed by the Lieutenant Governor who is a graduate of a Mississippi HBCU and who has experience in workforce development, economic development or public administration; and (*) One (1) individual appointed by the Speaker of the House of Representatives who is a graduate of a Mississippi HBCU and who has experience in workforce development, economic development or public administration. FURTHER, AMEND the title to conform.
